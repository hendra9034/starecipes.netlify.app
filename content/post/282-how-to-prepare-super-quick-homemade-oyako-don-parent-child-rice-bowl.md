##### The ingredients needed to make Oyako-don (Parent-child rice bowl 🐔 🐣):

1. Take 3 table spoon Soy sauce
1. Take 75 ml Mirin
1. Make ready 100 ml Dashi soup or chicken stock
1. Prepare  1 onion
1. Take 250 g chicken breast or thigh
1. Make ready 3 eggs
