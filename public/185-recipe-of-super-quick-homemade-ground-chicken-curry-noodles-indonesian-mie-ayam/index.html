The ingredients needed to make Ground Chicken Curry Noodles (Indonesian Mie Ayam):
  1. Make ready Cooked egg noodles or ramen
  2. Make ready Chicken oil
  3. Make ready Sweet soy sauce
  4. Take For chicken:
  5. Take 2 cups ground chicken
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped onion
  7. Take 1 tbsp curry powder
  8. Get 1 tsp chicken powder
  9. Take Salt to taste
  10. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  11. Take Oil
  12. Prepare For topping:
  13. Prepare Cucumber pickles
  14. Prepare Blanched Chinese choi sum (I used greens vegetables)
  15. Make ready Chili sauce
  16. Take Chopped green onion
  17. Prepare Fried shallots
  18. Make ready For the broth:
  19. Take 2 liter water
  20. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  21. Make ready 1 tbsp chicken powder
  22. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste
