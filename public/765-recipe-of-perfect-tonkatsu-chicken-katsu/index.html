The ingredients needed to make Tonkatsu  / Chicken katsu:
  1. Get Chicken breast 500g (it can be other meat too)
  2. Make ready 100 g flour
  3. Make ready 2-3 eggs
  4. Make ready 1 cup bread crumbs
  5. Take salt and pepper
  6. Get oil for deep frying

Steps to make Tonkatsu  / Chicken katsu:
  1. Cut chicken into slices. Thicker or thinner, completely to your taste! But no more than 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Salt and pepper chicken and let it rest for 10 mins
  3. Coat chicken with wheat flour, then egg, then bread crumbs
  4. Deep fry for 2-3 min, if thin. 4-5 minutes if thick.
