The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 lb chicken tenders
  2. Make ready 3 Chinese spoon Flour
  3. Get 2 Chinese spoon starch
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take Marinade
  6. Take 2.5 Chinese spoon Soy sauce
  7. Get 1.5 Chinese spoon 料酒
  8. Make ready 1.5 sesame oil
  9. Get 0.5 tbsp minced garlic
  10. Get 1 tbsp ground ginger
Instructions to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Mix chicken tenders and "marinade" in a ziploc bag, set aside for at least 20 min
  2. Add egg, flour, starch and rub
  3. Fry with medium heat oil for 5 min then increase to max heat until brown
