The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Chicken Potato Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 lbs ground chicken
  2. Make ready 3 large potatoes shredded
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Get 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  5. Make ready Tsp dill
  6. Prepare Tsp parsley
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp mayo
  8. Take 1 tsp salt and pepper
Steps to make Cheesy Chicken Potato Cake:
  1. Mix ground chicken, shredded potatoes, eggs, salt and pepper in a bowl until mixed well.
  2. Lay into a nonstick skillet with a bit of oil and flatten into an even layer. Fry for 12-15 min.
  3. Flip and fry other side 10-12 min.
  4. Meanwhile, mix cheese, dill, parsley, and mayo. Spread on top of chicken mixture, cover, and cook 10-15 more min on low heat.
  5. Cut into wedges and serve. It went well with a side of green beans.
