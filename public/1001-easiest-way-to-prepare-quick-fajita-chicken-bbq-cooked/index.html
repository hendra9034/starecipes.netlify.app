  5. Prepare 3 tbsps oil

Steps to make Fajita chicken (BBQ cooked):
  1. Put oil and all seasonings in a bowl and mix well
  2. Slice all veg and chicken
  3. Put into marinade and mix extra well
  4. Put into fridge for at least 1 hour
  5. Heat bbq as needed
  6. On a bbq sheet tray pour in the marinated chicken
  7. Place in BBQ cook for 10-15 mins ensuring meat is cooked fully
