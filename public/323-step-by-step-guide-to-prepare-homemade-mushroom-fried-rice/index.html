The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready small brown onion - thinly sliced
  2. Make ready small red onion or shallot - thinly sliced
  3. Get eggs
  4. Prepare red chilli (big) - thinly sliced or chopped
  5. Make ready white pepper powder
  6. Make ready Oyster mushrooms/ baby Oyster mushrooms - the more the better
  7. Get cold rice - grains separated
  8. Get Seasoning
  9. Take - Chicken seasoning powder or salt
  10. Make ready To Garnish
  11. Get chopped spring onion
Instructions to make Mushroom Fried Rice:
  1. Prep all ingredients; rice, mushrooms, onions, chillies & spring onion.
  2. Heat up oil in a deep pan or a wok. Sauté onions, both brown & red until almost translucent. Push to the side of the pan or wok. Break the eggs in, leave to set very lightly & then scramble the eggs, mixing it with the onions. 1. Add chopped or sliced chillies. Stir. Next, stir in oyster mushrooms. Cook well until wilted.
  3. Add in the rice. Cook over medium heat. Stir to combine rice with the rest of the ingredients. Season with salt or chicken seasoning powder (if using but highly recommended). Keep stirring for 3-5mins until everything is well-mixed. Taste & adjust seasonings accordingly if needed. Turn off the heat. Stir in chopped spring onion & add splashes of sesame oil (if using). Mix well. Dish up and serve immediately.
