The ingredients needed to make Easy fried chicken:

  2. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
