The ingredients needed to make Crispy fried chicken tenders:
  1. Make ready 2 kg boneless chicken
  2. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  3. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  4. Get to taste Salt
  5. Get 1 cup maida/All purpose flour
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Take 1 cup bread crumbs
  8. Get as needed Tomato Ketchup
  9. Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  10. Take as needed Milk
  11. Get as needed Oil for frying
  12. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli sauce

Steps to make Crispy fried chicken tenders:
  1. Take the boneless chicken cut into medium pieces marinate with red chilli powder,salt and black pepper powder,keep aside for 30 minutes..
  2. In a bowl add maida, some red chilli powder,black pepper powder and garlic powder,give it a good mix and keep aside
  3. Whisk the eggs by adding little milk and some red chilli sauce..mix well and keep aside
  4. Now take the marinated chicken pieces roll in maida mixture,dip in egg mixture then roll in breadcrums and keep ready
  5. Heat up the oil and deep fry those ready tenders..serve it with ketchup..readyüëç
