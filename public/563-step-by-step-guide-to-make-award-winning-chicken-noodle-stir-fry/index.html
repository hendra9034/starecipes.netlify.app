The ingredients needed to make Chicken Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. Take Brown Rice Noodles 🌾
  2. Take 4 Ounces Chicken 🍗
  3. Make ready 1.5 Ounces Mushrooms 🍄
  4. Prepare 2.0 Ounces Broccoli 🥦
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Coconut aminos
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp sirachah
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp sesame seed oil
  8. Prepare Squeeze lime juice or 1 lime
Steps to make Chicken Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. Prepare stir fry sauce - 1/2 cup coco aminos - 1tbsp sesame seed oil - 1tbsp sirachah sauce - 1 lime or lime juice
  2. Prepare chicken
  3. Steam broccoli and add salt and pepper
  4. Sautee Mushrooms in oil
  5. Cook brown rice noodles for 4-5 minutes in boiling water
  6. Strain noodles
  7. Mix ingredients and add sauce in wok or large pan
