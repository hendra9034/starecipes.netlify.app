The ingredients needed to make Mushroom and Leek Pasta with Chicken:
  1. Take chicken fillet
  2. Make ready leek
  3. Take Mushrooms
  4. Prepare Tarragon
  5. Get Greek yogurt or cream if not watching your weight
  6. Take garlic
  7. Get chicken stock
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Get Pepper
  10. Get Olive oil
  11. Make ready pasta any type
Steps to make Mushroom and Leek Pasta with Chicken:
  1. Heat a large fryingpan, add the olive oil. Slice the chicken and stir fry for about 3 to 5 mins
  2. Slice the mushrooms and leeks, chop the garlic, add to the chicken and cook for 3 mins stirring all the time.
  3. Add the stock, bring to the boil and simmer until reduced by half. Add the salt and pepper to your liking
  4. While the stock is reducing cook the pasta to the packet instructions
  5. Add the cooked pasta, tarragon and the greek yogurt or cream to the chicken mix and keep stirring for 30 seconds. Remove the pan from the heat and serve with the cooked pasta
