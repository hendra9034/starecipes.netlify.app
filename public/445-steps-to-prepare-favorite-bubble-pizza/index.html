Steps to make Bubble pizza:
  1. Make pizza dough by mixing flour,yeast,Veg oil,salt and warm water.
  2. Knead dough very well and allow to rise for 30 mins
  3. Divide dough into two,make balls with one portion.
  4. Flatten dough and spread pizza sauce.
  5. Add cheese and chicken
  6. Sprinkle dill and make another layer of chicken and cheese
  7. Brush bubbles with beaten egg
  8. Bake for 40 mins or until done.
