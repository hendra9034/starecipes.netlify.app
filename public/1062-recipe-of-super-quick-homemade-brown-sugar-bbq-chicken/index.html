The ingredients needed to make Brown sugar bbq chicken:
  1. Take 5 piece thick cut chicken breast
  2. Get 2 cup brown sugar
  3. Get 1 large bottle of bbq sauce
  4. Get 4 tbsp apple cider vinegar

I made a delicious sauce of bourbon, brown sugar, a little Italian dressing for some tang, and barbecue sauce of course for an insanely flavorful, super tender pulled chicken. My only gripe about this recipe is that I should have marinated this overnight so that the flavours can penetrate the chicken further. It is also not a very BBQ-y type of sauce (if you know what I mean) as it's mainly ketchup and sugar, and it doesn't really have the smoky flavour that most BBQ sauces have. Brown Sugar BBQ Chicken lets your slow cooker do allll the work.

Instructions to make Brown sugar bbq chicken:
  1. Mix apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, and bbq sauce in a bowl.
  2. Wash chicken breast and place at bottom of empty crockpot.
  3. Pour sauce mix on top of chicken breast. Cover.
  4. Cook on high for 6 hours.
  5. When done use a fork to pull chicken apart to shred. Enjoy!
