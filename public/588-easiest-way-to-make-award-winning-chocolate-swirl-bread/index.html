The ingredients needed to make Chocolate swirl bread:
  1. Prepare Main dough
  2. Prepare bread flour
  3. Make ready milk
  4. Take Yeast
  5. Take salt
  6. Prepare Sugar
  7. Get butter
  8. Get Chocolate dough
  9. Prepare chocolate powder
  10. Get milk
Steps to make Chocolate swirl bread:
  1. Mix everything together for the main dough, after 30 mins, add in butter to knead, the dough become sticky
  2. After 10-15mins, it will become non. Sticky dough ; divided the dough in 2 and add in the ingredient for the chocolate dough, and mix well.
  3. Wait an hour, for it to double in size
  4. Take the dough n press it to release the air, do the same with both dough
  5. Roll it into rectangular shape
  6. Put them tgt
  7. Roll them tgt
  8. Put oil in the case, let it rise for 30 mins
  9. Put it in oven 180 degree for 20 min
