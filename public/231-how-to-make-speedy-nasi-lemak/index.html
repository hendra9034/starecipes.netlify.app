Instructions to make Nasi Lemak:
  1. Marinate chicken for at least 2 hours.
  2. Cook rice in the rice cooker, add all ingredients (expect coconut milk) and 1 3/4 cup of water first to cook the rice.
  3. Once rice is cooked, open the lid and stir the rice and add coconut milk in. Heat for about 5 mins.
  4. Blend the ingredients for the sambal.
  5. In a pot, add sambal paste and oil.
  6. Then add the 1/2 onions, fried anchovies, salt and sugar. (do not add fried shallots, it will make it bitter!)
  7. Cover chicken with the potato starch and deep fried it.
  8. Cook the rice now.
  9. Serve rice with peanuts, cucumber, sambal and chicken.
