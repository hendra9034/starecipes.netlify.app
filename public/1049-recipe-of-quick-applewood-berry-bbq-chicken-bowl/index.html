The ingredients needed to make Applewood-Berry BBQ Chicken Bowl:
  1. Get 3 boneless, skinless chicken breast; medium dice
  2. Prepare 1/4 C applewood seasoning
  3. Prepare 4 strips bacon
  4. Make ready 1 C Sweet Baby Rays Hawaiian BBQ sauce
  5. Make ready 1/2 C berry juice
  6. Take 1 yellow bell pepper; thick julienne
  7. Make ready 1 red onion; thick julienne
  8. Take 2 lb potatoes
  9. Take 1 head garlic
  10. Take 1.5 stick butter
  11. Prepare 2/3 C heavy cream
  12. Get 1 C shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  13. Make ready 1/2 bundle cilantro
  14. Prepare 1 lime
  15. Prepare 1 avocado; thick julienne & halved
  16. Get 2 scallions
  17. Take 1 t ground coriander seed
  18. Get Kosher salt & black pepper; as needed
Instructions to make Applewood-Berry BBQ Chicken Bowl:
  1. Lay bacon on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until bacon is crispy. Pat dry with paper towels. Cut bacon into baton-like strips, or a large dice
  2. Cover potatoes and garlic cloves with cold, salted water and boil until potatoes are easily pierced with a paring knife. Drain. Return to pot and blast heat to high for 30 seconds while shaking pot to remove excess moisture from potatoes.
  3. Add butter, cheese, cilantro, cream, lime, salt, and black pepper to potatoes. Mash or whip with mixer.
  4. Drain bacon fat into a large, tall saute pan. Add chicken, bell pepper, and onions. Season with applewood seasoning, ground coriander seed, salt, and black pepper. Saute 2-3 minutes, or until chicken is 80% cooked.
  5. Add berry juice. Bring to a simmer. Cover and cook approximately 10 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
  6. Add bbq sauce. Stir. Simmer 1-2 minutes to warm sauce. Do not boil.
  7. Garnish with crispy bacon, avocado, and scallions.
