To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can have easy homemade jam ! using 3 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Easy homemade jam !:
  1. Take fresh strawberries (cut small)
  2. Make ready granulated sugar
  3. Make ready lemon juice
Instructions to make Easy homemade jam !:
  1. Wash and cut strawberries up and add into saucepan…
  2. Mash them lightly with a masher, add sugar and stir up.. pop on medium heat and stir until sugar is dissolved
  3. Heat on a higher setting and cook with a rolling heat, stir every 30 seconds with a wooden spoon, (for a round 30 minutes)
  4. Take off heat (mix wont be thick as this will happen when cooled down)
  5. Leave to cool for around 1 hour… and transfer to a clean jar !!!
  6. Serve with soda bread or toast!!! (See my soda bread recipe) enjoy!!!!
