The ingredients needed to make Chicken Parmesan With Spaghetti and Garlic Bread:
  1. Prepare 6 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare Salt and pepper (to taste)
  3. Take 1 cup flour
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  7. Prepare Olive oil or corn oil to fry
  8. Make ready 2 cups spagetti sauce
  9. Take Parmesan cheese (to taste)
  10. Take 1 pound linguine
  11. Take 8 slices mozzarella cheese
  12. Prepare Garlic bread (see recipe)
Instructions to make Chicken Parmesan With Spaghetti and Garlic Bread:
  1. Add flour, salt, and pepper in a bag….
  2. Shake it up!!!
  3. Put the Panko breadcrumbs in a bag….
  4. Crack 2 eggs….
  5. Add some milk to the eggs….
  6. Mix the eggs and milk together…. pour it into a dish….
  7. Take out the chicken breasts…. wash them…. cut out the extra rib meat…. pound them to flatten….
  8. Cover the chicken breasts in the flour…. cover it in egg wash too…. cover in the breadcrumbs….
  9. Fry them in olive oil until browned….
  10. Flip them…. cook until browned
  11. Drain them with a napkin…. sprinkle some parmesan cheese….
  12. Add mozzarella cheese…. cover them with sauce…. sprinkle a little bit more parmesan cheese….
