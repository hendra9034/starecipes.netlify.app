  2. Prepare onion finely chopped
  3. Prepare tomato finely chopped
  4. Get green chilli finely chopped
  5. Make ready finely chopped garlic
  6. Make ready ginger paste
  7. Make ready peanuts
  8. Make ready Salt to taste
  9. Take Few curry
  10. Get lemon juice
  11. Prepare sugar (optional)
  12. Get turmeric

  15. Prepare chopped coriander leaves
