The ingredients needed to make Chinese Barbecue Chicken Meatballs:
  1. Take 12 oz Ground Chicken
  2. Get 1 Egg
  3. Take 1/4 tsp Ground Coriander
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Ground Mustard Seed
  5. Make ready 2 tsp Brown Sugar
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1 pinch Ground Black Pepper
  8. Get 1/4 tsp Soy Sauce
  9. Get 2 tbsp Rice Flour
  10. Prepare 1 small Sliver Crystalized Ginger (finely minced)
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp Canola Oil
  12. Get 1 cup Water
  13. Make ready 10 oz Koon Chun Barbecue Sauce
  14. Get 1 tsp Sesame Seeds

Steps to make Chinese Barbecue Chicken Meatballs:
  1. Combine ground chicken gently with all ingredients except oil, water, barbecue sauce, and sesame seeds.
  2. In a medium skillet of medium heat, add canola oil.
  3. Shape chicken mixture into 8 equal-sized balls and places in heated skillet with oil. Saute on both sides for 3-4 minutes, or until browned.
  4. In a small bowl, stir together water and barbecue sauce. Pour over meatballs in skillet and leave over medium heat until sauce begins to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered for 45 minutes. Top with sesame seeds as garnish.
