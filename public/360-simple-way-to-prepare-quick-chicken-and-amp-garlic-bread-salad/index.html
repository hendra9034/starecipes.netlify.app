The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Garlic Bread Salad:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts, sliced, or 1 breast and 100g diced Quorn
  2. Make ready 2 slice wholemeal bread, cubed
  3. Take 1 bunch Rosemary
  4. Take s&p
  5. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  6. Get 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp evoo
  8. Get 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  9. Prepare 2 bayleaves
  10. Prepare spinach, rocket, dill, mint, parsley, handful of each
  11. Take 125 grams feta
  12. Take balsamic vinegar
  13. Take 1 orange
Steps to make Chicken & Garlic Bread Salad:
  1. S&P with rosemary, smash in p&m, smash Garlic, tbls oil, tbls ww vinegar. sprinkle over pinch cayenne pepper, rub over chicken or Quorn and sit on bay leaf in a bowl for 5 minutes to marinade, then toss in an oiled frying pan
  2. Mix all herbs in bowl with evo oil and sprinkle of orange juice. add thin slices of skinned orange on to greens. squeeze remaining oranges over cooking meat/ Quorn to boost the flavour
  3. Plate salad, orange, meat/ Quorn, crumble feta, drizzle with balsamic vinegar and serve. Can add seasonal soft fruit as I have here with raspberries.
