Instructions to make Ramzan Special : Afghani Tandoori  / Fried Legs for Iftaar:
  1. Take Chicken Leg Pcs
  2. Add Oil, Butter, Ginger Garlic Paste
  3. Add Salt and Vinegar
  4. Add Kasuri Methi, Curd and Freshcream
  5. Add Pepper powder and Garam Masala
  6. Add Elaichi Powder and COrnstarch / Besan
  7. Crush Cashew Nuts and add (Optional)
  8. Add Finely chopped green chilies
  9. Mix them well
  10. Set it aside for 30-60 mins
  11. Put it in Tandoor for about 5-7 mins
  12. Or can Deep fry for about 5-7 Mins till chicken is completely cooked
  13. Sprinkle Chaat Masala on the hot legs
  14. Serve Hot and Enjoy the Iftaar Delicacy
