The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Spicy Japanese Chicken Wing Recipe | Nagoya Style Tebasaki:
  1. Get 6-8 Chicken wings
  2. Make ready Flour (cake flour / low protein flour)
  3. Make ready 2 tbs Soy sauce
  4. Prepare 2 tbs Mirin
  5. Make ready 1 tbs Sake
  6. Make ready 1 tbs Sugar
  7. Take 1 tsp Grated garlic
  8. Prepare 2 tbs Sesame seeds
Steps to make Sweet & Spicy Japanese Chicken Wing Recipe | Nagoya Style Tebasaki:
  1. Prepare 6 to 8 pcs of chicken wings and wipe the excess water using kitchen paper. - Slice a little bit the meat part. - Add some flour to cover the whole chicken wings.
  2. Prepare the frying pan. Use medium heat. - Take out the chicken wings after it become - a nice golden brown color.
  3. Make the sauce: - Put all seasonings in a pot. (soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, grated garlic) - After boiled, turn off the heat. - Grate 2 tbs sesame seeds. Then pour it into the sauce.
  4. Mix the sauce with the chicken wings. - Add little sesame seed on top. - If you like, you can add chili or red pepper. - - Enjoy!!
