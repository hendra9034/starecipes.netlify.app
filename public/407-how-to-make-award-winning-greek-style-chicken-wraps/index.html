The ingredients needed to make Greek-style Chicken Wraps:
  1. Take 1 c grape tomatoes, halved
  2. Make ready 3 T black olives, chopped
  3. Get 1/4 c feta cheese, crumbled
  4. Prepare 1-1/2 T lemon juice
  5. Take 1 tsp dried oregano
  6. Make ready 1 T olive oil
  7. Get 1/8 tsp ground red pepper
  8. Take 4 oz chicken, cooked and chopped
  9. Get 1 med cucumber, chopped
Steps to make Greek-style Chicken Wraps:
  1. In med. bowl, combine all ingredients. Serve over shredded lettuce or wrapped in lettuce or tortilla.
