Steps to make Crispy chicken strips:
  1. Cut chicken breasts into strips. Coat them in flour and set aside.
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl, crush corn flakes in another bowl and season them with salt or spices of your choice.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan. Coat chicken strips in eggs, then in corn flakes. Fry in hot oil. Remove from oil once golden.
  4. Serve with a dip sauce of your choice.
