The ingredients needed to make Chocolate and mint aero:
  1. Prepare plan flour
  2. Take Baking powder
  3. Take Cocoa powder
  4. Prepare Light brown sugar
  5. Prepare butter
  6. Take eggs
  7. Prepare Milk
  8. Make ready A large shared bag of mint aero bubbles
Steps to make Chocolate and mint aero:
  1. Grease and line the slow cooker bowl
  2. Mix the flour baking powder and cocoa powder in a large bowl
  3. Creme the butter with sugar then add the eggs slowly mixing after each addition
  4. Add the milk and whisk until smooth
  5. Fold in the flour mixture
  6. Pour the cake batter into this slow cooker bowl
  7. Add about half the mint aero bubbles a scattering them over top
  8. Put the lid on and turn the slow cooker on to high
  9. Cook 2 3 hours on high untill firm after a hour add in the remaining areo
