The ingredients needed to make Homemade burgers:
  1. Get lean mince beef (muscle food)
  2. Take salt, ground:pepper, nutmeg, ginger, paprika, thyme, garlic
  3. Get big brown onion chopped small
  4. Prepare egg
  5. Make ready Sunflower oil for cooking
  6. Get Emmental slices
  7. Get Mayonnaise and Sriracha sauce
  8. Prepare Baby leaf lettuce
  9. Take Ogorki / gherkins / pickles sliced
  10. Get Salad tomatoes sliced
  11. Get Caramelised onion. Use one onion finely sliced plus soy sauce
  12. Prepare brioche burger buns
Instructions to make Homemade burgers:
  1. In a bowl work the mince with a wooden spoon. - Add onion plus 1 tsp of each spices. - Add the egg to bind. - Let it rest in the fridge for an hour +
  2. Caramelise the onion in a small frying pan on low heat. - Turn once then add soy sauce and let it caramelise. It will take time but worth it ! Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Form 4 big patties.
  4. Cook in sunflower oil. - Let it rest on kitchen roll to remove the oil excess. - Add the cheese slices for them to melt.
  5. Warm up the brioche buns in the oven.
  6. Sauce: mix mayo and Sriracha.
  7. Build your burger and enjoy. - I do not have a photo finish. I'll add one the next time I make them.
