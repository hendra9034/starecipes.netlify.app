Steps to make Pan Fried Chicken Breast:
  1. Make small slice cuts on one side of chicken breast
  2. Season it with salt and pepper
  3. Put lemon juice, soya sauce, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, red chilli as mentioned
  4. Put it to rest, preferably 2-4 hrs
  5. Take 2 table spoon oil on pan, heat it and cook the breast for 5-7 minutes each side
  6. Add 2 table spoon butter to keep it juicy and moist from inside
  7. Garnish it with some coriander leaves and peri peri salt
