  7. Prepare 3 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions to make Sheet pan barbecue chicken and sausage:
  1. Wrap corn individually in aluminum foil
  2. Cut all veggies
  3. Please everything but corn in a Ziploc bag and mix or you can use a bowl
  4. Cover baking pan with aluminum foil pour mixture onto pan put the corn on the side, Bake 425 for 28 to 35 minutes or until chicken is cooked brush with barbecue sauce before serving serve with dinner rolls
