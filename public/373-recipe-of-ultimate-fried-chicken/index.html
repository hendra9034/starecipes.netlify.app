The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Make ready Chicken
  2. Make ready 75 ml Coconut milk
  3. Make ready 1 lemongrass
  4. Get 2 cm ginger
  5. Make ready 2 tbs garlic powder
  6. Prepare 2 Indonesian bay leaves
  7. Take 1 teaspon turmeric powder
  8. Make ready 1 teaspon coriander powder
  9. Take 2 cm galangal
  10. Get to taste Salt
  11. Prepare Chili (option)
  12. Get 3 orange leaves
  13. Make ready 100 ml Water
  14. Make ready Cooking Oil to fry
Steps to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken into 8 pcs and clean it up
  2. Put all ingredients into the pan mix them and add chicken
  3. Boil for about 15 minutes or untill no water in medium heat
  4. Remove chicken from the pan let it cold down
  5. Fry chicken untill golden brown and ready to serve,, happy cooking :))
  6. U guys also can put it in the fridge before u fry. U can fry it whenever u want :)
