The ingredients needed to make 100% Buckwheat Flour Bread:
  1. Take 300 ml Warm Water
  2. Take 2 teaspoons Dry Yeast
  3. Prepare 2 cups Buckwheat Flour *about 300g
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon Caster Sugar
  5. Get 1 teaspoon Salt *can be reduced
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Olive Oil, melted Butter OR Oil of your choice
  7. Get Sesame Seeds *optional
Instructions to make 100% Buckwheat Flour Bread:
  1. Place Warm Water and Dry Yeast in a bowl, mix gently, and set aside.
  2. Combine Buckwheat Flour (100%), Sugar and Salt in a large bowl. Add Yeast mixture and Oil, and mix well. Cover with a plate or plastic wrap, and set aside in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Line a loaf tin with baking paper, use a scraper or spatula to pour the dough into the tin. Flatten the surface and sprinkle with some Sesame Seeds. Cover with a tea towel or plastic wrap, and set aside in a warm place for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove from loaf tin to wire rack to cool.
