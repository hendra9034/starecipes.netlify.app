  3. Take 1/3 cup chopped garlic

  5. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
  6. Take 2 tbsp butter
  7. Get 1 tbsp white cooking wine
  8. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  9. Get 1 tsp paprika

Instructions to make Awesome BBQ Chicken:
  1. In a small bowl pour olive oil, white wine, salt, and paprika into dish and mix well.
  2. Pour mixture over chicken and rub chicken with the seasoned mixture then let sit.
  3. In a small fruing pan and butter, chopped onion and garlic. Sautee on medium-low heat for about 2min. or until onion and garlic get soft.
  4. Cover baking dish in cooking spray. And chicken to baking dish.
  5. Put the garlic and onion on top of chicken with the butter sauce as well.
  6. Set oven to broil on high. Allow it to heat up, 5-10min.
  7. Cover chicken with aluminum foil then plave in oven 4-5 inches under broiler.
  8. After 10min flich the chicken over. Cook for another 10min then flip chicken over.
  9. Cook for another 15min or until fully cooked.
  10. Remove chicken. - Transfer chicken to another baking pan or dish
  11. apply both sides of chicken with bbq sauce.
  12. Set broiler on low and put chicken back in the oven uncovered further down from the broiler.
  13. Remove chicken, add as much bbq sauce as you like.
