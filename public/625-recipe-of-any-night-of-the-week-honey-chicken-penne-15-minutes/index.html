The ingredients needed to make Honey chicken Penne (15 minutes):
  1. Prepare 400-450 g chicken breast cut in cubes
  2. Take 2 TBS rapeseed oil
  3. Take 2 middle sized onions chopped
  4. Make ready 1 TSP Curry
  5. Prepare 1 TSP paprika powder
  6. Take 1 TSP salt
  7. Prepare 1/3 TSP chili
  8. Prepare 2 TSP Oregano
  9. Prepare Pinch pepper
  10. Prepare 500 ml heavy cream
  11. Prepare 1 TBS mustard
  12. Prepare 4 TBS honey
  13. Take 400 g penne or other sorts of noodles

Steps to make Honey chicken Penne (15 minutes):
  1. Fry the onions for 2-3 minutes and add the chicken cubes, fry them from all sides.
  2. Add the spices and the heavy cream and bring it to boil. Cover the pan with a lid. Lower the heat to medium and let it simmer till the chicken is done. (About 10 minutes)
  3. Add the mustard and the honey and mix it well.
  4. While the chicken simmers, bring salt water to boil and cook the noodles till done.
  5. Pour off the water and put the noodles to the sauce and mix it through.
