The ingredients needed to make Oven Buttermilk Fried Chicken:
  1. Get 200 g flour
  2. Get 100 g bread crumbs
  3. Prepare Paprika
  4. Make ready Pepper
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Get Cayenne Pepper
  7. Make ready Garlic Powder
  8. Make ready Onion Powder
  9. Get 4 crushed honey and oats granola bars
  10. Take 500 ml Buttermilk
  11. Get 100 ml melted butter (about 6.5 table spoons)
  12. Take 20 pieces chicken (which ever you may prefer)
Steps to make Oven Buttermilk Fried Chicken:
  1. Coat the chicken in the spices you prefer (paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne, chilli powerder, onion powder, garlic powder etc) and then cover with the buttermilk
  2. Let the chicken marinate in the fridge for a minimum of 6 hours in the spice and buttermilk mixture
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, bread crumbs, crushed gronola bars and spices together. Remember this is the coating off the chicken so make sure it has alot of flavour.
  4. Once the dry ingredients are mixed together and the chicken has marinated in the marinated. Remove the pieces of chicken from the marinade and coat them in the dry seasoning.
  5. Once all you chicken has been marinated and then coated with the seasoning, place the pieces on a baking try and brush the top of the chicken peices with the melted butter for golden crispy chicken.
  6. Bake in the oven for 35-45min at 210 Celsius which is 410 Fahrenheit. (Don't forget to preheat the oven)
