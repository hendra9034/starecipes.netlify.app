The ingredients needed to make Southern style fried chicken legs:
  1. Take 4-5 chicken legs, washed
  2. Make ready 2 cups flour
  3. Get 1/2 cup buttermilk
  4. Take 2 eggs, beaten
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp pepper
  9. Take 1/4 tsp paprika
  10. Get Oil for frying
