The ingredients needed to make Southern style fried chicken legs:
  1. Prepare 4-5 chicken legs, washed
  2. Get 2 cups flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup buttermilk
  4. Make ready 2 eggs, beaten
  5. Take 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  9. Get 1/4 tsp paprika
  10. Prepare Oil for frying
