The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken Breast:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken.breast
  2. Take Half cup flour
  3. Prepare White pepper
  4. Make ready 2 Eggs
  5. Make ready Pinch salt
Steps to make Fried Chicken Breast:
  1. Combine salt,white pepper and flour.Mix it well
  2. Crack and beat eggs.
  3. Heat the pan then.pour cooking oil
  4. Take the chicken breast dip in.egg then roll in flour mixture.Then do it again twice
  5. Deep fry it until cook
