The ingredients needed to make Nido Oriental Soup with Chicken and Elbow Pasta > Chinese Egg Drop Soup:
  1. Get 900 ml water
  2. Make ready 2-3 handfuls elbow macaroni pasta
  3. Take 1 Knorr's Nido Oriental Chinese Style Soup packet from your Asian or Filipino store
  4. Take strips Leftover chicken or cooked chicken
  5. Get 1 egg, beaten
Steps to make Nido Oriental Soup with Chicken and Elbow Pasta > Chinese Egg Drop Soup:
  1. Bring water to a boil in pot. Then add your elbow pasta.
  2. Cook 7 minutes then lower to a simmer. Start adding the Knorr packet slowly and stir constantly. Some of the powder would form into lumps, squish and stir with a spatula.
  3. Take out your leftover chicken and cut into strips with your fingers or scissors. Or cook some chicken breast beforehand. - - I used leftover spicy roast chicken and this made the soup delicious!
  4. Add the chicken and continue stirring gently until lumps have dissolved. - - Taste and add a little more water if the chicken made the soup saltier.
  5. Lastly, add a beaten egg to the pot while stirring it with a fork so the egg would cook into those pretty white streaks.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy as a side, main or as an afternoon snack.
