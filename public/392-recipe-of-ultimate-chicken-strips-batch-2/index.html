The ingredients needed to make Chicken Strips batch 2:
  1. Make ready Chicken——————–
  2. Get 2 pounds chicken breast boneless and skinless
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup kosher pickle juice
  4. Prepare Spices———————-
  5. Get 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon onion powder
  7. Take 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon white pepper powder
  9. Prepare Coating———————-
  10. Take 1 cup all purpose flour
  11. Make ready 1 large egg
  12. Make ready 1 tablespoon water
  13. Take 1 cup breadcrumbs
  14. Get Oil———————————-
  15. Prepare As needed oil to fry in
Steps to make Chicken Strips batch 2:
  1. Cut the chicken into strips.
  2. Add to a ziploc bag, with the pickle juice. Let this marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Beat the eggs and water together.
  4. Season the chicken with the spices.
  5. Dredge the chicken in the flour.
  6. Then dip into the egg - wash.
  7. Then coat with the breadcrumbs.
  8. Fry in the oil till done.
  9. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil. Repeat till done.
  10. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
