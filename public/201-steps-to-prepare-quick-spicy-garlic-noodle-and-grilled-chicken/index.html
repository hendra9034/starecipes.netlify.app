The ingredients needed to make Spicy Garlic Noodle and Grilled Chicken:
  1. Make ready Egg Noodles
  2. Get Fresh red Thai chili or fresh cayanne pepper or sriracha sauce
  3. Make ready Green onion for garnish
  4. Take Garlic Oil :
  5. Take 10 Garlic cloves, grated or using garlic press
  6. Make ready 2-3 tbs Ginger, grated or minced (must use fresh ginger)
  7. Take 10-15 Green onion, thinly sliced
  8. Get Salt
  9. Make ready 2 cup Canola oil
  10. Get Grilled Chicken :
  11. Take 1 lbs Boneless chicken breast or thigh
  12. Prepare Fresh ginger, grated or using ground ginger
  13. Get Garlic powder
  14. Prepare Onion powder
  15. Take Oyster sauce
  16. Get Salt and ground pepper (white or black)
  17. Prepare Soy Sauce
  18. Prepare Sweet soy sauce (can subtitute with brown sugar)
  19. Make ready Sesame oil
Instructions to make Spicy Garlic Noodle and Grilled Chicken:
  1. How to make Garlic Oil : heat canola oil (do not use oil that have strong taste such as olive oil) until smoky hot. Add the rest ingredients (garlic oil ingredients) into the hot oil. Stir for 2-3 second. Turn off the heat. Pour it in a clean jar. Let it cool and store in the fridge.
  2. Cut the chicken into half inch thickness.
  3. Mix all the seasioning for grilled chicken.
  4. Marinate the chicken for 3 to 6 hours.
  5. Grilled the chicken until it's cooked.
  6. Boil the chilies until they get softened then grind them.
  7. How to serve : boil the noodles and drain. Add some ground chili and some garlic oil in a bowl. Add the cooked noodle. Mix it well. Taste it. Add soy sauce if needed. Slice the grilled chicken and put on top of noodle. Sprinkle with green onion. Enjoy.
