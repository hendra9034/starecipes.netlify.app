Steps to make A Ramen delight:
  1. Add oil to Wok with Oyster sauce, Hoisin, Dark Soy Sauce and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and let that cook out for an extra 3 minutes.
  3. Add the broth and stir into the mixture before adding water (500ml - 1L).
  4. Let the broth cook for 45 minutes to an hour.
  5. Flatten and spice the chicken with any spices of your liking, I used Moroccan spice, garlic powder, Cajun spice and a touch of masala. Then fry for 6-8 minutes either side.
  6. Blanch the bokchoy in butter for 3 minutes or so then take out.
  7. Either fry or boil egg (your choice)
  8. Once the broth is complete, you can dish the food and bring the ramen bowl to life.
