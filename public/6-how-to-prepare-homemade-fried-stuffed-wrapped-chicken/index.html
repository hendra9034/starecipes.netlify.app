The ingredients needed to make Fried stuffed/wrapped Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breast
  2. Prepare 6 slices bacon
  3. Prepare 1 poblano pepper
  4. Prepare 2 slabs of cream cheese
  5. Take 1 Cup flour
  6. Get 1/2 cup BBG seasoning
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Get Splash hot sauce
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup oil for frying
  10. Get 1 tsp diced garlic
  11. Get Preferably a cast iron skillet to fry and bake chicken in

Steps to make Fried stuffed/wrapped Chicken:
  1. Mix flour and BBQ seasoning together in one bowl
  2. Mix egg and hot sauce in another bowl
  3. Slice the poblano pepper into long slices
  4. Slice chicken breast in half to butterfly it.
  5. Place 2-4 slices of pepper on the inside of open chicken breast, place a large dab of cream cheese on top of peppers.
  6. Close chicken breast up and wrap with 3 slices of Bacon.
  7. Dip in egg wash, then cover with flour mixture.
  8. Heat your skillet to medium heat with oil covering the bottom. - When oil is hot add a tsp of diced Garlic to oil right before you place both chicken breasts into the skillet.
  9. Fry both sides and then put the lid on and place in the oven at 350° for an hour.
  10. For an easy side dish cube up 3-4 small potatoes and 1/2 an onion. Dump remaining egg wash onto the potatoes (stir well) then dump remaining flour mix on top (stir well)
  11. Heat 1/2 cup of oil up and fry potatoes until browned. Place in the oven to keep warm until chicken is finished cooking.
