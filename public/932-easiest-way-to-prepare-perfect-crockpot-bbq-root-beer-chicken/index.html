The ingredients needed to make Crockpot BBQ Root Beer Chicken:
  1. Make ready 2 packs boneless/skinless chicken thighs (about 3 1/2 lbs. each)
  2. Make ready 2 onions, sliced
  3. Make ready 10 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Prepare 1 can root beer
  6. Get 2 cups bbq sauce
  7. Get Hamburger buns

Steps to make Crockpot BBQ Root Beer Chicken:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs…
  2. Place thighs into your crockpot…
  3. Add into crockpot your sliced onions, minced garlic, black pepper, root beer and bbq sauce, stir gently …….
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 hours, stirring occasionally……
  5. Shred chicken thighs with 2 forks, stir and cook on high heat for 30 minutes……
  6. Serve over some hamburger buns and enjoy 😉!
