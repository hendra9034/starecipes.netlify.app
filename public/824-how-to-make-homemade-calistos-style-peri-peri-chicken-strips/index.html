The ingredients needed to make Calistos Style peri peri chicken strips:
  1. Make ready 1 whole chicken or 6-7 chicken fillets 2-3 freshly crushed garlic
  2. Make ready Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp lemon pepper
  4. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  5. Make ready 3-4 Tbsp calistos medium peri peri sauce
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp calistos peri peri spices
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp red masala
  8. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  9. Take 3-4 Tbsp oil or olive oil or butter
  10. Prepare 1 tsp garlic and herb spices
  11. Make ready Peri peri sauce
  12. Make ready 2-3 tbsp calistos sauce
  13. Get 1 Tbsp calistos peri peri spices
  14. Get 2 tsp tomato paste
  15. Prepare 2 tsp lemon juice
  16. Take 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
  17. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  18. Take 2 Tbsp mayo
  19. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  20. Make ready 2-3 Tbsp fresh cream
  21. Prepare 2 Tbsp butter or olive oil
Instructions to make Calistos Style peri peri chicken strips:
  1. Mix all ingredients - salt, lemon pepper, vinegar, calistos medium peri peri sauce, calistos peri peri spices, red masala, paprika, oil, garlic and herb spice - together and marinate with chicken. Leave overnight or 1-2 hours. - Cook in the pot or in the oven till completely done. - Pour sauce over the chicken and grill in the oven until golden brown. keep basting with leftover sauce.
  2. Peri peri sauce:- - Mix calistos sauce, calistos peri peri spices, tomato paste, lemon juice, mixed herbs, paprika, mayo, sugar, fresh cream and butter or olive oil and boil slightly in a pot. Pour over chicken and grill. - Serve hot with chips and rice. - Enjoy ðŸ˜Š - #mubinarecipes
