The ingredients needed to make Sticky Crab Soup:
  1. Get 1 Can chicken stock
  2. Make ready Cube diced tofu
  3. Get Diced mushroom
  4. Get 50/50 measure of water to the chicken stock
  5. Prepare Crab meat
  6. Prepare Eggs
  7. Prepare Tapioca flour
  8. Make ready Seasonings
  9. Get After cooked
  10. Take Pepper

  12. Prepare Chives
  13. Prepare Optional ingredients
  14. Make ready Minced meat (pork/beef)

  16. Take Brain (yes brain)
  17. Take Century eggs
  18. Prepare Corn
  19. Take Carrot
Instructions to make Sticky Crab Soup:
  1. Pour the thing in
  2. Wait boil
  3. Mix tapioca with some water (like how you would woth yeast)
  4. Pour tapioca in a mix
  5. Pour eggs
  6. Put a lid on and turn off the heat
