The ingredients needed to make Chicken fingers:

Instructions to make Chicken fingers:
  1. Take chicken boneless..cut into medium strips marinate with salt,blackpepper powder and paprika powder..keep aside for an hour
  2. Now Take a bowl add maida,add paprika powder,mix herbs and garlic powder mix well keep aside
  3. Take a bowl add eggs and whisk it..keep aside, take cornflakes in a bowl and random crush it..keep aside
  4. Now take marinated chicken and roll in maida and keep ready all the chicken by rolling in maida
  5. Heat up the oil..now dip this maida rolled strips into egg batter..than roll up in crushed cornflakes and deep fry it..
  6. Fry all strips in same way..and take out in serving tray serve with ketchup..or decorate with ketchup and green bell peppers (as shown in picture) readyüëç
