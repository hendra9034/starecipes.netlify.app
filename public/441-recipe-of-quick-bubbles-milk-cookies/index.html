The ingredients needed to make Bubbles milk cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 150 g butter
  4. Get 1 big egg
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup milk
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions to make Bubbles milk cookies:
  1. Mix sugar and butter well, add egg and mix
  2. Sieve the flour and mix with all dry ingredients
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the mix and mix well.
  4. Cover and keep for like 20 minutes.
  5. Form smalls rounds with the dough arrange it in a baking tray and bake.
