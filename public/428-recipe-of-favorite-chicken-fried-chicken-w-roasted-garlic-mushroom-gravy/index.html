The ingredients needed to make Chicken fried chicken w/ roasted garlic mushroom gravy:
  1. Take 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1 stick butter
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon olive oil
  4. Take 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  5. Get 1 small onion (red or yellow)
  6. Get 1 clove garlic (roasted)
  7. Make ready 2 boneless skinless chicken breast (cut in half)
  8. Get 4 oz whole milk
  9. Take 2 oz Gourmet Fournet Roasted garlic creole onion dip
  10. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon salt
  11. Get 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  12. Take 3 tablespoon Tony Chacheries original creole seasoning
  13. Make ready 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  14. Prepare 1 tablespoon onion powder
  15. Take 6 oz penne pasta
  16. Prepare 1 cup your favorite brown gravy
  17. Get 1 cup water

Instructions to make Chicken fried chicken w/ roasted garlic mushroom gravy:
  1. Thinly sliced chicken breast in half. Trimming the fat off also. Mix flour, salt, pepper and 1 tablespoon of creole seasoning. Lightly coat chicken, then dip chicken in milk. Meanwhile, heat large skillet on medium heat. Then, Start heating 1 tablespoon of butter mixed with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. When oil and butter are heating up, coat chicken in seasoned flour for a second time. Place chicken in skillet for 6 minutes on each side or until the internal temperature is 165 degrees.
  2. In the same skillet the chicken was pan fried in melt 1/2 stick of butter, onion and garlic then Add freshly sliced mushrooms and sauté. When mushrooms are finished cooking add 1 cup of brown gravy and heat. Once your gravy is done take a tablespoon at a time and mix with 2 oz of Gourmet Fournet Roasted garlic creole onion dip until dip is warm enough to mix with gravy.
  3. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil. Add 1 tablespoon of salt, then add 6 oz of penne pasta. Boil for 7 minutes or desired tenderness. Drain. Toss penne with remaining butter from recipe.
