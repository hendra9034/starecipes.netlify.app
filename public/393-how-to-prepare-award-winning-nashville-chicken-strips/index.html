The ingredients needed to make Nashville Chicken Strips:
  1. Take Hot oil—————–
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup cayenne pepper powder
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon chili powder
  4. Get 2 tablespoons your favorite hot sauce
  5. Take 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
  6. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  7. Take 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  8. Get Strips—————————
  9. Prepare 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast
  10. Take 1 teaspoon onion powder
  11. Get 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  12. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  13. Get As needed oil to fry in
  14. Take Toppings————–
  15. Make ready 1 large kosher pickle
