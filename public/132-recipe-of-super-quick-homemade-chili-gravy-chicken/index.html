The ingredients needed to make Chili gravy chicken:
  1. Take 1 pack curry cut chicken
  2. Get 1/2 cup corn starch
  3. Take 1 onion (cut in cubes)
  4. Make ready 1 capsicum (cut in cubes)
  5. Get 3-4 green chillis
  6. Get 2 tbsp ginger paste
  7. Prepare 1 small bowl garlic (finely chopped)
  8. Prepare 4-5 tbsp dark soy sauce
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp vinegar
  10. Take 1 tbsp red chili flakes
  11. Take 2 eggs
  12. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato puree
  13. Prepare 1 bowl chopped mushrooms
