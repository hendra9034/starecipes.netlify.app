The ingredients needed to make Macaroni Sopas:
  1. Make ready 5 pieces fresh round Mushroom
  2. Take 1 Chicken thigh
  3. Get Half tsp Chicken powder
  4. Take Half cup Evaporated milk
  5. Take Dash White pepper
  6. Make ready 1/3 Carrots
  7. Make ready Half small Cabbage
  8. Take 2 Quail eggs
  9. Make ready Minced Garlic
  10. Make ready Half Onion
  11. Take A few drops Fish sauce
  12. Get One.bowl Elbow macaroni
  13. Get Peppercorns
  14. Make ready Some slices Ginger
Instructions to make Macaroni Sopas:
  1. Boil macaroni until half boil then set aside.
  2. Boil chicken with peppercorns ginger and salt.Let it boil until cook.Then remove and slice into small pieces.Dont throw the chicken stock.Boil quail eggs peel and set aside
  3. Heat a pot and stir fry garlic and onion and the veges.Pour the chicken stock and let it cook until half cook.Then drop macaroni eggs then pour evap milk.Let it simmer then season with the seasonings
