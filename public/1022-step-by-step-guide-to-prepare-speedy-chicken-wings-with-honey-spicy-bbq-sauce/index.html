The ingredients needed to make Chicken Wings with Honey Spicy BBQ sauce:
  1. Take 1 kg Chicken wings
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp garlic powder
  3. Prepare 1 tsp cayyene pepper
  4. Take 1 tsp paprika powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Take 1 tbsp chicken powder
  7. Prepare little salt
  8. Take 1 cup spicy bbq sauce
  9. Take 1 tsp honey
  10. Make ready cooking spray
Steps to make Chicken Wings with Honey Spicy BBQ sauce:
  1. Marinate chicken wings with : garlic powder,cayyene pepper, paprika powder, chrused black pepper,chicken powder,and bit of salt. for about 1 hr
  2. Place the marinated in a tray with alumunium foil,spray with oil then baked in thr oven 220 degree celcius for about 55 mins, flip in every 15-20 mins to cook evenly
  3. Meanwhile prepare honey and spicy bbq sauce in a large bowl,and adds in the cooked chicken wings mixed them well.
  4. Put back the chicken wings with the sauce in oven to broil for about 5-10 mins
  5. Ready to eat. i eat with salads and baked potato. so yumm
