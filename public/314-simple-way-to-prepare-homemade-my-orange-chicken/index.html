The ingredients needed to make My Orange Chicken:
  1. Prepare 4-5 boneless chicken thighs or chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch
  4. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp ginger powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  9. Prepare Canola oil for frying
  10. Get Orange Chicken Sauce
  11. Make ready 3/4 cup orange juice (I used 3 Oranges)
  12. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp corn starch
  13. Take 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  14. Take 3 tbsp soy sauce
  15. Get 1 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  16. Prepare 2 tbsp brown sugar
  17. Take 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  18. Prepare 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  19. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  20. Make ready Red pepper flakes (optional)
  21. Take Sesame seeds for topping (optional)
  22. Prepare 1 orange (zested)
Steps to make My Orange Chicken:
  1. Dice the chicken into 1/2 inch pieces and place in a bowl. Add in the flour, corn starch, salt, black pepper, onion powder, paprika, and ginger powder to coat the chicken.
  2. In a deep drying pan, bring the oil to medium-high heat. Fry the chicken until golden brown. Takes about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk all the sauce ingredients and a bowl. Transfer to a hot skillet or frying pan and cook on low-heat, simmering until the sauce thickens up.
  4. Transfer the fried chicken to the sauce and mix to coat well. Let the sauce and chicken simmer together for 1-2 minutes and then remove from heat.
  5. Serve with white rice. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and chopped green onions. (optional)
