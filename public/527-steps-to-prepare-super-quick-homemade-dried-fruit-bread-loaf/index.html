The ingredients needed to make Dried fruit bread loaf:
  1. Make ready 2/3 mug sieved strong brown bread flour
  2. Make ready 1 and 1/2 plain white flour
  3. Get 1/2 atta flour
  4. Take Half a mug homemade (or shop bought)thick yogart
  5. Get 1 tspn cinnamon powder
  6. Prepare 1 tspn ginger powder
  7. Prepare 3 whole cloves
  8. Prepare 1 tspn fennel seeds(optional or can decor at top only)
  9. Get 1 tspn salt
  10. Take 4 tablespoon brown sugar
  11. Take Handful dried cranberries(rinced before)
  12. Take Handful raisins(rinced before)
  13. Get 2 handfuls dried cubes appricots(rinced before)
  14. Take Quater cup of fermented fruits pulp juice(can use fresh instead)
  15. Prepare Quater cup warm hot water and half pack dry yeast
  16. Get 3 heaped tablespoon heaped butter
  17. Take 3-4 tablespoons cooking oil
  18. Get Large handful Pine nuts for later garnishing
