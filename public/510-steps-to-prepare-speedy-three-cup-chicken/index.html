The ingredients needed to make Three Cup Chicken (三杯鸡):
  1. Take 2 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 10 garlic cloves
  3. Get 2 large ginger roots
  4. Make ready 5 dried red pepper
  5. Make ready 1/3 cup sesame oil
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup rice wine
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce
  8. Take 3 tbsp sugar
  9. Take 3 cup fresh basil

Three cup chicken (san bei ji 三杯鸡) is a uniquely Taiwanese delicacy of chicken braised in sesame oil, Chinese wine and soy sauces. Steps to make Three Cup Chicken (三杯鸡):
  1. Cut chicken breasts to small cubes.
  2. Add chicken cubes and cold water to a pot, then heat to boil. Once boiled, remove the water and set the chicken aside for later.
  3. Cut ginger roots to thin slices.
  4. Heat up a pan with medium-low heat and add 1/4 cup sesame oil. Add ginger slices to the pan, and cook to dry crispy slices. Then add garlic cloves and dried red peppers to the pan.
  5. Mix rice wine, soy sauce, and sugar in a separate bowl. Then add the sauce to the pan. Add chicken to the pan, mix well, and continue cooking for 5-10 minutes, or until the sauce is mostly cooked off.
  6. Add in fresh basil to the pan and mix well. Blend in the rest of sesame oil.
  7. Delicious Three Cup chicken ready to serve!
