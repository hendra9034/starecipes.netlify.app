The ingredients needed to make Tomato Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 cooked egg noodle nests, drained
  2. Take Cooking oil
  3. Get 2 tbsp ginger root, minced
  4. Take 1 onion, thinly sliced
  5. Get 4-5 garlic cloves, minced
  6. Take 6 oz vegetarian chicken, sliced
  7. Take Dash paprika
  8. Prepare Dash five spice
  9. Take 4-5 sprigs fresh lemongrass
  10. Take 4 tbsp tomato paste
  11. Prepare 5 cups stock
  12. Prepare 1 cup radish, cubed
  13. Get 6-8 pieces fried tofu
  14. Take 1/2 cup fresh tomatoes
  15. Make ready 2 carrots, diced
  16. Prepare 3 tbsp fish sauce
  17. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  18. Prepare 1 cup water
  19. Prepare basil & scallions (optional)
Instructions to make Tomato Noodle Soup:
  1. Saute onions, garlic, lemongrass, and ginger. Add spices and stir in chicken slices. Add tomato paste to mixture and heat until color deepens.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  3. Pour over cooked egg noodles. *Serve with basil and scallions.
