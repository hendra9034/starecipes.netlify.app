The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 cups cooked rice
  2. Get Dash soy sauce
  3. Take 1 tbsp rice wine
  4. Get 1/4 onion, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  6. Prepare 1 cup seasoned plant-based chicken, grilled and cubed
  7. Make ready 1 carrot, diced
  8. Get 1 cup peas
  9. Get 2 eggs, scrambled
  10. Make ready Salt to taste
Steps to make Vegetarian Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. Heat oil and cooked rice in a pan on med high heat.
  2. Add soy sauce, oil, rice wine, and onions. Season to taste.
  3. Stir in carrots, peas, chicken, and eggs.
