The ingredients needed to make Minute Mango Meatloaf:
  1. Take 2 lbs ground Meat (Beef, Chicken, Turkey, Pork, or Your Combination) or 907.185 grams
  2. Make ready 1 tsp fresh Ginger
  3. Make ready 2 cloves Garlic (l add more because I love garlic)
  4. Prepare 2 tsp Curry Powder
  5. Make ready 1 chopped Onion
  6. Make ready 1 beaten Egg
  7. Prepare Salt
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup Bread (or cracker crumbs)
  9. Get 1 (250 ml) jar of Mango Chutney
  10. Prepare Cilantro
Steps to make Minute Mango Meatloaf:
  1. INSTRUCTIONS: - Heat oven to 350 degrees. - In a mixing bowl, combine the ground meat, chopped onion, ginger, crumbs, garlic, curry powder, salt, egg and 3-4 tbsp of Mango Chutney. Use hands to mix ingredient together.
  2. Place 1/2 of the meat mixture in a cake or loaf pan. I chose a small cake pan because I did not think it would fit in my loaf pan.
  3. Scoop and smear a layer of Mango Chutney on top. Top off the rest of the meat mixture into the pan.
  4. Bake for 45-60 minutes until cooked thoroughly.
  5. Once baked, allow 10 minutes rest before garnishing with fresh Cilantro and another dollop of Mango Chutney.
  6. Serve with basmati rice (or garlic mashed potatoes) and steamed green beans.
