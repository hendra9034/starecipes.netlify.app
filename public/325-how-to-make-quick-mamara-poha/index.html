The ingredients needed to make Mamara poha:
  1. Get 3 cups puffed rice
  2. Prepare 1 onion finely chopped
  3. Get 1 tomato finely chopped
  4. Get 1 green chilli finely chopped
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  6. Take 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  7. Get 1/2 cup peanuts
  8. Make ready Salt to taste
  9. Take leaves Few curry
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  14. Get 2 tablespoon oil
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  16. Make ready 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  17. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
Instructions to make Mamara poha:
  1. In a sieve add the puffed rice and wash it without pressure. Keep it in the sieve so any access water is removed. Set aside. - - On medium heat, add add oil in a pan and add peanuts and fry until light golden. Remove in a bowl and set aside.
  2. In the same pan and remaining oil add mustard seeds and cumin seeds once it starts fluttering add in curry leaves.
