The ingredients needed to make Homemade Trail Mix:
  1. Get flax seeds or Linseeds
  2. Get sunflower seeds
  3. Get puffed rice
  4. Make ready roasted pistachios
  5. Take oats
  6. Make ready organic extra virgin coconut oil
  7. Make ready rice malt syrup 1 tbsp for reserve
  8. Take shredded coconut
  9. Make ready dried apricots chopped finely
  10. Make ready dried goji berries
  11. Get dark chocolate chopped finely
  12. Get Cinnamon
Instructions to make Homemade Trail Mix:
  1. Preheat your oven to 190 degrees
  2. Melt 1 tbsp coconut oil in the microwave and add 2 tbsp of rice malt syrup
  3. Combine Coconut oil and rice malt syrup with all dry ingredients except for the chocolate, goji berries, pistachios and cinnamon
  4. Spread the mixture onto a large baking tray lined with baking paper and bake for 15 mins, mix well then bake for another 10 minutes or until golden brown
  5. Remove mixture from the oven and mix in the last tbsp of rice malt syrup and cinnamon
  6. Wait until the mixture is completely cool and add goji berries, pistachios and chocolate
  7. Store in an airtight container.
