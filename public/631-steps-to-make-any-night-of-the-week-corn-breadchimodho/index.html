The ingredients needed to make Corn Bread(chimodho):
  1. Take plain flour
  2. Get mealie meal
  3. Prepare white sugar
  4. Take tspn baking powder
  5. Take melted butter
  6. Get tspn salt
  7. Make ready tspn turmeric (optional)
  8. Prepare eggs
  9. Get milk
  10. Get tspn vanilla extract
Instructions to make Corn Bread(chimodho):
  1. Preheat oven at 180°
  2. In a bowl, add eggs,melted butter, vanilla essence and whisk to combine.
  3. Next mix all dry ingredients together.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients bit by bit combining using a hand mixer
  5. Next add milk bit by bit combining everything together.
  6. Grease a loaf tin and add the batter to it,let it rest for 15minutes.
  7. Now transfer it to the oven and bake for 30minutes or until if a toothpick is placed in the center it comes out clean.
  8. Set aside
