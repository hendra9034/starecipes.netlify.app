Instructions to make Stir fry chicken spegettis:
  1. Marinate chicken with peri peri sauce 3 tbsp oyster sauce 2 tbsp salt to taste and black pepper to taste for 30 mins
  2. Now boil spegettis as per packet instructions
  3. In a pan add 4 tbsp oil and add in garlic cook for 1 min or until fragrant
  4. Add marinated chicken cook until changes color on high flame
  5. Then remove chicken pieces in a plate and add tomato puree if your chicken got tender too fast if not add puree in it let it cook on high flame until water dries.
  6. When water dries add all veggies aand cook on high flame for 1 min
  7. Season with salt black pepper and add remaining peri peri sauce and oyster sauce stir well
  8. Add spegettis and stir well add chicken stock mixed with corn flour and mix well cook until sticky.tada done
