The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Roast Chicken with herbs:
  1. Get 1 chicken 1,5 kg
  2. Get 1/4 cup garlic butter
  3. Make ready 3 table spoons Olive oil
  4. Take 1/4 up white wine
  5. Get 1 lemon
  6. Get Salt and pepper
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  8. Prepare 5 Garlic cloves
  9. Get 3 sprig whole rosemary
  10. Take 3 lemongrass sprigs, cut in half and chop the lower half
  11. Make ready 5 lemon leaves, cut into 3 pieces
Instructions to make Garlic Butter Roast Chicken with herbs:
  1. Mix the melted butter, olive oil, wine, parsley, salt and pepper and lemon. Massage the chicken with the mixture thoroughly inside and outside
  2. Stuff the garlic, lemon leaves, upper half of lemongrass, rosemary and lemon halved inside the chicken. Tie the chicken legs together.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400F or 200C under fan force for 10mins. - Toast for 1 hour - then turn down to 340F or 170C for another 15mins. - Baste half way through the toasting time.
  4. Take the chicken out to a toast pan, baste again and cover with foil. - Turn off the oven. Put the covered chicken in the oven again and leave to rest for 10min before serving.
