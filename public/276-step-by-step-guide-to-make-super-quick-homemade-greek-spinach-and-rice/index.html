The ingredients needed to make Greek Spinach and Rice:
  1. Take 500 g spinach
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Take 2 spring onions
  4. Take 1 leek
  5. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
  6. Take 1 tomato (grated)
  7. Get 90 ml olive oil
  8. Make ready 190 g calrose or long grain rice
  9. Make ready 500 ml vegetable stock
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp pepper
  12. Take 30 ml lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
  13. Take 1 handful dill
Steps to make Greek Spinach and Rice:
  1. In a large pot add half of the olive oil (45ml), onion, spring onions, leek and sauté them for 3 minutes.
  2. Add the spinach and simmer it for 5 minutes.
  3. Continue by adding the tomato paste, grated tomato, rice, vegetable stock and stir.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes and check occasionally if it needs extra water.
  5. Lastly, add the salt, pepper, lemon juice, dill and the remaining of olive oil and stir.
