Instructions to make Kentucky fried chicken wings:
  1. Start by splattering a little wet batter into the dry dredge to create tiny clumps
  2. Dip wings into the wet batter and shake off excess.
  3. Dredge the wings and press into the flour and shake off any excess.
  4. Fry wings at 350 until golden brown, about 10 to 12 minutes. You can also fry for 6 minutes and take them out to rest and fry another 6 minutes to achieve the double fry Korean style.
