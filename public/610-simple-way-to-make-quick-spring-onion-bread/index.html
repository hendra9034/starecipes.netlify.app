Instructions to make Spring onion bread:
  1. Mix dry ingredients and mix well to combine. Add the butter and mix till ingredients are well combined.
  2. Gradually add water to the flour mixture (you might use less or more water) until a soft dough forms
  3. Knead the dough until soft and pliable
  4. Coat the dough with the oil and cover with a damp cloth and allow to rise until doubled in size
  5. Punch down the dough and spread it out. Sprinkle the spring onions in the middle of the dough. Roll the dough and form a loaf shape
  6. Place the dough on an oven tray and allow to rise again until doubles in size
  7. Preheat the oven and brush the milk over the bread. Bake the bread for 45-50 minutes at 180 degrees (Celsius)
