The ingredients needed to make Sticky Hoisin Fried Chicken:
  1. Make ready 600 gr (1.5 lbs) bone-in chicken, cut into small pieces
  2. Make ready 3 garlic cloves, grated
  3. Make ready 1 cm (0.5 inch) ginger, grated
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Prepare Green onion to taste, separate the white and green parts, chopped
  6. Take to taste ground black/white pepper
  7. Make ready Sauce Ingredients:
  8. Prepare 2 tbs hoisin sauce
  9. Get 2 tbs sugar
  10. Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce
  11. Make ready 2 tsp sesame oil
  12. Make ready 4 tbs water
  13. Prepare to taste ground white/black pepper
  14. Get 1/2 tsp cornstarch, dissolved with 1/2 tablespoon of water

Instructions to make Sticky Hoisin Fried Chicken:
  1. Marinade the chicken pieces with garlic, ginger, salt and pepper. Put in the fridge and let it stand for an hour minimum.
  2. Preheat the oil for deep frying. Fry the chicken until cooked. Put aside.
  3. Mix all the ingredients for sauce except the cornstarch mixture.
  4. Put the sauce mixture and the white part of green onion in a wok. Keep stirring until it is boiling.
  5. Add the cornstarch mixture. Stir until it gets thickened.
  6. Add the fried chicken. Stir well for one or two minutes. Sprinkle with the green part of green onion.
