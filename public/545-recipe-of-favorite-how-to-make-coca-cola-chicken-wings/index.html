The ingredients needed to make How to Make Coca Cola Chicken Wings:
  1. Prepare 12 pcs Chicken wings
  2. Make ready 300 ml Coca Cola
  3. Get 6 tsp Soy sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tbs Dark soy sauce
  5. Get 2 cloves Garlic
  6. Make ready Ginger
Steps to make How to Make Coca Cola Chicken Wings:
  1. Preparation: - ● After washing the chicken wings, put them in a pot and boil them with hot water. - ● Use another pot and add: - Coke + Soy Sauce (Light Soy Sauce) + Dark Soy Sauce (Soy Sauce) + Garlic + Ginger. - - * If you don't have dark soy sauce, - substitute with 1tsp soy sauce + 1 tsp sugar
  2. Put the chicken wings into the pot and cover the pot. - Boil on low heat for 25 minutes. - - After 25 minutes, you can turn to medium or high heat to drain the sauce. - - Enjoy!
