The ingredients needed to make Garlic Chicken Penne Pasta with Spicy Marinara:
  1. Get 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into 1 1/2 inch chunks
  2. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  4. Take 5 cloves garlic minced
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp virgin olive oil
  6. Get 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh parsley, divided
  7. Prepare 1 jar spicy marinara
  8. Prepare 8 once penne pasta
  9. Get 2 tbsp shredded parmesan cheese
Steps to make Garlic Chicken Penne Pasta with Spicy Marinara:
  1. Cooked Penne Pasta al dente and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss chicken, garlic, Olive Oil and 1 tablespoon parsley - medium bowl.
  4. Cook chicken in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high - heat, stirring occasionally, 8 minutes or until almost cooked.
  5. Stir in sauce and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat - to low and simmer 5 min. or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
  6. To serve, toss chicken mixture with penne, then sprinkle with - cheese and remaining parsley.
