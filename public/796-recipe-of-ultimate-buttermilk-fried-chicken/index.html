The ingredients needed to make Buttermilk Fried Chicken:
  1. Take 2-3 pounds chicken drumsticks
  2. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 2-4 tbs garlic powder
  4. Prepare to taste Salt & pepper
  5. Make ready 3 cups buttermilk
  6. Make ready Oil for frying
  7. Take 1 tbs baking powder
  8. Get 2 tbs minced garlic
  9. Make ready 2 spoonfuls butter
