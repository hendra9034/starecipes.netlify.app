The ingredients needed to make Sharon's Golden Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Take Pack chicken wings (I buy whole wings and cut them myself)
  2. Make ready 2 cups flour
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons seasoning salt
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons onion powder
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  6. Take 2 tablespoons black pepper
  7. Prepare Canola oil
Steps to make Sharon's Golden Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Pour canola oil in deep fryer or frying pan. Heat at 375 degrees
  2. In a large bowl mix flour with all of the seasonings
  3. If you are using whole wings cut them at the joints
  4. Rinse chicken wings. Shake off extra water and place in flour/seasoning breading
  5. Once deep fryer is ready (or oil in frying pan) place wings in a few at a time. Do NOT over crowd
  6. Fry wings to your liking
  7. Enjoy!!
