The ingredients needed to make Olive Bread:
  1. Take 500 g self raising flour
  2. Make ready 250 ml water
  3. Make ready 8 g dry yeast
  4. Take 120 ml olive oil
  5. Get 1 tsp sugar
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 120 g kalamata olives
  8. Prepare oregano
Instructions to make Olive Bread:
  1. In a bowl, add the water, yeast, oil, sugar and flour, and mix them well (approx 5 minutes).
  2. Add the olives and mix lightly until they are incorporated to the dough.
  3. Grease the dough with olive oil and cover the bowl with a kitchen towel, allowing it to rest for 1 hour.
  4. Divide the dough in two pieces and transfer it in two greased loaf tins.
  5. Sprinkle some oregano on the top and bake in a preheated oven for 45-50 minutes at 180ºC (350ºF).
