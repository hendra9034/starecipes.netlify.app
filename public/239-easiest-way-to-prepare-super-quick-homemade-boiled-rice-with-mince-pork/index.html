Instructions to make Boiled rice with mince pork:
  1. Pound the garlic with the skin on till small. Oil in the pan then fries it until they turning crisp and golden.
  2. Soak Shiitake mushrooms in the water until they're soft then cut into a thin pieces.
  3. Put water in the pot following with coriander roots, pork bouillon, light soy sauce, salt and Shiitake mushrooms. Boil it for 10-15 minutes.
  4. After that put the mince pork and pork liver into the pot and stir everything together. Boil until the pork cooked.
  5. When the Pork is cooked, transfer the cooked rice from the rice cooker into the pot. Stir and boil it for 5 minutes. Taste and seasoning with salt if needed.
  6. On the plate, sprinkle with fried garlic, pepper powder and chopped coriander leaves. Enjoy🌿
