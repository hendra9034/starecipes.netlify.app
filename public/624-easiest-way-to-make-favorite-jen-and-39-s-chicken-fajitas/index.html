The ingredients needed to make Jen's Chicken Fajitas:
  1. Make ready 1 Pound raw chicken, sliced
  2. Prepare 1 stick salted butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 onion sliced (half slices)
  4. Get 1 green bell pepper sliced
  5. Get 1 red bell pepper sliced
  6. Get 2 teaspoons Lawry's garlic salt
  7. Get 4 cloves fresh garlic chopped
  8. Make ready 1 medium tomato chopped
  9. Make ready Fajitas Tortillas
  10. Take Toppings and Chips
  11. Get Salsa, sourcream and cheese if desired
  12. Prepare Excellent served with corn chips ðŸ˜€
Instructions to make Jen's Chicken Fajitas:
  1. Melt butter in a large frying on low heat.
  2. After butter is melted put chicken in pan, and mix in a teaspoon of garlic salt.
  3. While chicken is still raw, mix in bell peppers, onions, garlic and tomatoes. Top off with the other teaspoon of garlic salt, more can be added if desired. Mix
  4. Cook slowly while mixing occasionally. This helps the flavors blend.
  5. When chicken is done, turn up the heat to a higher temperature to brown chicken and vegetables. While burning stir every one to two minutes. Burning it a little really kicks up the flavor.
  6. Set fajitas aside. Heat up tortillas and add desired toppings to fajita.
