The ingredients needed to make Romantic Times Cheese and Tomato pasta #mycookbook:
  1. Prepare 400 g pasta
  2. Make ready Homemade Tomato sauce (I'll share it later)
  3. Take 120 g cheddar cheese
  4. Make ready 120 g white soft cheese
  5. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Get Salt and pepper
  7. Prepare Some Dill
  8. Make ready Some Thyme
  9. Get Some parsley
  10. Prepare Salted Boiling water
Steps to make Romantic Times Cheese and Tomato pasta #mycookbook:
  1. Cook your pasta in the salted boiling water, drain it into a separate container when it's cooked enough.
  2. Put some olive oil on a hot pot, the ass the homemade tomato sauce(it contains onion pepper and in sometimes it may contain garlic)(it has its own recipe that I'm gonna share later so stay tend until then).
  3. Add salt and pepper (remember to let the herbs (thyme, dill, and parsley) to the last few minutes of the cooking process. So now you're going to have to add your cheddar cheese, and you're gonna have to stir the whole mix until the cheese starts melting. Now add the herbs and let the mix cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Plate your pasta topping it with some white cheese.
