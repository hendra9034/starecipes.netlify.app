Instructions to make Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps:
  1. Place chicken in crock pot
  2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  3. Cover with wing sauce
  4. Cook on low 7-9 hrs
  5. Remove chicken from crockpot and shred with fork.
  6. Spread some wing sauce on a large burrito size tortilla,add lettuce, chicken, cheese and a dollop sour cream.
  7. Roll it up and enjoy.
