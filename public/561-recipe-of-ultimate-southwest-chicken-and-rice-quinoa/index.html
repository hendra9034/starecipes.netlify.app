The ingredients needed to make Southwest Chicken and Rice Quinoa:
  1. Make ready 3 tbs vegetable oil
  2. Get 3 bell peppers sliced into strips
  3. Take 1 can corn
  4. Make ready 1 tbs flour
  5. Take 2 cups water
  6. Take 2 lbs chicken cut into cubes
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup brown rice
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup quinoa
  9. Get 3-4 garlic cloves
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp paprika
  11. Get 2 tsp salt
Instructions to make Southwest Chicken and Rice Quinoa:
  1. Heat 1tbsp of oil in a large skillet on medium high heat and add peppers and corn to cook for about 6 minutes
  2. Remove everything, set aside and cover for later
  3. Toss chicken cubes in the flour. Add remaining oil to the skillet and cook chicken until golden on each side (about 3 minutes) do not worry if chicken isn’t completely cooked, it will continue cooking in the following steps
  4. Toss rice, quinoa, paprika, salt, and garlic together in a bowl
  5. Add to the skillet. Mix around evenly and cover. Add water and Simmer on medium low heat for 22 minutes or until rice is tender.
  6. Stir in pepper/corn mixture and remove from heat. Keep covered and Let stand for 2 minutes.
  7. Mix one last time and serve
