The ingredients needed to make Crispy tofu and rice noodles ramen üçú üå±:
  1. Make ready 21 cup vegetable stock
  2. Get 100 g rice noodles
  3. Make ready 1 pack Enoki mushrooms
  4. Take 1 pack pea snaps
  5. Get Half block medium firm tofu - cut up in 4cm cube shape
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp cooking oil
  7. Take Sauce base
  8. Get 2 tbsp mirin
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp soysauce
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp sake or chinese cooking wine
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp grated ginger
  12. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar
  13. Get Garnish
  14. Prepare Japanese chilli called Shichimi Togarashi or can use normal chilli flakes
  15. Make ready Radish/grated carrot
  16. Prepare Coriander
Instructions to make Crispy tofu and rice noodles ramen üçú üå±:
  1. Put some veggies stock in a medium high heat saucepan and leave it to boil.
  2. On the other pan, heat on, add cooking oil until medium heat. Pan try tofu and turn them all over until all sides crisped up. Take it out on a plate
  3. Back to saucepan, season your soup with sauce base ingredients. You can add more sauces if prefer
  4. Add noodles, Enoki mushrooms and pea snaps, stir well for a few more minutes
  5. Serve it in a noodle bowl topped up with Japanese chilli flakes- Shichimi Togarashi, radish, coriander or can add shredded carrot too. Add crispy tofu before served
