The ingredients needed to make Japanese Curry With Rice:
  1. Take 2 tablespoons cooking oil (I use olive oil)
  2. Take 500 grams diced lamb
  3. Make ready 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  4. Take 4 blocks S & B Golden Curry Sauce (small size)
  5. Take 2 carrots, cut according to taste
  6. Get 2 large potatoes, cut into cubes
  7. Take Steam Basmati rice for serve (can use other rice or noodles)
Instructions to make Japanese Curry With Rice:
  1. Heat the oil in a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.  Saute the onions until they are half wilted, add the meat and saute for about 5 minutes then add the potatoes and carrots, stir until evenly distributed.
  2. Add about 600ml of liquid chicken stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer until all ingredients are tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. Finally, turn off the heat, break the S & B GOLDEN into pieces and put them in the pan.  Stir until the curry mixture is completely melted.  Simmer for about 5 minutes and stir constantly.  Until completely blended.
  4. Served with hot basmati rice and sprinkled with coriander leaves, Japanese Curry is ready to eat for today's dinner.
