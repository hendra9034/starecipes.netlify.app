The ingredients needed to make Tamarind Barbecue Chicken:
  1. Prepare Chicken barbecue———
  2. Prepare 2 cup tamarind marinade see my recipe
  3. Get 1-1/2 pound chicken breast boneless and Skinless
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Take 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup water
  8. Take Broccoli———–
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 stick butter
  11. Prepare 2 pounds broccoli
  12. Take 1/2 cup water
  13. Get Rice————
  14. Make ready 1-1/2 cup rice
  15. Take 1/2 cup saffron flowers not the strands see note below
  16. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  17. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
  18. Get 1 tablespoon fenugreek
Instructions to make Tamarind Barbecue Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken into strips
  2. Add marinade and chicken strips to a ziploc bag
  3. Put into refrigerator for at least an hour
  4. Take the lime rinds out of marinade and add the chicken strips to a hot pan let simmer for 5 minutes then add the marinade.
  5. Simmer covered for 20 minutes
  6. Remove the chicken and let reduce
  7. Simmer till reduced about a third
  8. Add sugar and stir simmering 4 minutes
  9. Mix the cornstarch and water add chicken back and simmer 5 minutes
  10. Add the cornstarch solution and stir till thickened
  11. Cook the broccoli with water salt and butter cook till done
  12. Cook the rice with butter, saffron flowers, salt, water water and fenugreek
  13. Note saffron flowers are the actual flowers of the saffron Plant. The stem is the expensive spice it has the same flavor, but cheaper. You have to use more of the flowers but it's so very cheap to buy. You usually can get them in the Latino section in the grocery store.
  14. Serve with the broccoli around the edges, rice in the middle, and chicken and sauce on top.
  15. I hope you enjoy!!!
