The ingredients needed to make My super easy honey pork and sirracha ramen soup:
  1. Make ready 4 pork chops
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp honey and light soy sauce
  3. Take 1 tsp garlic powder and ginger powder
  4. Take 3 pks plain ramen noodle
  5. Make ready 2 stock cubes veg/chicken u choose
  6. Get 3 Lt of water for stock
  7. Prepare 1 lg onion diced
  8. Prepare 1 Lg pepper diced
  9. Take 8 button mushrooms sliced
  10. Make ready 2 LG carrots grated
  11. Make ready 1/2 head kale chopped
  12. Take 3 tsp garlic powder and ginger powder
  13. Prepare 2 tsp chinese 5 spice
  14. Get 1/4 cup light soy sauce
  15. Prepare 2 tsp Brown sugar
  16. Get 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
  17. Get to taste As much sirracha
  18. Prepare 2 tbsp oil for stir fry veg
  19. Prepare Chopped spring onion for garnish
Instructions to make My super easy honey pork and sirracha ramen soup:
  1. Pre heat oven 200c make marinade for chops the 2tbsp honey and soy sauce and 1 tsp garlic and ginger powder
  2. Prepare veg while chops marinade dice the onion and pepper,grate the carrots and slice the kale and mushroom
  3. Put chops in oven then in a large cooking pot add oil heat gently add onions and peppers saute for 2 mins
  4. Add carrots mushrooms and kale and the seasoning and water bring to a boil
  5. When chops are done (depending on their thickness)allow to rest while u add noodle to the veg soup
  6. Cook for 5 mins the put noodles into a bowl top with veg and broth place sliced pork on top (garnish with spring onions optional)
  7. If u leave out the chop this can be a vegetarian ramen noodle soup
  8. Enjoy
