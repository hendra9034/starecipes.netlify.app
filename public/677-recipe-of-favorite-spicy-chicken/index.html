The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1.5 lbs (600 g) chicken breast
  2. Take 3 tsp. chili paste (preferably 고추장[Gochujang])
  3. Take 2 tbsp. red pepper powder (preferably 고춧가루[Gochutkkaru])
  4. Prepare 2 tsp. sesame oil
  5. Prepare 4 tsp. soy sauce
  6. Prepare 3 tsp. sugar
  7. Get 2 pieces ginger
  8. Make ready 3 garlic cloves
  9. Prepare to taste salt
  10. Prepare as needed sunflower oil (or similar oil)
  11. Prepare to taste cooked rice

Instructions to make Spicy Chicken:
  1. Mix chili paste, red pepper powder, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, ginger, garlic and salt until the chili paste is fully dissolved. This will be the marinade.
  2. Cut the chicken breast in small pieces and lay them in the marinade for 30min - 2h.
  3. While waiting prepare the rice.
  4. Stir-fry chicken in skillet with sunflower oil.
