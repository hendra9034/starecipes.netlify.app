The ingredients needed to make Honey BBQ Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 20 pcs mid joint chicken wings
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp paprika powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  7. Get Cooking spray
  8. Take Honey BBQ sauce
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup honey
  11. Prepare 2 tbs/28 gr butter
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  13. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder

Steps to make Honey BBQ Chicken Wings:
  1. Wash, pat dry, and season the wings with the salt, garlic powder, black pepper, sugar, and paprika. I marinate the chicken overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to 190celcius. Spray the wings with some cooking oil. Place the wings in a single layer on the cookie sheet, and place in the oven. - Cook for 35-40 minutes, depending on the size of the wings, turning once
  3. Meanwhile, mix all ingredients to make BBQ honey sauce, simmer over low heat
  4. When the wings are done baking, carefully dip them in the sauce, and place them back in the oven for another 5-10 minutes each side, or until the sauce is bubbling.
  5. Ready to serve :)
