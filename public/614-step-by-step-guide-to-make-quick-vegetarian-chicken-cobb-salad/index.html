The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Cobb Salad:
  1. Prepare 8 oz grilled plant-based chicken, diced
  2. Make ready 1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped
  3. Get 10-14 cherry tomatoes, halfed
  4. Prepare 3 oz blue cheese, crumbled
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp onion, thinly sliced
  6. Prepare 4-5 small bell peppers, juilenned
  7. Take 2-3 boiled eggs, quartered
  8. Prepare Balsamic vinaigrette
Steps to make Vegetarian Chicken Cobb Salad:
  1. Chop iceberg lettuce into bite sized pieces and divide onto plates.
  2. Place remaining ingredients on lettuce. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
