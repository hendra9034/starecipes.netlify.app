Instructions to make Chicken wings roast:
  1. Wash and cut the wings into two
  2. Marinate with turmeric powder, chilli powder, crushed pepper and salt, keep for 30 min
  3. Pour oil in a pan, fry the wing by batch in medium flame until golden brown, spread it on a paper towel
  4. Mix all the sauce ingredients in a bowl, keep a pan, pour the sauce and add fried wings stir well in low flame for 5min, and 5more min in high flame
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve
