The ingredients needed to make Simple Fried Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo:
  1. Take Chicken and sauce ———
  2. Prepare 1-1/2 pounds chicken breast boneless and skinless
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  5. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
  7. Make ready 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  8. Take 16 ounces jar of your favorite Alfredo sauce
  9. Take pasta——
  10. Prepare 1 pound Fettuccine noodles
  11. Make ready 3 quarts salty water
  12. Get 2 tablespoon extra Virgin olive oil
  13. Get 2 tablespoons extra Virgin olive oil
Instructions to make Simple Fried Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo:
  1. Bring the water to boil add salt, and olive oil. Add the pasta and cook per instructions on box.
  2. Dice the chicken, add garlic, salt, flour, and pepper it..
  3. Pan fry in as needed oil to fry
  4. When chicken is done move to paper towels.
  5. Add chicken to the pasta
  6. Add parsley, and Alfredo sauce. Warm then add the pasta
  7. Let the pasta warm in the sauce. Mix well but don't break the pasta.
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!
