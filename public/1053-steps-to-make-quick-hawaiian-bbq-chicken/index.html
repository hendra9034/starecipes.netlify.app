The ingredients needed to make Hawaiian BBQ Chicken:
  1. Prepare 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup soy sauce
  3. Make ready 1/2 Tsp minced garlic
  4. Prepare 1/2 Tsp minced ginger
  5. Prepare 1/2 Tsp onion powder
  6. Take 1/2 Tsp black pepper
  7. Prepare 1/4 Tsp sesame oil
  8. Get 3/4 cup water

Instructions to make Hawaiian BBQ Chicken:
  1. In a large bowl mix together the soy sauce, ginger, garlic, onion powder, black pepper, and water.
  2. Butterfly the thighs so that they maintain an even thickness
  3. Add the chicken thighs to the marinade cover with plastic wrap, and chill in the refrigerator for 4 to 5 hours.  (Before putting the chicken in you can pull out a 1/4  cup  of the marinade and keep it in reserve to put on your rice or cooked chicken)
  4. Pre-heat your griddle pan or oven to 350 F. If using the BBQ grill use medium heat.
  5. Cook the chicken for 10 to 15 minutes on the griddle or BBQ, 20 to 25 on a baking sheet in the oven, or until the chicken is done.
  6. Serve with sticky rice (see my recipe) over a bed of shredded cabbage, and enjoy!
