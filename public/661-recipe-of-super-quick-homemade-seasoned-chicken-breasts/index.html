The ingredients needed to make Seasoned Chicken Breasts:
  1. Prepare 3-4 Chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic
  3. Take 1/2 onion powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  5. Prepare 1/2 cayenne
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  7. Make ready 1/2 rosemary
  8. Take 1/4 marjoram
  9. Prepare 1/4 parsley
  10. Take 1/4 celery salt
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  12. Take 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  13. Take Olive oil
Instructions to make Seasoned Chicken Breasts:
  1. Pound the chicken flat. When I say pound, I mean pound. Release all that frustration inside of you!
  2. Add olive oil and rub on skin. Both sides. This will feel weird. Breathe and keep going.
  3. Mix spices and herbs together in small bowl. Rub chicken evenly with mixture on both sides. Again, weird. But don’t stop. Stay the course my friend.
  4. Place chicken on cookie sheet and cook in 425 F oven for about 18-20 minutes. Take out of oven and let rest on counter, for about 3-5 minutes lightly wrapped in foil.
  5. Cut into slices, cubes, etc. for those awesome recipes you’re going to make now that you have perfected chicken breasts. Give yourself a high five!
