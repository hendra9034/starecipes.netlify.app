The ingredients needed to make Bread pizza:
  1. Make ready 1 tin corn soup
  2. Make ready 6 medium sized boiled potatoes
  3. Prepare as per taste Salt
  4. Get 1 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  6. Take 1/2 cup chopped onions
  7. Take 4 Bread
  8. Prepare as required Grated cheese for topping

Steps to make Bread pizza:
  1. For filling
  2. Boiled the potatoes.
  3. Then peel of the potatoes skin and mash them.
  4. Heat butter in a deep pot
  5. Add onions cook till they are pinkish.
  6. Add the corn soup and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Then add the mashed potatoes in the mixture and add the spices. Mix well.
  8. Cook them for 5 minutes. Let it cool.
  9. Then take a bread, put the mixture on the bread and on top add grated cheese.
  10. Per heat the oven 10 minutes before placing the bread pizza
  11. Apply oil on the baking tray.
  12. Place the bread pizza on the tray and bake for 15 minutes at 180°C.
  13. Repeat the steps for the bread pizza.
  14. Serve the bread pizza
