The ingredients needed to make Crispy Sriracha Buttermilk Oven Fried Chicken:
  1. Prepare 4 boneless chicken breast
  2. Prepare 1 cup low-fat buttermilk
  3. Get 1/4 cup sriracha sauce
  4. Prepare 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  5. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  6. Get Half teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon paprika
  9. Prepare Half teaspoon seasoning salt
  10. Make ready 3 tablespoons canola oil

Instructions to make Crispy Sriracha Buttermilk Oven Fried Chicken:
  1. On a cutting board slice each chicken breast into strips
  2. In a bowl combine the buttermilk and Sriracha and mix to combine add the chicken strips and submerge in buttermilk mixture. Cover with plastic wrap or toss all of the ingredients into a resealable bag. Refrigerate for at least two hours
  3. Preheat oven to 425¬∞
  4. In a shallow dish add the Panko, flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and seasoning salt. Mix to combine
  5. Dip the chicken strips in the mixture, ensuring they are fully coated
  6. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper and brush with canola oil
  7. Transfer the chicken to the baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes. Flip the chicken over and bake for 10 minutes
  8. Transfer the chicken into a paper towel lined plate to remove extra grease
