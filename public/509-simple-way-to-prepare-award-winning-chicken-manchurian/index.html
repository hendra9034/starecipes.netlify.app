The ingredients needed to make Chicken manchurian:
  1. Prepare 300 gm chicken breast
  2. Get 1 big onion (cube cut)
  3. Make ready 1 green capsicum (cube cut)
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Take 1 bag mushrooms (sliced)
  6. Make ready 2-3 ginger stick (finely chopped)
  7. Get 5-6 garlic pods (finely chopped)
  8. Take 2-3 green chilis (finely chopped)
  9. Get 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  10. Get 2 tbsp black pepper powder
  11. Make ready 3-4 tbsp corn starch
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  13. Make ready 4-5 tbsp tomato puree
  14. Take 2 tbsp white vinegar
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp sesame seed (for garnish)

Steps to make Chicken manchurian:
  1. Make batter with egg, black pepper powder and corn starch and mix with the chicken well. Keep aside for 30min.
  2. Golden fry the chicken bites.
  3. Meanwhile add some chopped ginger, garlic, green chilis, onions and capsicum, mushrooms and sauté in a wok. Add in vinegar, soy-sauce, tomato puree and stir well.
