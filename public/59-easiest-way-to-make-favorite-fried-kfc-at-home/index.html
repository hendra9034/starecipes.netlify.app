The ingredients needed to make Fried KFC at Home:
  1. Get 500 g Chicken
  2. Get 100 ml Water
  3. Take 1.5 Tbsp salt
  4. Take 1 TBSP sugar
  5. Make ready Shallot
  6. Prepare 240 g all purpose flour
  7. Get 60 g corn starch
  8. Get 1 Tsp Baking powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 Tbsp salt
  10. Take 1 Tsp Chilli powder
  11. Take 1 Tsp Garlic powder
  12. Get 1 Tsp Onion powder
  13. Make ready 1 Tsp Black pepper
  14. Make ready 2 Eggs
  15. Prepare 100 ml Water
  16. Take Cooking Oil
Instructions to make Fried KFC at Home:
  1. Clean the chicken well…in a bowl add 100ml water,1.5Tbsp salt,1Tbsp sugar and shallot mix add the chicken cover and refrigerate for 6 hours.
  2. Remove half of the seasoned flour into another bowl then add 2 eggs and 100ml Water…mix
  3. Bring the chicken and let dry,then dip the chicken into the liquid mixture and then lift it up to the seasoned flour.
  4. Finally Fry the chicken!!
