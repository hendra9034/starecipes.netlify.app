The ingredients needed to make Chicken and onion egg Ramen:
  1. Make ready 2 x 2minute noodles chicken
  2. Take 1 x onion
  3. Take 1 x chicken stock cube
  4. Make ready 1 x cup of frozen vegetables
  5. Prepare 1 x shaker of pepper
  6. Make ready 1 x shaker of chili powder
  7. Prepare 12 x eggs
  8. Get 1 x bottle of soy sauce
  9. Take 1 x bottle of Worcestershire sauce
  10. Make ready 1 x bottle of rice wine vinegar
  11. Take 1 x bottle of sesame oil
  12. Take 1 x jar of sugar
  13. Make ready 1 x bottle of salt
Instructions to make Chicken and onion egg Ramen:
  1. Take 8 eggs, poke small holes in the bottom of each of them
  2. Boil a big pot of water
  3. When the water is boiled add the eggs with a slotted spoon and boil for 8 minutes
  4. When done add the eggs to an ice bath and remove their shells
  5. Chop the onions finely and prepare a small pan, add some rice wine vinegar, some salt, pepper, sugar, and chili powder
  6. Let heat until boiled, and let cool down. Add the mixture to a zip lock bag with the onions and squeeze out the air and seal. Set aside.
  7. In a bowl, get another zip lock bag, in it, add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, rice wine vinegar, sugar, sesame oil, pepper and chili powder, and add the eggs. Squeeze out the air and seal.
  8. Put the eggs and the onions in the fridge to marinate for at least an hour.
  9. When an hour or more has passed and you're ready to eat, grab 2 eggs, and an onion. Cut the onion in half and slice it finely.
  10. In a mug, add the onion and the 2 raw eggs. Mix them around, and heat a pan up.
  11. While the pan is heating, boil some water in a kettle. Grab a bowl, and grab the instant noodles. in a bowl add pepper, chilli powder, a chicken stock cube, and the flavour satchel from the noodles. Bend the noodle cakes in half so you have 4 half cakes.
  12. Add oil to the pan and allow it to heat up. Pour the egg and onion mixture into the pan, shape it into a half circle shape. Let cook until you can flip it. Cook until done then take off heat.
  13. Clean the mug used before and add a cup of frozen vegetables, wash them with warm water to defrost them. After multiple washes, add boiling water and set aside.
  14. Add boiling water to the spice bowl. Use a fork to mix it around and add the noodle cakes and cover with a bowl.
  15. Cut the omelette into strips. Take 3 of the marinated eggs out of the fridge and take out the onions too.
  16. Cut one of the eggs down the middle, cut the other 2 into long slices.
  17. Stir the noodles and the vegetables, add new boiling water to the vegetables if needed.
  18. When the noodles are done, add the vegetables, eggs, omelette, pickled onions, and crack a raw egg on top.
  19. Serve immediately!
