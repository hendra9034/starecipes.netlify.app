The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Chicken with Tomatoes:
  1. Make ready 1 large pack chicken breast (boneless / skinless)
  2. Take 1 onion, sliced
  3. Take 1 green pepper, sliced
  4. Make ready 10 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Make ready 2 cans (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes, undrained
  6. Take 1 can (4 ounce) stems and pieces mushrooms, drained
  7. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon oregano leaves
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon adobo
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sazon with achiote
  11. Take 1/2 cup white cooking wine
  12. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  13. Get Cooked white OR yellow rice

Steps to make Crockpot Chicken with Tomatoes:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken breast, then cut into chunks and place into your crockpot……
  2. Add into crockpot your sliced onion, green pepper and minced garlic……..mix lightly……
  3. Add in your diced tomatoes and stems and pieces mushrooms……mix lightly……
  4. Now add in your black pepper, oregano leaves, adobo, Sazon, white cooking wine and ground cumin, mix lightly…….
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 - 6 hours, stirring occasionally…..
