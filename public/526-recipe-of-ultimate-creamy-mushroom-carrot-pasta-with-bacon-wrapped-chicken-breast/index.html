The ingredients needed to make Creamy mushroom carrot pasta with bacon-wrapped chicken breast:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 medium carrot
  3. Make ready 1/2 medium onion
  4. Prepare 4 oz fresh baby spinach
  5. Prepare 1 can progresso creamy mushroom soup
  6. Take 1 bacon wrapped chicken breast (see recipe)
  7. Prepare Honey mustard BBQ sauce
  8. Get Hot sauce (if you like)

Instructions to make Creamy mushroom carrot pasta with bacon-wrapped chicken breast:
  1. Make carrot noodle. I used vegetable spiralizer.
  2. In large sauce pan, saute onion with olive oil on medium heat for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add creamy mushroom soup and cook until it begins to lightly boil.
  4. Add carrot noodle and spinach. Simmer for 3-4 minutes. Then serve on plate.
  5. Slice bacon-wrapped chicken breast and place it on pasta. Drizzle honey mustard BBQ sauce on chicken. Also Drizzle hot sauce on pasta if you like.
