The ingredients needed to make Pandemic Stay at home Fried Chicken Strips:
  1. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  2. Get 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  3. Prepare 2 teaspoon ground Spainish paprika
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  6. Take 2-1/2 chicken breast strips boneless and skinless
  7. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  8. Get 2 cup peanut oil to medium fry in
Instructions to make Pandemic Stay at home Fried Chicken Strips:
  1. Season the chicken and heat the oil. Add the flour and coat the chicken. Let rest 5 minutes before frying.
  2. Fry the chicken in the hot peanut oil turn as needed. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil.
  3. Serve, I hope you enjoy!!!!!
