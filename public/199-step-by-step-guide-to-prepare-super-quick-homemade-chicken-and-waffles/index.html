The ingredients needed to make Chicken And Waffles:
  1. Prepare Chicken Brine
  2. Make ready 2-3 lbs Boneless Chicken Breasts or Thighs
  3. Get 2 Cups Whole Milk or Buttermilk
  4. Take 1 Tbsp Salt
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp Hot Sauce or Chili Sauce
  6. Prepare 1/2 Tbsp Onion Powder
  7. Take 1/2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  8. Take 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  9. Make ready 1/4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
  10. Make ready Chicken Breading
  11. Make ready 2 1/2 Cups All-purpose Flour
  12. Prepare 1/3 Cup Cornstarch
  13. Take 2 Tsp Salt
  14. Get 2 Tsp Baking Powder
  15. Take 2 Tsp Onion Powder
  16. Make ready 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  17. Get 2 Tsp Black Pepper
  18. Make ready 1/4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
  19. Make ready Waffles
  20. Prepare 2 Cups All-purpose Flour
  21. Take 1/4 Cup Sugar
  22. Take 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  23. Prepare 1/2 Tsp Salt
  24. Take 1 1/2 Cups Milk
  25. Prepare 8 Tablespoons Salted Butter (One Stick) Melted
  26. Make ready 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
  27. Take 4 Eggs (4 Egg Whites, 2 Yolks) Separated
Instructions to make Chicken And Waffles:
  1. Cut Chicken Breasts into @ 2" Pieces. Mix all Brine Ingredients in a bowl. Place Chicken pieces in Brine. Make sure all Chicken is covered in Brine, cover and Refrigerate for at least 2 Hours to Overnight.
  2. Mix all Chicken Breading Ingredients in a Mixing Bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a Large Mixing Bowl, add all Dry Waffle Ingredients. Melt Butter, then set aside to cool. In a Small Mixing Bowl, Crack all 4 Eggs over an Egg Separator, Leaving just the Whites. Place Yolks in a Separate Bowl. Whisk Egg Whites until stiff peaks are formed. In a Large Measuring Cup or Bowl, Add Milk, Vanilla, 2 Egg Yolks, and Cooled Melted Butter. Add this Liquid To the Dry Waffle Mix and stir until mostly combined. Fold in Egg Whites. Set aside.
  4. Heat Cooking Oil in a Large Cast Iron Skillet over Medium to Medium High Heat. Oil should be about 1" high and reach between 350° - 375°. Set up a dredging station. Pour a decent amount of the Dry Chicken Breading Mix onto a plate or in shallow bowl. Dredge each piece of Chicken in the Breading Mix, and set on a plate to rest for 5 Minutes. Dredge in the Mix again.
  5. Fry Chicken in small batches for about 7-8 Minutes, turning half way through. Don't overcrowd pan. Place cooked Chicken onto a wire cooling rack/sheet pan. Place in a low oven to keep warm.
  6. Heat Waffle Maker and cook Waffles. Top Waffles with Chicken and toppings or sauce of choice. A spicy honey butter sauce is often customary, and I might post a separate recipe for that one, at some point. Maple syrup or honey is fabulous too. Enjoy!
