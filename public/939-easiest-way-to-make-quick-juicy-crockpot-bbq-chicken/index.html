The ingredients needed to make Juicy crockpot BBQ chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs boneless chicken breast
  2. Take 1 cup bbq sauce
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Italian dressing
  4. Get 1/4 cup brown sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp worcestshire sauce
  6. Make ready Salt to taste
  7. Make ready Garlic powder
