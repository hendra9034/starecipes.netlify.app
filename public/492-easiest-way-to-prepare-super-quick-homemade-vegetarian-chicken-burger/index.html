The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Burger:
  1. Make ready 1 brioche hamburger bun
  2. Make ready 1 Gardein plant-based chicken patty, baked
  3. Prepare 1 slice tomato
  4. Make ready 2-3 slices shallots
  5. Take 2 leaves butter lettuce
  6. Make ready Sauce
  7. Prepare 1.5 tbsp mayonnaise
  8. Get 1 tsp relish
  9. Take Dash black pepper
  10. Prepare 1/4 wedge lemon, juice
  11. Get Pinch salt
  12. Get 1/2 tsp honey
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp dill
