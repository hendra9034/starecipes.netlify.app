The ingredients needed to make Fried chicken breast:
  1. Take 1/4 kg chicken breast (diced into cubes)
  2. Get 2 cubes chicken cube
  3. Prepare 2 cups vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 2 red bell papers
  5. Prepare 2 green bell papers
  6. Get 1/2 tbsp curry powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 cucumber

Steps to make Fried chicken breast:
  1. Wash chicken breast and marinate with curry and chicken cubes for 30 minutes
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and fry chicken breast until golden brown
  3. Serve with cucumbers and green and red bell papers
