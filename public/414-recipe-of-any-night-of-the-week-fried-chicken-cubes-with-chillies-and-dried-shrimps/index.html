The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken Cubes With Chillies And Dried Shrimps:
  1. Make ready 1 piece chicken breast (cut into cubes)
  2. Get 2 tbsp oil
  3. Get 3 tbsp shrimps (dried, cleaned and drained)
  4. Get 5 shallots (peeled and diced)
  5. Make ready 2 red chillies (seeded and cut into chunks)
  6. Make ready 2 stalks spring onion (cut into sections)
  7. Make ready marinade:
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Get 1 little pepper
  10. Get 1 tbsp cornstarch
  11. Take seasoning:
  12. Take 1 tsp light soya sauce
  13. Prepare 1 tsp oyster sauce
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp water
Steps to make Fried Chicken Cubes With Chillies And Dried Shrimps:
  1. 1 Combine chicken with marinade and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. 2 Heat up 2 tbsp oil, pan-fry chicken over high heat until golden brown. Dish up.
  3. 3 Stir-fry dried shrimps until aromatic. Add in shallots, red chillies and stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. 4 Add in chicken, seasoning and stir-fry until well mixed. Dish up and serve.
