The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Bread (Bread machine):
  1. Make ready water
  2. Get medium egg (beaten)
  3. Take milk powder
  4. Take oil (I used olive, but your choice)
  5. Take sugar
  6. Make ready salt
  7. Prepare strong white bread flour
  8. Get cocoa powder
  9. Take fast action yeast
  10. Make ready chocolate chips
  11. Make ready Honey
Instructions to make Chocolate Bread (Bread machine):
  1. Place water, beaten egg and oil in bread pan, sprinkle over milk powder.
  2. Place flour on top of liquid, sprinkle cocoa powder over top, make small dents in the corners.
  3. Place sugar, salt and yeast in separate corners.
  4. Set machine to sweet bread setting, add chocolate chips when machine bleeps.
  5. When cooked remove carefully from pan and place crust side up on wire rack, whilst hot brush lightly with honey to glaze.
  6. As you can see from picture, top of loaf broke away when removing from pan !
  7. You can substitute chopped nuts,dried fruit or chopped crystalized ginger for the chocolate chips.
