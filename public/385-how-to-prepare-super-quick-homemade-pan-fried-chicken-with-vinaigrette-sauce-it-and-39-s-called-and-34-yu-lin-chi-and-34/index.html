The ingredients needed to make Pan fried chicken with vinaigrette sauce. It's called "Yu Lin Chi":
  1. Prepare 350 g Chicken thigh
  2. Take 15 g Ginger
  3. Take 15 g Green onions
  4. Take 15 g Parsley
  5. Make ready Some Corn starch
  6. Take (Seasoned)
  7. Get 2/3 Teaspoon Salt
  8. Make ready 1 little Black pepper
  9. Take 2 Teaspoons Cooking sake
  10. Get (Vinaigrette Sauce)
  11. Prepare 4 Tablespoons Soy sauce
  12. Get 3 Tbs Sugar
  13. Prepare 4 Tbs Vinegar
  14. Take 2 Tbs Chicken broth
  15. Make ready 1 Tbs Sesame oil
  16. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Cooking sake
Instructions to make Pan fried chicken with vinaigrette sauce. It's called "Yu Lin Chi":
  1. (Vinaigrette Sauce) Chop parsley, ginger and green onions. Add all the sauce's ingredients in a pot and let it boil, take it off the stove. Pour the sauce into chopped parsley, ginger and green onions. sauce is ready for chicken.
  2. Open chicken thigh with even thickness and add seasonings. Dust some corn starch only inside of chicken thigh.
  3. Add 1 tbs canola oil in a frying pan, place chicken skin side down and fry till 80% done, turn them over and fry some more.
  4. Cut chicken to bite size pieces, pour sauce on top, this is it.
