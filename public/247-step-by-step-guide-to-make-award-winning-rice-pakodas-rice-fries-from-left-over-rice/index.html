The ingredients needed to make Rice pakodas/rice fries (from left over rice):
  1. Take 1 cup Left over rice,basmati rice
  2. Make ready 1 medium size onion,finely chopped
  3. Get 3 Green chillies,finely chopped
  4. Get Curry leaves finely chopped
  5. Make ready Ginger minced or grated
  6. Take Lemon juice of 1 lemon
  7. Get Coriander leaves,finely chopped
  8. Take 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Get 1 tsp chilli powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  11. Take Pinch pepper powder
  12. Take Salt to taste
  13. Get 1/4 tsp baking powder (gives more crispy fries)
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp gram flour(chickpea flour)
  15. Get 1 tsp rice flour
Steps to make Rice pakodas/rice fries (from left over rice):
  1. Mash rice by adding a bit of salt using a potato masher

  3. Mix well,if needed add few drops of water to make a perfect dough
