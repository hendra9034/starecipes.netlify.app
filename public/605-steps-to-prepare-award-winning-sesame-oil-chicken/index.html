Instructions to make Sesame oil chicken (麻油雞):
  1. Boil water into wok and put some salt - Cut chicken into slices and put inside - - Wait until you see water boil then close fire this is to reduce chicken smell
  2. Take the chicken meat out and put asidde
  3. Cut garlic into slices
  4. Cut ginger into slices
  5. Put sesame oil into wok and heat with small medium fire
  6. Throw in garlic slice
  7. Throw in ginger slice
  8. Goreng until brownish color
  9. Takr the chicken and put into it
  10. Put soya sauce and add in sesame oil as needed
  11. Then let the chicken入味
  12. Then put water several times each time with small amount to create gravy
  13. Then let it heat up and boil for 3 - 5 mins
  14. Then put some ajinomoto
  15. Done
