The ingredients needed to make Ramen Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare Ramen noodles
  2. Get ginger chopped fine
  3. Prepare garlic mince
  4. Get red chillies
  5. Prepare shrimps
  6. Get button mushrooms sliced
  7. Get spring onion bulbs sliced fine
  8. Take spring onion greens chopped fine
  9. Get carrot julienned
  10. Take vinegar
  11. Make ready soya sauce
  12. Get sweet chilli sauce
  13. Make ready oil
  14. Get chicken Stock
  15. Get boiled egg
Instructions to make Ramen Noodle Soup:
  1. Clean and devein the shrimps, cut the mushrooms into slices, cut the spring onion bulbs, julienne the carrots, chop the ginger, mince the garlic
  2. In a pan heat oil, add chopped gingers, minced garlic, red chillies, spring onion bulbs sliced and sauté until little brown in colour, add mushrooms and little salt
  3. Cook mushrooms until moisture is released. Add vinegar, soya sauce, and sweet red chilli sauce, stir. Add few julienned carrots, stir
  4. Add stock, add noodles and the ramen noodle packet, stir, add spring onion greens chopped little, stir, set aside
  5. Plate with halved boiled eggs, carrot julienned, spring onion green chopped, drizzle soya sauce on the boiled eggs, serve
