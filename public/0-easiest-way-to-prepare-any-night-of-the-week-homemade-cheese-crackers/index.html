The ingredients needed to make 🧀🍪Homemade Cheese Crackers🧀🍪:
  1. Prepare 45 g (1/3 cup) All-purpose flour
  2. Take 55 g (1/2 cup) cheddar cheese grated
  3. Take 30 g (1/8 cup) unsalted butter cold cut into cubes
  4. Make ready 0.6 g (1/8 tsp) salt
  5. Take 1-2 tsp ice cold water
  6. Take Parchment paper (optional)
Steps to make 🧀🍪Homemade Cheese Crackers🧀🍪:
  1. Combine Cheddar cheese, flour, butter and salt in a food processor pulse until dough is consistency of course sand. Add water, 1 tsp at a time, until dough is moistened.
  2. Wrap dough in cling film and refrigerate for 30 minutes. - Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Roll dough onto a lightly floured work surface and cut into desired shapes, such as flowers or fish, using a cookie cutter. Arrange shapes on the prepared baking tray.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and crispy, 8 to 10 minutes.
