The ingredients needed to make Ayam Goreng (Indonesian Fried Chicken):
  1. Prepare 1 kg Chicken Wings
  2. Get 2 cm fresh turmeric (or 2 tsp powder)
  3. Prepare 2 cm fresh ginger
  4. Get 2 cm fresh galangal
  5. Get 5 cloves garlic
  6. Make ready 1 cloves red onion
  7. Get 1 teaspoon coriander seeds (toasted for better flavour)
  8. Prepare salt pepper
  9. Make ready chicken powder
  10. Prepare 5 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Ayam Goreng (Indonesian Fried Chicken):
  1. To make marinate : blend garlic,red onion,turmeric, ginger,galangal,coriander seeds,and olive oil until become a paste. add salt pepper and chicken powder to taste
  2. Marinate chicken wings in 1-2 hour or more for better result
  3. Deep fried the chicken till golden brown
  4. Best serve with hot jasmine rice, chillies, and any stir fried vegetable, but in here. i have stir fried kangkoong or morning glory. superb! :)
