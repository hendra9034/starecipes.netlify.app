Instructions to make Sweet Barbecue Sauce Chicken Wings:
  1. Grill the wings until they are fully prepared.
  2. Leave the wings to cool. When they're cooled, remove the skin from the wings.
  3. Add all the ingredients besides the wings in a big pan or a wok. Mix them while cooking on a big fire for 3-5 minutes.
  4. When the sauce is boiling, add the wings and mix for 3 minutes.
