The ingredients needed to make How to Make Easy Cheesy Bread نان پنیری:
  1. Make ready 300 g plain flour
  2. Prepare 200 ml lukewarm milk
  3. Take 15 g dry yeast
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  7. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  8. Take For topping
  9. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  10. Get cheese of your choice, I used a collection of cheese that I had in the fridge like Cheddar cheese, Feta cheese
Instructions to make How to Make Easy Cheesy Bread نان پنیری:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Combine lukewarm milk, oil, 1 egg, sugar, salt and yeast in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add flour and stir it all together with a spoon until it is all well combined. Now, you have a slightly lose dough.
  4. Cover the bowl with a kitchen cloth and let your dough rise for 1 hour. - After an hour,
  5. After an hour, Preheat the oven to 180C degree. Line the baking tray with parchment paper. - place the dough on a floured surface. Sprinkle top of the the dough with flour and slightly stretch and fold the dough 3-4 times until you have a nice and soft but not lose dough as you see in the photos.
  6. Place the dough on the baking tray and shape the dough into a round disk. Brush it with egg yolk.
  7. Then topping with cheese.
  8. Bake the bread at 180C for 20-25 minutes. - Remove your cheesy bread from the oven and let it completely cool and then cut it and serve.
