The ingredients needed to make Homemade mole cake:
  1. Prepare Corpus
  2. Take eggs
  3. Prepare sugar
  4. Take vanilla sugar
  5. Take butter
  6. Get milk (or more if needed)
  7. Take semi-smooth flour (polohrubá múka)
  8. Take cocoa
  9. Get baking powder
  10. Make ready Filling
  11. Take mascarpone
  12. Make ready whipped cream (šľahačková smotana)
  13. Prepare vanilla sugar
  14. Prepare dark chocolate
  15. Get bananas
Instructions to make Homemade mole cake:
  1. Separate egg whites from egg yolks. Whisk egg whites with half of sugar into the stiff snow. Then in another bowl, mix the butter with remaining sugar (also vanilla) into a foam. Add egg yolks by one into butter and sugar foam and stir well, then add lukewarm milk and dry corpus ingredients (flour, cocoa, baking powder). Lightly add snow and mix. Bake in a round greased tin (approx 28 cm) at 180°C for 25-30 min. Let it cool.
  2. Grate the chocolate. Whisk mascarpone, whipped cream and vanilla sugar, but beware to not over whip it. Add grated chocolate and stir well.
  3. Mark the cut approx 2 cm out of edges in corpus. Scoop out the inside part of corpus into 2-3 cm depth. Set aside the scooped corpus. Cut lenghtwise bananas into halves and put it into scooped hole. Add filling cream and make a hill. Sprinkle the crumbled remaining corpus (the one which you set aside) over the cream filling.
  4. Let it cool in a fridge for at least 1 hour (optimal overnight). - Enjoy your cake 😋
