The ingredients needed to make Fried Lemon Chicken:
  1. Take 4 pieces chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 4 cloves sliced garlic
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp mirin sauce
  5. Get 2 tbsp soya sauce
  6. Get 1 tbsp honey
  7. Take 1 lemon juice
  8. Prepare Pepper
  9. Make ready Spring onion
Steps to make Fried Lemon Chicken:
  1. Combine shaoxing wine, mirin, soy sauce and honey in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken with salt and pepper for half an hour. Pan fry chicken for 3-5 minutes until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Add garlic slices and the combined sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes. Lastly add lemon juice.
  4. Garnish with finely chopped spring onion.
