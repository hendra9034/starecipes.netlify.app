The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken Smothered in Cheese:
  1. Prepare 4 piece chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 Bottle of BBQ Sauce
  3. Get 8 oz block of cheese I used chedder
Instructions to make BBQ Chicken Smothered in Cheese:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. In a 10x13 glass dish place chicken in the dish sprinkle some seasoning I used garlic but what ever you like on your chicken
  3. Place cut cheese on top of chicken
  4. Smother chicken in BBQ sauce
  5. Place in oven for 30 mins depending on the thickness of the chicken might take longer
