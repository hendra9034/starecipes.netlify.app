The ingredients needed to make Crispy Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready Chicken wings
  2. Take Crispy Fry®️ breading
  3. Prepare For boiling chicken
  4. Make ready Fish sauce
  5. Make ready Maggi®️ Magic Sarap
  6. Make ready Rosemary
  7. Make ready Vinegar
  8. Make ready Salt & pepper
  9. Take Marinate
  10. Prepare Calamansi or Lemon
  11. Prepare Maggi®️ Magic Sarap
  12. Take Oyster sauce
  13. Take Salt & pepper
Instructions to make Crispy Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. In a pot, boil water with the ingredients above. Put in the chicken, let it simmer in low heat for 15-20mins. (This is optional, it makes your chicken juicier and bigger after you fry it)
  2. If you dont have time to boil, you can just marinate the chicken with the ingredients above.
  3. Cover chicken with Crispy Fry®️ breading.
  4. Fry chicken in medium heat until golden brown.
