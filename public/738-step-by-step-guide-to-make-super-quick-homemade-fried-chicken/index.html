Instructions to make Fried chicken:
  1. Mix flour, salt, red chilli powder and oregano.
  2. Cut boneless chicken into medium sized pieces.
  3. In a bowl add chicken, ginger-garlic paste,red chilli powder, turmeric and salt.
  4. In a bowl add eggs and beat it.
  5. In a plate spread breadcrumbs.
  6. Roll chicken first in flour, then dip it in egg and then roll in breadcrumbs.
  7. Keep it in the fridge for 1-2 hours.
  8. Deep fry.
