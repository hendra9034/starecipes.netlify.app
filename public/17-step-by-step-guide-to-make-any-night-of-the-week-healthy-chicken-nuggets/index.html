The ingredients needed to make Healthy chicken nuggets:
  1. Prepare 600 g chicken breast fillet
  2. Prepare 60 g Oats
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Get 2 Tsp Paprika
  5. Get 2 Tsp Garlic powder
  6. Make ready 2 Tsp Nandi Peri peri salt or Chicken salt
  7. Get Kcal: 155 Protein: 25g Fat: 3g Carbs: 6g

Instructions to make Healthy chicken nuggets:
  1. Pour oats into your blender or food processor. Blend until the oats have turned into a fine flour. Add the spices to the flour mix
  2. Cut the chicken fillets into cubes
  3. Stir one egg in a bowl
  4. Dip the chicken pieces in the egg crust and then in the flour mix and put on a baking tray
  5. Bake in the oven at 175 degrees for 20-25 min
  6. Serve with a sweet and sour sauce
