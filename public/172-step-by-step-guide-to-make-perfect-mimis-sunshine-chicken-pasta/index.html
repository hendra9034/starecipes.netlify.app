The ingredients needed to make Mimi’s Sunshine Chicken Pasta:
  1. Prepare 4 large chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 1/4 coconut paste or milk
  3. Prepare 2 small pints whipping cream
  4. Make ready 1/4 skim milk
  5. Get 6 oz trimmed pea pods
  6. Prepare 4 large peppers cut in strips, yellow, orange, red
  7. Make ready 2 sweet onions / quartered
  8. Take 1 tbsp Rogan Josh Seasoning
  9. Prepare 1 pinch curry
  10. Get 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  11. Prepare Coat chicken in Cajun seasoning and sauté on stove, or out in air fryer. Costing needs to come out crispy
  12. Take 2 lemons- juiced
  13. Take 1/2 parm cheese
  14. Prepare Boil egg noodles or I like Italian dry colored noodles
  15. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
Instructions to make Mimi’s Sunshine Chicken Pasta:
  1. Coat chicken w spicy Cajun blend. Air-fry or cook these in your cast iron skillet. Keep moist set aside.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of butter to med hot.. use cast iron. Add coconut milk, milk, whipping cream, all spices.. bring to low boil.
  3. Add onions, and peppers. Continue to stir, add additional seasoning to taste. Sauce should simmer 10 -15 minutes, stir. Add lemon, and pea pods after 12 minutes.
  4. Taste sauce / boil water and noodles.Drain and set aside. Save 1/4 cup pasta water add to simmering sauce.
  5. Cut up chicken w has been cooked, add to sauce and simmer another ten minutes. Taste sauce. Add Rogan John or Cajun spice as needed to taste. You can also use some nutmeg to sweeten
  6. Serve up- pour noodles in pasta bowl and add sauce / chicken. Add Parmesan on top.
