The ingredients needed to make Ramen cheese bomb:
  1. Make ready boiled masala ramen or Maggie
  2. Prepare cheese slice
  3. Prepare bread slice
  4. Prepare salt and black pepper powder
  5. Prepare Oil to fry
Instructions to make Ramen cheese bomb:
  1. First flat the bread slice with the help of rolling pin
  2. Now set cheese slice
  3. Make a layer of Maggie or ramen and sprinkle salt and black pepper powder
  4. With wet hands make a shape like bomb proper round
  5. Now deep fry until golden
  6. So yummy evening snack is ready
