The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken & Bacon Jalapeno Poppers:
  1. Get 8 Jalepenos
  2. Make ready 1 (8 oz) block of cream cheese softened
  3. Get 1 cup shredded cheddar
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp onion powder
  7. Take 16 peices deli sliced chicken (or turkey)
  8. Take 16 slices bacon
  9. Take Your favorite bbq sauce. I prefer a sweet and spicy

Steps to make BBQ Chicken & Bacon Jalapeno Poppers:
  1. Mix the cream cheese, shredded cheddar, salt, pepper, and onion powder in a bowl.
  2. Cut the stems off of the jalapenos and then cut them in half lengthwise. Then remove the inside stem and seeds. You should have 16 jalapeno boats.
  3. Using all of the cream cheese mixture, evenly divide into each jalapeno boat.
  4. Then wrap each boat with a slice of deli chicken and wrap a slice of bacon around that.
  5. Place all of the jalapeno poppers on a greased shallow baking sheet in an oven preheated to 400Â°F for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and set oven to broil. Pour bbq sauce over each popper and then broil for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
