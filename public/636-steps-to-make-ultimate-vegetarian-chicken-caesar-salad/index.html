The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Caesar Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 cups kale, chopped
  2. Prepare 4 cups romaine, chopped
  3. Take 10 oz Gardein plant-based chicken strips
  4. Make ready 3 oz shredded parmesan
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  6. Make ready Caesar salad dressing

Instructions to make Vegetarian Chicken Caesar Salad:
  1. Marinade chicken strips to taste and grill or bake until done. Let cool.
  2. Toss greens together and divide onto plate.
  3. Top with parmesan and tomatoes. Add chicken strips.
  4. Drizzle with dressing.
