The ingredients needed to make Fried Bun Chicken Sliders:
  1. Prepare Dough
  2. Prepare 2 and 1/2 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1.5 tsp yeast
  4. Make ready 4 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1.5 tsp salt
  6. Take 3 tbsp oil
  7. Make ready 1 cup warm milk
  8. Make ready Chicken Marination
  9. Make ready 400 g boneless chicken cubed
  10. Get 2 tsp paprika
  11. Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  12. Take 1 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 3 tbsp yoghurt
  14. Take Sauce
  15. Get 1 medium onion chopped
  16. Make ready 2 green chilies** (another half chili for extra spice)
  17. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste (see tip)
  18. Get 1 tsp chili powder** (for extra spice)
  19. Get 1/2 capsicum chopped
  20. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  21. Get 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  22. Make ready 4 tbsp sriracha sauce
  23. Make ready Buns
  24. Make ready Breadcrumbs
  25. Make ready 2 eggs
  26. Make ready Other
  27. Make ready Coleslaw
Instructions to make Fried Bun Chicken Sliders:
  1. DOUGH: Mix the 4 tbsp sugar, 1.5 tsp yeast, and half of the 1 cup warm milk, cover and keep aside for 20 minutes.
  2. DOUGH: In another bowl, add 2 and 1/2 cup flour, 1.5 tsp salt, and 3 tbsp oil and mix well. Add in the activated yeast mixture along with the rest of the milk. Mix with wooden spoon and add water if necessary to make a soft, non sticky dough. Leave to rise.
  3. Before marinating the chicken, clean it thoroughly. - (see tip)
  4. CHICKEN: Marinate chicken in 2 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 3 tbsp yogurt, and 1 tsp salt. For best results leave to marinate overnight, otherwise 1 hour minimum.
  5. CHICKEN: Shallow fry the chicken in 1.5 tbsp oil until cooked and golden brown on the outside. Set aside.
  6. SAUCE: In the same pan, add the onions and green chilies and sautÃ© until soft. Then add the 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and sautÃ© for 1 minute. Add capsicum and sautÃ© for 5 minutes.
  7. SAUCE: Add the fried chicken back into the pan. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  8. SAUCE: Mix 1/2 cup mayonnaise with 4 tbsp sriracha. Incorporate the chicken from the pan and mix well. Add the 1 tbsp honey.
  9. BUNS: Divide the dough into 16-20 small balls. Slightly flatten the dough ball in your hand, or it'll be too thick after frying. [[Leave the balls to expand for 5 minutes]]
  10. BUNS: Blend two eggs until frothy. Dip the dough balls into the blended mix of the eggs and then breadcrumb it twice. [[the dough might expand and cracks may appear between the breading, which is why double breading helps!]]
  11. Deep fry in medium hot oil until the buns turn golden brown. Remove and let dry.
