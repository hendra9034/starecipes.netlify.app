The ingredients needed to make My Egg fried Rice with the malaysian curry sauce:
  1. Get 1 day old cooked rice
  2. Take Sesame oil
  3. Get Egg
  4. Get Cooked curry sauce. Of your choosing
Instructions to make My Egg fried Rice with the malaysian curry sauce:
  1. Add the Wok to the heat and when hot add the sesame oil.
  2. Next t add the rice turning over while it fries make a well in middle add the egg and stir quickly like you were making scrambled eggs.
  3. Next add the curry sauce and mix.
  4. Add to a serving bowl and add some chopped spring onion on top.
