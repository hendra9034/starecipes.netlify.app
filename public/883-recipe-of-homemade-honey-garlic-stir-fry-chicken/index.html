The ingredients needed to make Honey garlic stir fry chicken:
  1. Get For the marinade
  2. Take 2 chicken Brest cut in 1 inch cubes
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup honey garlic sauce, I usually make my own but that’s a different recipe
  4. Take Pinch salt
  5. Prepare Pinch ground pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tsp spicy paprika
  7. Make ready 1 tbs canola oil
  8. Take For the veggies
  9. Take 700 g mixed veggies
  10. Get 1/2 cup salted cashews
  11. Make ready 1 tbs soy sauce
  12. Take 3 tbs honey garlic sauce for the finishing

Steps to make Honey garlic stir fry chicken:
  1. In a bowl put all the ingredients for the marinade and mix well. I usually like to marinade all my meats overnight, but a min of 2 hrs will do.
  2. In a steaming bowl add 4 cups of water and bring to a boil. I usually use a pot and put veggies in a strainer and let them steam about 5 min than take them out and let them dry a few minutes
  3. On a big skillet put 2 tbs canola oil and add cashews and chicken. Fry at medium heat for about 10 min or till chicken is cooked right through.
  4. At his point add the veggies with the chicken and fry another 5 min or to your liking.
  5. Before you take the food off the stove add remaining soy sauce and honey garlic
  6. Enjoy. Kali orexi goes good with a nice glass of Cabernet Sauvignon
