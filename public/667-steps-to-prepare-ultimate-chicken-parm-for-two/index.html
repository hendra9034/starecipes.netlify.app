The ingredients needed to make Chicken Parm for two:
  1. Get 1/2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  3. Prepare 1 egg beaten with a generous splash of water
  4. Take 2 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  5. Make ready 1 cup your favorite marinara sauce
  6. Take Salt and pepper
  7. Make ready 1/4-1/2 cup grated parm cheese
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil (cover the bottom of the skillet to about a 1/4 inch)
  9. Take 8 slices mozzarella cheese (not fresh)
  10. Take Fresh basil

Instructions to make Chicken Parm for two:
  1. Pound the chicken thighs to a 1/4 inch or less thickness and season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Dredge the chicken in four lightly coating both sides and shake off excess.
  3. Dip the floured chicken in the egg mixture and then coat with panko. Set aside.Pre heat oven to 375 degrees on an upper rack.
  4. Using a non-stick skillet heat the oil over Med heat until it shimmers. Add the chicken thighs and lightly brown on both sides…about 3 or so minutes per side. Remove and place on a small cookie sheet and then dust generously with grated parm and then place four slices of mozzarella cheese on each thigh. - Heat your marinara sauce under low heat.
  5. Place browned chicken thighs covered with parm and mozzarella cheese in the oven for about 8-10 minutes until bubbling hot and then place under the broiler to lightly brown the cheese.
  6. Remove from the oven, plate each chicken thigh and scoop the warmed marinara sauce on top of each one and add fresh sprigs of basil.
