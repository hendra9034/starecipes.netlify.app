The ingredients needed to make Deep Dish BBQ Chicken Pizza:
  1. Take 3 cups unbleached bread flour
  2. Make ready 2 cups white whole wheat bread flour
  3. Get 2.5 tablespoons active dry yeast
  4. Make ready 3 tablespoons mustard powder
  5. Get 1 tablespoon pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  7. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  8. Get olive oil
  9. Prepare 1 chicken breast
  10. Make ready 1/2 of an onion
  11. Make ready 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  12. Make ready 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  13. Make ready 1 red bell pepper
  14. Get BBQ sauce
  15. Make ready Italian cheese Blend
  16. Take parmesian cheese
Instructions to make Deep Dish BBQ Chicken Pizza:
  1. First: prepare your dough by mixing together both flours, bread seasonings and yeast. Then add water until there is no more flour in the bowl. Let rest for 8-12 hours, Mix olive oil into the dough, let rest for 1 hour or less. Kneed the dough and separate into two even sized pizza (ingredients enough for 2-3 pizza crusts) put dough's into the fridge and store for a day before use.
  2. While preparing the toppings, form your dough to the pan in which it will bake and pre heat the oven to 450 degrees farenheight.
  3. Prepare chicken breast by slicing into strips. If preparing ahead of time, marinate the chicken in a mixture of red wine vinegar and southwest style mustard and chives. Cook chicken and cube it.
  4. Prepare the caramelized onions by slicing your half onion into strips and adding to a stove pot until they are sautéed. Add brown sugar and balsamic vinegar and mix well. Cook until the sauce evaporates into the onions.
  5. Prepare the Red Bell pepper by cutting it into small pieces, setting the pieces onto a paper towel lined dish and cooking in the microwave for 1 minute to get most of the moisture out.
  6. Assemble your pizza by putting a layer of cheese down first, then add an even layer of bbq sauce on top. On your toppings evenly in any order, and cover the top with your extra cheese.
  7. Bake on 450 degrees for 25-30 minutes, let sit for 15 mins afterwards before cutting into, and enjoy!
