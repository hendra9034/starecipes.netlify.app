The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Traditional Fried Chicken (Ayam Ungkep):
  1. Take 1.5-2 kg chicken thigh, bone-in, skin-on
  2. Make ready 1 tbs grated garlic
  3. Make ready 1/2 tbs garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1 inch ginger, grated
  5. Make ready 2 candlenut, grated / mashed (you can ommit if you don't have it)
  6. Take 1 tbs coriander powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tbs cumin powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbs turmeric powder
  9. Get 1 tsp nutmeg powder
  10. Take 2 tbs sea salt
  11. Prepare 2 tsp sugar
  12. Make ready to taste white pepper powder
Instructions to make Indonesian Traditional Fried Chicken (Ayam Ungkep):
  1. Mix all the spices. Rub to the chicken well. Let it stand for an hour.
  2. Put all the chicken pieces into the slow cooker. Set to "high" for 4 to 5 hours (depending how big the chicken pieces).
  3. After the chicken are cooked, at this point you can devide per portion in ziplock bags then put them in the freezer. When you need them, take out a portion then thaw it in the fridge.
  4. Preheat the oil then deep fry the chicken or like what I did here is frying them in an air fryer.
