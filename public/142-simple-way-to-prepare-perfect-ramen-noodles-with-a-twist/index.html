The ingredients needed to make Ramen noodles with a twist:
  1. Make ready 2 packets ramen noodles
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 2 mushrooms
  4. Get Green onions
Steps to make Ramen noodles with a twist:
  1. Get all your ingredients ready
  2. Place the noodles in a pot with hot water and the seasoning, next bring to a boil till the noodles are cooked, when the noodles are cooked drain the water and set aside
  3. Chop up your mushrooms and sauté on a pan till they get a golden color
  4. On the same pan as the mushrooms, fry your eggs
  5. Next place everything into a bowl with some green onions and enjoy !
