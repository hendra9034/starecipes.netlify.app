The ingredients needed to make BBQ Bacon Wrapped Chicken:
  1. Make ready 3 large chicken breast
  2. Get 1 packages bacon
  3. Get 1 packages cream cheese
  4. Make ready 1 sweet baby rays honey bbq sauce
  5. Make ready 1 salt
  6. Make ready 1 pepper
  7. Get 1 onion powder
  8. Prepare 1 garlic powder
Instructions to make BBQ Bacon Wrapped Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken in halves to make thinner. Then cut into 2 inch by 2 inch strips.
  2. In a bowl season with pepper, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Cut pieces of cream cheese to fit the strip of chicken, then wrap with bacon. Place in a shallow baking pan. Space the chicken a half inch apart.
  4. Preheat oven to 400°F. Bake for 30 minutes or until chicken is done. About half way through drain some of the fat off.
  5. When the chicken is done drizzle with bbq sauce and place back in the oven on broil. Let it broil till bacon is crispy and bbq sauce is bubbly. Take out flip all of the strips drizzle with bbq sauce place back in the oven and let that side crisp up and bbq sauce bubble.
  6. Take out of the oven and let cool for a few minutes. Enjoy :)
