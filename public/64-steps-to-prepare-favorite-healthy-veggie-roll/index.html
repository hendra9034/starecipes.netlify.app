The ingredients needed to make Healthy veggie roll:
  1. Prepare 1 egg thin (I prepared this flat bread earlier with almond flour and egg batter)
  2. Get 1/2 cup grated beetroot
  3. Get 1/2 cup chopped kale (you can use spinach)
  4. Make ready 1 piece boiled chicken breast
  5. Get 1 Onion
  6. Get Few green chili
  7. Prepare 1 table spoon mayonnaise
  8. Prepare Salt and black pepper

Steps to make Healthy veggie roll:
  1. Blend the boiled chicken breast, mix with mayonnaise, salt and pepper
  2. Mix this with crushed beetroot and Kale separately
  3. Take the egg flat bread for making roll
  4. Add a layer of green, then beetroot, then chopped onion and green chilies
  5. Place onion, chili over it
  6. Fold two sides of the bread first
  7. Start rolling, the sticky chicken paste should be placed at one side to stick the roll tightly.
  8. Cut the roll and enjoy. No cooking required.
