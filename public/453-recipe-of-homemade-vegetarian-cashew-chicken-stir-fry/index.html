The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Cashew Chicken Stir-fry:
  1. Get Cooking oil
  2. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, minced
  3. Get 10 oz plant-based chicken, cubed
  4. Get 1 onion, diced
  5. Get 3/4 oz cashews, toasted
  6. Get 1/3 cup carrots, chopped
  7. Make ready 4 baby peppers, seeded + chopped
  8. Make ready 2-3 ribs celery, chopped
  9. Get as needed Water
  10. Get Sauce
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp dry cooking wine
  12. Prepare 2 tsp sesame oil
  13. Get 1 tsp soy sauce
  14. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
  15. Get 2 tbsp cornstarch
  16. Get 1/3 cup stock
  17. Get Salt to taste
  18. Prepare 2.5 tbsp oyster sauce
  19. Take Dash white pepper
  20. Make ready 1 tbsp aged black vinegar
Steps to make Vegetarian Cashew Chicken Stir-fry:
  1. In a bowl, combine all sauce ingredients. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan. Add garlic, onions and chicken. Cook for 3-4 mins. Stir in carrots, peppers, celery, and a few tablespoons of water. Cover for 2-3 mins. Uncover and stir in 1/2 sauce mixture. Toss and coat evenly. Add in remaining sauce. Stir until sauce thickens.
