Steps to make Juicy chicken burger:
  1. Mince the chicken and chop the vegetables.
  2. Prepare the minced chicken. Add mayonnaise, salt, pepper, onions and some rice vinegar. Add some bread crumbs.
  3. For the vegetables, add the carrots, onions and beans in a nonstick pan and shallow fry them. Add the cooked vegetables to the minced chicken mixture.
  4. Now, it's time to fry the chicken patties. Shape the patties and roll them on bread crumbs. Fry them on medium - low heat.
  5. As a sauce, toss up some bell peppers, onions, with mustard, honey, and chilli sauce. Add sauce to taste.
  6. Last step: bread, mayo on top, patty, and some of the sauce we made. Don't forget the lettuce.
