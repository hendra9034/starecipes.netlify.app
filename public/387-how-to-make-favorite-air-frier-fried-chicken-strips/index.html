The ingredients needed to make Air frier fried chicken strips:
  1. Make ready 3 chicken breast
  2. Take Garlic powder
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Make ready 2 Tbls water
  5. Take 2 Tbls hot sauce
  6. Get 2 Cups corn starch
  7. Prepare 3 Tbls paprika
  8. Take 1/2 Cup oil

Instructions to make Air frier fried chicken strips:
  1. Trim fat off chicken breasts, slice in half and pound flat. Sprinkle garlic powder over both sides.
  2. Mix eggs, water, hot sauce in 1 bowl. Mix corn starch and paprika in a 2nd bowl. Add oil to the 3rd bowl. Dip in each bowl in the same order.
  3. Place 2 stripes in your frier or what ever will fill but not over fill your frier. Set your air frier to 400° for 15 minutes.
  4. Adjust time if needed.
