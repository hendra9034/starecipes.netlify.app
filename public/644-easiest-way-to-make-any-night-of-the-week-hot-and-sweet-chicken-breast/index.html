The ingredients needed to make Hot and Sweet Chicken Breast:
  1. Take 1.5 pounds Chicken Breast
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Paprika
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Pepper or 15 turns on a grinder
  8. Prepare Oil for the pan
Instructions to make Hot and Sweet Chicken Breast:
  1. Set oven to 400 for pre heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together all other ingredients besides the chicken and oil to make a spice mix. Reduce the Cayenne if you don't like heat.
  3. Get a pan ready, use foil if use wish, and oil it up.
  4. Take the chicken breast and lay it into the spice bowl. Get both sides coated and add them to the oiled pan.
  5. Slide the pan into the oven for 35 minutes and it should be good to go.
