The ingredients needed to make 20min chicken, chickpea & spinach curry - no jars in sight!:
  1. Make ready 400 g chicken breasts
  2. Get 200 g spinach
  3. Take 400 g chickpeas (drained)
  4. Make ready 3 tomatoes
  5. Take 1 inch ginger
  6. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  7. Take 1 red onion
  8. Prepare 2 peppers
  9. Take 1 table spoon curry powder (Tandoori Masala)
  10. Get 2 red chillis
  11. Take 250 g Greek style yogurt
  12. Make ready 100 ml chicken stock
  13. Take Squeeze lemon juice
  14. Take 200 g Rice
  15. Get Handful cashew nuts
Instructions to make 20min chicken, chickpea & spinach curry - no jars in sight!:
  1. Cook rice as per packet instructions üçö
  2. Chop tomatoes, ginger and garlic and chilli
  3. Place in the blender with yoghurt and Tandoori masala. Blend.
  4. Stir paste until the sauce thickens. The longer you reduce it the more flavour you get in!
  5. Chop the peppers and onions
  6. Stir peppers onions and chicken in the sauce until chicken is cooked üçó
  7. Add chickpeas and stock and continue to stir for five minutes
  8. Finally stir in the spinach üåø
  9. Drain the rice and season with pepper and lemon juice
  10. Crush cashews in a pestle and mortal
  11. Serve topped with a teaspoon of yogurt and crushed cashews. Enjoy üòä
