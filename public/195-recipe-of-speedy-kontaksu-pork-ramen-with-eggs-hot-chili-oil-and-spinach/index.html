The ingredients needed to make Kontaksu (pork) Ramen with eggs hot chili oil and spinach:
  1. Make ready 3 cups sifted all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/4 tps baking soda
  3. Get 1/2 cup very hot water (not boiling)
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Make ready For the pork
  6. Get 1 pound pork
  7. Take 3-4 slices think Sliced pepper bacon
  8. Get Salt & pepper for seasoning
  9. Take For broth
  10. Get 1 brown onion cut in quarters
  11. Take 3 cloves garlic
  12. Take 1 inch fresh ginger root
  13. Get 3 pounds pork bones
  14. Take 1 pack button mushrooms
  15. Prepare 2 quarts water
  16. Take Salt & whole peppercorns
  17. Take 2 boiled eggs Sliced in half
  18. Take 2 green onions Sliced very thin
  19. Get Hot chili oil if wanted
  20. Get Bean sprouts optional

Steps to make Kontaksu (pork) Ramen with eggs hot chili oil and spinach:
  1. Broth in a large pot add all ingredients I bones, ginger,ect bring to boil. Then turn down to low simmer every hour skim off scum cook for 8 hours add water if needed. I know this a long time but so worth it. Strain broth bring back to simmer
  2. Season pork with salt pepper wrap with bacon wrap with butcher twine add to broth cook for 4 to 5 hours slice in to 1/4 in slices put under broiler or torch the pork untill crispy. Set aside.
  3. Noodles make like my home made pasta or just buy ready made ramen noodles cook 3 minutes drain and put in bowls then broth place pork on top put some thinly Sliced green on on top and a boiled egg Sliced in half.
