The ingredients needed to make Split lentil soup شوربه عدس:
  1. Prepare 1 cup washed lentil
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup washed rice
  3. Take 2 diced onions
  4. Prepare 1 diced carrot
  5. Take 4 cups broth
  6. Prepare 2 cup hot water
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt and pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon cumin
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. Take 1 teaspoon turmeric
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  12. Take 2 tablespoon olive oil
