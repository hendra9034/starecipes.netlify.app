The ingredients needed to make Rotisserie Chicken Shawarma on the Kamado (BBQ):
  1. Make ready 5 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare Marinade
  3. Take 1 cup olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 cup lemon juice
  5. Get 1 tablespoon minced garlic
Instructions to make Rotisserie Chicken Shawarma on the Kamado (BBQ):
  1. 3 hours before cooking: combine ingredients in a bowl, marinate in a container, making sure to flip them every half hour.
  2. Start bringing your BBQ up to temperature of 150C (300F)
  3. Build your rotisserie spit by layering the chicken in a criss-cross pattern and clamp on each side
  4. Cook until the thickest part of your chicken reads 75C (170F)
