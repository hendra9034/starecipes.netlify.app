The ingredients needed to make Chicken nuggets:
  1. Prepare 700 g chicken breast
  2. Prepare 50 g crushed celery and garlic
  3. Prepare Salt to taste
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon of MSG
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon of white pepper
  6. Prepare 2 tsp lemon juice
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp cornstarch

  14. Get 1/4 all-purpose flour
  15. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder

  18. Prepare Final coating

Steps to make Chicken nuggets:
  1. Mix the chicken celery and garlic and salt and MSG in a blender then the white pepper and lemon juice and water and oil and cornstarch. Blend till you get a doughy mix.
  2. Mold to nuggets then cover in bread crumbs.
  3. Mix the solid ingredients of the batter, the gradually add cold water till its resembling thick cream
  4. Dip the nuggets in the batter, let exess fall from it, the coat in breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat a saucepan with oil for 10 mins then add your nuggets gradually. Fry till a brownish golden apears.
