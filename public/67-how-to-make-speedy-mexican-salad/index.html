The ingredients needed to make Mexican Salad:
  1. Prepare Rice
  2. Make ready Cheese
  3. Get 3 Eggs
  4. Get 1 Avocado
  5. Get Secret Sauce
  6. Make ready 1 Chicken Breast
Instructions to make Mexican Salad:
  1. Rince The Rice Until Clean
  2. Use The Cheese Grater For The Cheese
  3. Add Your Secret Sauce
  4. Cut The Avocado Then Smash It
  5. Boil the eggs for 4-7 Minutes And Add Some Salt And Pepper (to taste)
  6. Cook The Chicken Breast And Add 1tsp of paprika while cooking and also add salt and pepper
  7. Assemble This Thing
