The ingredients needed to make CAJUN CHICKEN WINGS, QUINOA WITH CAPERS AND OLIVES. JON STYLE:
  1. Make ready 1 lb. Chicken wings disjointed
  2. Prepare 2 minced garlic cloves
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup finely diced yellow onion
  4. Get 1/2 finely chopped jalapeño pepper or cayenne pepper
  5. Get 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp chili powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp paprika powder
  10. Make ready 1/8 tsp ground pepper
  11. Get 1/2 tsp. salt
  12. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
