The ingredients needed to make Bubble gum flavour boondi ladoo pastry:
  1. Prepare Sugar syrup Ingredients:-
  2. Get sugar
  3. Take water
  4. Make ready Flavouring
  5. Prepare Batter Ingredients:-
  6. Take gram flour
  7. Take water
  8. Get oil
  9. Make ready Oil for frying
  10. Get ghee
  11. Make ready Few strands of saffron
  12. Get Slivered pistachios
  13. Make ready Vol au vent cases
Steps to make Bubble gum flavour boondi ladoo pastry:
  1. In a bowl sieve the gram flour and add oil and water and make a smooth batter. - Let it rest for an hour.
  2. While its resting prepare sugar syrup 10 minutes before making bundi. - In a large pan add sugar and water and make sugar syrup. It should be of no strings at all. Add in flavour and colour to the syrup. I have used bubblegum flavour and used pink food colour. You can make green, orange, red etc
  3. Add food colour and mix the batter well. - In a large frying pan add oil and heat on medium heat. Once the oil is hot, hold a grater or holed ladle over the oil (about 2”)and pour the batter gently using a ladle. The bundi will start dropping in the oil. Let the bundi fry for a minute or so. It should all float on top and remove using a ladle and remove any oil by shaking it and put in the syrup.
  4. Stir the bundi and remove once all soaked in syrup. Transfer in a colander so any excess syrup is drained. Repeat for the rest of the batter and once the excess syrup is drained transfer in a dish and add ghee, saffron and nuts to it. You can also add raisins and trutti frutti. If not using flavour add cardamom powder. - I have given a twist to it. I blitz the boondi in a blender and made small ladoos and put it on top of vol au vent cases. Enjoy
