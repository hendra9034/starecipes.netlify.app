Steps to make Restaurants style fried chicken:
  1. Break an egg in a bowl and whisk thoroughly
  2. Add salt and white pepper to the egg. Whisk more
  3. Now take maida in a bowl. Add salt, red chilli powder, garlic powder ginger powder along with onion powder, oregano, chilli flakes and some more of white pepper and basil
  4. Mix all of it thoroughly
  5. For Marination: - Take chicken drumsticks in a bowl
  6. Add salt and white pepper to the bowl. Mix well with bare hands
  7. Keep aside for an hour to marinate
  8. Prepare Chicken: - After marination, take chicken pieces one after another and coat it thoroughly with the spice mixture, egg mixture and bread crumbs respectively
  9. After coating the chicken piece, deep fry it in a hot oil pan
  10. Hot and crispy Restaurant Style Fried Chicken is ready to serve.
