The ingredients needed to make Julie’s Oven Fried Chicken:
  1. Get 1 package chicken drumsticks (6 count)
  2. Take For the coating.
  3. Get 1 cup all purpose flour
  4. Take 1 cup corn meal
  5. Make ready 1 TBS. black pepper
  6. Get 2 tsp. garlic salt
  7. Get 2 tsp. Cayenne pepper
  8. Take 2 tsp. poultry seasoning
  9. Prepare 2 tsp. paprika
  10. Prepare 1 raw egg
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
Instructions to make Julie’s Oven Fried Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk the raw egg and milk together.
  4. Dip each drumstick in the egg mixture and then coat well with the flour mixture.
  5. Lay drumsticks in a 9x11 baking dish. Coat generously with extra virgin olive oil (about 1/2 cup).
  6. Bake uncovered in a 350°F oven for an hour.
  7. I served them with fresh baked buttermilk biscuits.
