The ingredients needed to make Chicken BBQ Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 cups cooked chicken cubed
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  3. Take 6 cups romaine lettuce chopped
  4. Get 3/4 cup ranch dressing
  5. Make ready 1 cup corn canned or thawed from frozen
  6. Take 1 canned black beans rinsed and drained
  7. Get 1 cup tomatoes seeded and diced
  8. Get 1 cup jicama diced
  9. Make ready 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese shredded
  10. Take 1 avocado diced
  11. Take 1/2 cup green onions thinly sliced
  12. Get 1 1/2 cups tortilla strips

Steps to make Chicken BBQ Salad:
  1. Place lettuce, corn, black beans and jicama in a large bowl and toss 1/2 cup of the ranch dressing.
  2. Arrange the tomatoes, avocado, green onion and tortilla strips over the lettuce
  3. Toss the chicken in the BBQ sauce and place it on top of the lettuce.
  4. Drizzle with remaining 1/4 cup of ranch dressing and addictional BBQ Sauce.
