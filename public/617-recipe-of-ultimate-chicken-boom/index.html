Steps to make Chicken boom:
  1. Cut the chicken into cubes then put it on a low heat with oil salt pepper and roast it for about 15 min
  2. In another pan pour 3 tbspns of oil than add the onion garlic cinnamon cumin chili powder until it brown than add the tomato puree and let them simmer gently for about 10 min
  3. After the chicken have roasted, add them to the sauce and let them simmer for 10 min
  4. For the final touch add 2 cubes of butter and turn off the heat and let the butter melt in the mixture's temperature
  5. As for the rice. Add oil than carrot and mais roast the about 3 min
  6. Add the rice and turmeric, salt,pepper and finally the water
  7. For 1 cup of rice = 2 cups of water
