The ingredients needed to make Tarragon Chicken Thighs with Veggies 游때游때游때:
  1. Prepare 2 large packs skinless/ boneless chicken thighs
  2. Get 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Prepare 15 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Take 1 tablespoon tarragon leaves
  5. Take 4 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup olive oil
  7. Take 1/2 cup white cooking wine
  8. Take 1 yellow onion, sliced
  9. Make ready 1 green pepper, sliced
  10. Make ready 1 red pepper, sliced
  11. Prepare 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  12. Take 1 orange pepper, sliced
  13. Get 1 1/2 pounds fresh green beans, rinsed
  14. Get 3 zucchini, peeled and sliced into chunks
Steps to make Tarragon Chicken Thighs with Veggies 游때游때游때:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs…….set to the side……
  2. In a bowl combine your black pepper, minced garlic, tarragon leaves, apple cider vinegar, olive oil and white cooking wine, set to the side……….
  3. In a large baking pan place your sliced onion, green pepper, red pepper, yellow pepper, orange pepper, green beans and zucchini……….
  4. Place your chicken thighs on top of veggies…….
  5. Pour your tarragon marinade all over chicken thighs and veggies…….
  6. Bake, covered, in a preheated 400 degree oven for 1-2 hours, or until veggies are tender and chicken is cooked thru, stirring once……
