The ingredients needed to make Ramen with Pan Seared Miyazakigyu Wagyu Ribeye:
  1. Get 1 Double 8 Cattle Company Miyazakigyu Wagyu Ribeye
  2. Get 1 TSP Togarashi Seasoning
  3. Get 5 Eggs (soft boiled)
  4. Get 1 BUNCH Green Onion (sliced into 1/4 inch pieces)
  5. Prepare 1 SMALL CAN Bamboo Shoots (drained)
  6. Prepare 8-10 Fresh Shiitake Mushrooms (sliced)
  7. Get 1 PIECE Nori (cut into bite-size pieces)
  8. Make ready 3 PACKAGE Fresh Ramen Noodles (Chef recommends Hakubaku brand)
  9. Get Broth
  10. Get 1 QT Beef Stock
  11. Get 1 QT Chicken Stock
  12. Prepare 2 CUP Water
  13. Prepare 1/4 CUP Soy Sauce
  14. Prepare 1 TBSP Sriracha
  15. Get 1 INCH KNOB Ginger (sliced)
  16. Take 4 Garlic Cloves (smashed and skins discarded)
  17. Prepare 4 Dry Shiitake Mushrooms
  18. Get 1 Yellow Onion (large diced)
  19. Take 1 Large Carrot (large diced)
  20. Get 1 Celery Stalk (large diced)
Instructions to make Ramen with Pan Seared Miyazakigyu Wagyu Ribeye:
  1. PREPARING THE BROTH - Heat a large Dutch oven on medium-high heat with 2 tablespoons of grapeseed oil. Once the Dutch oven is hot, add in the diced carrot, celery and yellow onion.SautÃ© for 5 minutes or until the onions are translucent. Add in the chicken stock, beef stock, water, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, sriracha, and shiitake mushrooms. Bring up to a boil, and reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. PREPARING THE EGGS - Fill a small saucepan with 1 inch of hot water. Bring the water to boil. Add in the whole eggs, and cover. Cook the eggs for 6 and a half minutes. Then strain, and run cold water over the eggs. Once the eggs have cooled, remove the shells. Cut the eggs in half.
  3. PREPARING THE MIYAZAKIGYU WAGYU RIBEYE - Heat a stainless steel sautÃ© pan on medium-high heat with 2 tablespoons of grapeseed oil. Season the Miyazakigyu Wagyu ribeye with the Togarashi seasoning on both sides. Sear the ribeye for 3 minutes on both sides (cook for additional or less time depending on desired doneness). Let the steak rest for 10 minutes. Then, cut the steak into very thin strips against the grain.
  4. FINAL STEPS - Strain the broth through a fine mesh strainer, and discard the vegetables. Divide the ramen noodles between 4 bowls. Pour the hot broth over them.
