The ingredients needed to make Yellow Rice and Lentils with Chicken:
  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Get 2 pound chicken thighs
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  5. Get To taste granulated onion powder
  6. Get To taste granulated garlic powder
  7. Prepare As needed oil to fry in i used peanut oil
  8. Make ready Yellow Rice
  9. Get 1-1/2 cup yellow rice mix
  10. Take 1 quart chicken broth
  11. Take 2 tablespoons harissa
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon capers
  13. Get 1/2 sweet onion diced
  14. Get 15 ounces English peas
  15. Make ready 1-1/2 cup water
  16. Take 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  17. Take Lentils
  18. Make ready 1 quart chicken broth
  19. Prepare 1 pound green split lentils
  20. Take 1/2 sweet onion
  21. Get To taste salt
  22. Get 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  23. Get Cheese
  24. Prepare 2 ounces fontina cheese chunks
  25. Make ready 1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese topping
Steps to make Yellow Rice and Lentils with Chicken:
  1. Heat the oil and dice the onion. Sauté the onion till translucent.
  2. Sauté for 5 minutes then add the lentils. Coat the lentils then add broth. Bring to a boil then simmer 20-25 minutes.
  3. For the rice heat the oil. Add the onion and harissa. When onions are translucent add the rice and coat all of it. Add the broth and allow to cook for 12 minutes.
  4. Add the capers, then the peas. Allow most of the liquid to cook out.
  5. Cut the chicken into bite size pieces. Season and coat with cornstarch allow to rest 20 minutes.
  6. Add the lentils to the rice. Add the water to the rice mixture and allow to simmer 8 minutes covered.
  7. Get the fontina cheese chunks and add to the rice. Add Reggiano cheese to top cover and remove from heat and do not disturb.
  8. Fry the chicken in batches. Move to paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
