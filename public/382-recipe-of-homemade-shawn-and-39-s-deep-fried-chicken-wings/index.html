The ingredients needed to make Shawn's Deep Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 4 cups flour
  2. Take 2 TBS garlic salt
  3. Take 1 tbs Cayenne pepper
  4. Take 4 tsp chili powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 3 tsp pepper
  7. Prepare 2 tsp basil
Instructions to make Shawn's Deep Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Mix all ingredients and store in a container for all chicken wing batter needs! This mixture will be good for many many batches of wings.
  2. Put wings in moderately salted water until ready to use, roughly 30 minutes.
  3. Coat wings in a small portion of batter.
  4. Deep fry for 9 minutes at 425 F. Peanut oil is recommended.
