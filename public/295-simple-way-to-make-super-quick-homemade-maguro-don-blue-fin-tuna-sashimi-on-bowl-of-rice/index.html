The ingredients needed to make Maguro don - Blue fin tuna sashimi on bowl of rice:
  1. Get 100 g Fresh blue fin tuna maguro
  2. Prepare 2 cup Steamed white rice, short grain
  3. Make ready Half Cucumber
  4. Get Half Avocado
  5. Get Nori, dried seaweed
  6. Take Soysauce
  7. Get Wasabi
Instructions to make Maguro don - Blue fin tuna sashimi on bowl of rice:
  1. Chop the maguro in dice.
  2. Slice both avocado and cucumber thinly.
  3. Put every ingredients on top of just steamed warm rice bowl. Then, pour a bit of soysauce mixed with a bit of wasabi. - - Please, please do not put too much soysauce, please….
