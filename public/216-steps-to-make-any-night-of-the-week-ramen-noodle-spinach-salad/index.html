The ingredients needed to make Ramen Noodle Spinach Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 bag baby spinach
  2. Take dressing:
  3. Get 2 TB olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 TB sugar
  5. Make ready 1 TB soy sauce
  6. Get 1 TB rice vinegar
  7. Get crunch:
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Grapeseed oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 package Ramen noodles
  10. Take 1/2 c slivered almonds
  11. Get 1/4 c sesame seeds
Steps to make Ramen Noodle Spinach Salad:
  1. Mix ingredients for the dressing together
  2. Heat up the tsp of olive oil on a medium sized pan
  3. Add in ingredients for the crunch and toast until golden brown
  4. Mix the crunch with the spinach and add in the dressing
  5. Enjoy this delicious salad!
