The ingredients needed to make City Chicken Detroit Style:
  1. Prepare 1 pound pork tenderloin, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
  2. Take 1 pound veal stew meat, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
  3. Get 1 medium onion, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  4. Prepare 2 celery tops, very coarsely chopped
  5. Make ready 1 cup flour
  6. Make ready 2 cups panko, made finer using a rolling pin
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup whole milk
  10. Get 2 eggs
  11. Take 20 4" wooden or bamboo skewers that have been sprayed with olive oil spray
  12. Take 3 cups vegetable oil
Instructions to make City Chicken Detroit Style:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Place onion in a small microwaveable bowl and microwave for about 15 to 20 seconds. Onion should be soft, but not mushy. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  3. Thread alternating pieces of pork and veal (about 5-6 pieces) on each skewer, threading a piece of onion between each cube of meat.
  4. Mix the flour with the salt and pepper. Dredge the meat skewers in flour, shaking off excess flour.
  5. Beat the eggs, add milk, and whisk together. Dip meat skewers into egg and milk mixture.
  6. Coat with breadcrumbs.
  7. Heat oil in large frying pan. Fry meat skewers in medium hot oil until lightly brown on all sides.
  8. Transfer city chicken skewers to greased baking pan. Top with celery. Cover lightly with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
  9. Serve with cucumbers in sour cream and a side of fried potatoes.
