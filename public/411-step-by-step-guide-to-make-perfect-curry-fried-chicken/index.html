The ingredients needed to make Curry Fried Chicken:
  1. Prepare 750 grams chicken drumsticks
  2. Prepare 2 teaspoons curry powder
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoons onion powder
  4. Get 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  6. Take 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. Get 1.5 teaspoons salt
  8. Make ready 1 egg
  9. Get 3 tablespoons cornstarch
Instructions to make Curry Fried Chicken:
  1. Mix all the ingredients except cornstarch. Put in the refrigerator overnight.
  2. Heat the oil in the deep fryer to 350°F.
  3. Add the cornstarch to the chicken. Mix well.
  4. Fry the chicken until they are fully cooked, about 15 to 20 minutes depending on how big the chicken pieces are.
