The ingredients needed to make Oriental Ramen Salad:
  1. Take *Salad Mixture:
  2. Make ready 3-4 C. Cubed Chicken Breasts or Small Beef Stew Meat, cooked
  3. Make ready 1 Package Cole Slaw Mix
  4. Make ready 1 C. Sunflower Seeds
  5. Get 1 C. Slivered Almonds
  6. Prepare 2 Bunches Green Onions, green parts included
  7. Make ready 2 Packages Chicken or Beef Ramen Noodles, broken up
  8. Make ready 1-2 Large Cans Sliced Water chestnuts
  9. Prepare 1 Can Baby Corn Cobbs, halved
  10. Get **Dressing:
  11. Prepare 1 C. Vegetable Oil
  12. Take 1/3 C. Apple Cider Vinegar
  13. Make ready 1/2 C. Granulated White Sugar
  14. Prepare Flavor Packets from Ramen Noodles (beef for beef, chicken for chicken)
Steps to make Oriental Ramen Salad:
  1. Mix salad ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  2. In another bowl mix dressing ingredients together. Pour over salad mixture and mix until everything is coated well.
  3. Cover bowl with lid or plastic wrap. Refrigerate overnight to let flavors combine and noodles to soften a little.
  4. Take out of fridge n give a good stirring to coat. Then serve n Enjoy.
