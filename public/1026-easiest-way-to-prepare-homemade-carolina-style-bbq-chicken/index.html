The ingredients needed to make Carolina Style BBQ Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs chicken
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Apple cider vinegar
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup franks hot sauce
  5. Take 2 Tbsp. Oil
  6. Take 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
Instructions to make Carolina Style BBQ Chicken:
  1. Combine 1/2 cup brown sugar, mustard, vinegar, hot sauce, oil, Worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper – mix well – pour 1 cup of mix over the chicken in a ziplock bag – refrigerate & marinate!
  2. Put remaining mix in sauce pan – add 1/4 cup brown sugar – simmer for 5 mins. stirring often (reserve for serving sauce)
  3. Grill over high heat 10-15 minutes – baste once with marinade (do not baste during last 5 minutes)
  4. Serve chicken with the reserved sauce & enjoy!!
