Recipe of Favorite Muesli Bars

The ingredients needed to make Muesli Bars:
  1. Get oats
  2. Make ready plain cereal eg. corn flakes, wheat flakes, rice bubbles etc. You can even use store bought muesli
  3. Make ready almonds, roughly chopped
  4. Prepare black raisins
  5. Take raisins
  6. Take Jaggery
  7. Take honey
  8. Take Ghee
Steps to make Muesli Bars:
  1. Gently roll the otas and plain cereal with a rolling pin. Just crush it, we don't want to make it powdered. This is necessary if corn flasks are added to blend it in the bars.
  2. Mix the almonds and raisins to the dry muesli mixture. Please note that you can also use dried fruits like cranberries, apricots and pumpkin, sunflower, sesame seeds.
  3. In a pan add 1 tsp ghee. Then add the jaggery and stir continuously till it reaches a viscous consistency.
  4. Add honey to the mixture and add this to the rolled muesli immediately. Please note, don't let the jaggery cool down otherwise it will not hold the muesli bar together.
  5. Mix the jaggery honey mixture with dry ingredients.
  6. Now take a baking tin or any other plate.
  7. Grease the plate with little ghee.
  8. Pour the mixture into the tray and press the mixture down as hard as you can with your hands or a large spoon. Rub a little oil on your hands/ spoon to stop the mixture sticking. Spend a few minutes doing this really well as it will help hold the muesli bar together.
  9. Once the mixture is arranged properly into the tray, use a large knife the cut into bar shaped pieces. You don't have to separate the bar pieces, just make slight impressions, so that once the mixture is gets cool it can be seperated from the cut marks.
  10. Let the bars be in fridge for 2-3 hours till they get harden.
