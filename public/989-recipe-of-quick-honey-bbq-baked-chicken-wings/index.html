The ingredients needed to make Honey BBQ Baked Chicken Wings:
  1. Get 1 Family Tray Of Chicken Wings
  2. Prepare Onion
  3. Get Bell pepper
  4. Prepare Minced Garlic
  5. Get Lemon pepper
  6. Take Garlic salt
  7. Get Paprika
  8. Get Chicken Bouillon
  9. Prepare 1 Cup water
  10. Make ready 1 stick butter
  11. Take Honey
  12. Make ready 1 bottle honey bbq sauce
  13. Get Crush red pepper
Instructions to make Honey BBQ Baked Chicken Wings:
  1. Clean and Rinse Chicken Wings
  2. Pre heat the oven to 400.
  3. Season your chicken wings, front to back evenly with lemon pepper, garlic Salt and paprika.
  4. In a pan, place your seasoned chicken wings. ADD, Bell pepper, onion, 1 tbsp of Chicken Bouillon, 1tbsp of minced garlic, butter and 1 cup of warm water.
  5. Wrap with foil and place in the oven for 45 mins.
  6. After the wings have baked for 45 mins, take out of the oven sprinkle crush red pepper (not too much if you don't like spicy foods). Honey and Bbq sauce. Put it back in the oven for 20mins uncovered.
  7. After 20 mins, take the chicken out and serve.
