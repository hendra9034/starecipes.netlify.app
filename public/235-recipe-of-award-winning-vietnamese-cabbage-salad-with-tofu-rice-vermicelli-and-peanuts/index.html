The ingredients needed to make Vietnamese cabbage salad with tofu, rice vermicelli and peanuts:
  1. Prepare rice vermicelli noodles
  2. Get vegetable oil
  3. Prepare firm tofu, cut into 5 mm slices
  4. Take white cabbage, shredded
  5. Prepare carrots about 300 g, grated
  6. Make ready ground white pepper
  7. Take basil leaves
  8. Make ready Vietnamese mint leaves
  9. Make ready coriander leaves
  10. Take crushed peanuts
  11. Prepare Sea salt and white peper
  12. Take Lime wedges to serve
  13. Prepare Asian Dressing:
  14. Make ready Juice of 1 lime
  15. Get sesame oil
  16. Prepare brown sugar
  17. Prepare rice wine vinegar
  18. Take kecap manis
  19. Take grated ginger
  20. Make ready long red chilli, finely chopped
  21. Take garlic clove, grated
  22. Get sea salt
Instructions to make Vietnamese cabbage salad with tofu, rice vermicelli and peanuts:
  1. To make the dressing, whisk together all the ingredients until well combined. Taste and adjust the flavours, adding more salt, vinegar, sugar or oil if necessary. 1. Soak the rice vermicelli in hot water for about 5 minutes, drain. 1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Sprinkle the tofu with a big pinch of salt and pepper and fry in the pan in a single layer. Cook for 2 minutes on each side, or until golden. Allow to cool, then cut into thin strips. 1. Combine the shredded cabbage, carrot, tofu, vermicelli, white pepper and herbs in a large bowl. Pour over the dressing and use your hands or a pair of tongs to toss until well combined. 1. Transfer to a serving plate and sprinkle over the peanuts. Serve with some lime wedges on the side.
