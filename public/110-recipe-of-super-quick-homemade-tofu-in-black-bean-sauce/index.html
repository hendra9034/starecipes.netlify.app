The ingredients needed to make Tofu in Black Bean Sauce:
  1. Take Black bean or tauci
  2. Take Gatlic and shallots.minced
  3. Prepare 2 cubes tofu
  4. Take 2 chicken thighs
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Take Flour
  7. Make ready White pepper
  8. Prepare Cooking oil
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp cooking wine
Instructions to make Tofu in Black Bean Sauce:
  1. Cut chicken.into bite size add egg white pepper and flour.Mix it until all the chicken coated with batter.Then heat pan and add oil make sure its hot then fry chicken.until golden brown.When done set aside. 1. In the same pan gry the tofu too until golden.brown then when done remove and set aside.
  2. Remove some cooking oil.from the pan.and sautee garlic and shallots until aromatic then add in.black.beans.Stir well and drop chicken.and tofu.Pour wine and a little water mix then remove
