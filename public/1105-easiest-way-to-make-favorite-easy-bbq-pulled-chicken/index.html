The ingredients needed to make Easy Bbq Pulled Chicken:
  1. Get 1 medium onion, sliced
  2. Get 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  4. Get 300 grams leftover cooked chicken, shredded
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp bbq sauce, your favourite
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  7. Get 1 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  8. Prepare garlic salt
  9. Take 3/4 tbsp firmly packed brown sugar
  10. Make ready 4 tbsp ketchup
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Chili sauce
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp pickled jalapeÃ±os
Steps to make Easy Bbq Pulled Chicken:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add in the onion and garlic. Fry until the onion has softened. About 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add in the rest of the ingredients but not the chicken, reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the sauce has thickened and become syrup like.
  4. Add in the chicken and continue simmering for a further 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve as you please.
