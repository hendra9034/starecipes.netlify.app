The ingredients needed to make Batter fried chicken:
  1. Make ready 7-8 pieces chicken
  2. Get 1 tbsp vinegar
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp curd
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ginger paste
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  6. Prepare To taste salt
  7. Take 1 pinch sugar (optional)
  8. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp cornflour
  10. Take 1 egg
  11. Make ready 1 pinch orange red food colour
  12. Make ready 1/4tsp baking powder
  13. Get 1 cup refined oil
