The ingredients needed to make Bakcang Medan:
  1. Make ready 1 kg rice
  2. Get 5 pcs chinese sausage
  3. Make ready 1 handful shitake mushroom
  4. Take 1 package chestnut
  5. Make ready 10 cooked egg yolk
  6. Take 500 gr pork shoulder
  7. Prepare 1 handful ebi
  8. Get Sauce
  9. Make ready 1 tbs salt
  10. Take 1 tbs sugar
  11. Take 1 tbs soy sauce
  12. Make ready 1 tbs oyster sauce
  13. Take 1 tsp gohiong
  14. Get 1 tsp chicken powder
  15. Take 1 tsp kecap manis + pepper
  16. Get Tools
  17. Make ready Daun lotus
  18. Get Tali untuk ikat
Instructions to make Bakcang Medan:
  1. Rendem ebi with hot water
  2. Rebus daun lotus
  3. Heat 2 tbs of oil, fry garlic and add ebi
  4. Stir till aromatic and put it aside
  5. Stir fry garlice Use the oil to stirfry lapchiong and shitake
  6. Add the chesnut and then the pork
  7. Add all the sauces, salt, sugar, soy sauce, oyster, chicken powder, gohiong, kecap manis
  8. Put it aside
  9. Use the leftover oil to fry garlic and add rice, use the sauce again
