The ingredients needed to make My Garlic Tomato + Mascarpone Brushetta on Garlic Bread Toasted❤:
  1. Take Cherry Tomatoes
  2. Take leveltbls Pasatta sauce
  3. Make ready Heaped tbls Mascarpone Cheese
  4. Get 1 large Vine Tomato chopped into tiny pieces
  5. Take dried Basil
  6. Get 3 Slices Garlic Bread Toasted
  7. Take 1 tsp Grated Parmesan Cheese
  8. Make ready sprinkle of salt
Instructions to make My Garlic Tomato + Mascarpone Brushetta on Garlic Bread Toasted❤:
  1. Mash the Tomatoes into a pulp add the Cheese and mix until smooth add the Passata mix into a paste.
  2. Chop up the large vine tomatoe into little pieces add half to the paste and stir to mix.
  3. Toast the Garlic slices and lay them next to each other
  4. Using a spoon share the paste between the 3 slices of toast. Next add the half of chopped tomatoes on top of each.
  5. Sprinkle the dried basil sparingly over the top of each. Add a little salt on each.
  6. Lastly Sprinkle the grated parmesan on top of each one and serve. Enjoy
