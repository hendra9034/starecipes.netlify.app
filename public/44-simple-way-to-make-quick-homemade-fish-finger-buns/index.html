The ingredients needed to make Homemade Fish Finger Buns:
  1. Get thick skinless cod
  2. Get polenta or gluten free breadcrumbs
  3. Get paprika
  4. Make ready salt and pepper for seasoning
  5. Take egg beaten
  6. Take For the slaw:
  7. Get small purple cabbage shredded
  8. Take carrot grated
  9. Prepare onion sliced
  10. Make ready mayo check it is dairy free
  11. Make ready chives chopped
  12. Make ready To serve:
  13. Take gluten free buns
Instructions to make Homemade Fish Finger Buns:
  1. Cut the cod into 12 even sized fish finger shapes - Crack the egg into shallow bowl
  2. Mix together the polenta, paprika and seasoning - Spread onto a shallow plate
  3. Dip each of the pieces of cod into the egg and then the polenta - Shallow fry the fish fingers in a large frying pan for 3-4 minutes on each side over a medium heat
  4. Make the slaw by stirring together the cabbage, carrot, onion and mayo - Lightly toast the gluten free buns - Place three fish fingers in each bun and top with the slaw
