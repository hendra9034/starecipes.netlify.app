The ingredients needed to make Stir Fried Chicken Keema:
  1. Prepare 1/2 kg Minced Chicken/chicken Keema
  2. Make ready 1 cup Onion chopped
  3. Get 2 Tomato medium sized chopped
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Chilli powder
  6. Make ready 2 tsp Coriander powder
  7. Take 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  8. Make ready 2 tsp Garam Masala
  9. Prepare to taste Salt
  10. Get Ginger Julians to garnish
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp Oil
Instructions to make Stir Fried Chicken Keema:
  1. Take oil in a pan, wait till the oil heats up then add in finely chopped onions and fry till it becomes soft.
  2. Add in ginger garlic paste and fry for about 2 mins. Stir occasionally.
  3. Now add in tomatoes and fry till the tomatoes become soft and cooked completely. Then add the Chilli powder, Coriander Powder, Turmeric powder, Garam Masala, Salt and stir well.
  4. Use a fine sieve or a muslin cloth to clean the minced chicken as the particles are very fine they might get washed away while washing.
  5. Now add in the cleaned Minced chicken and mix well. switch off the flame.
  6. Don't add water as the chicken will get cooked in it's own water. Mix well and in about 8-10 mins the chicken will get cooked.
  7. Add in finely chopped green chillies and ginger Julienne to garnish.
