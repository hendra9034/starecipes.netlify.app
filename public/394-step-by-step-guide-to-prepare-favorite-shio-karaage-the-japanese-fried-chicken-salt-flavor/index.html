The ingredients needed to make ”Shio Karaage ” the Japanese Fried Chicken salt flavor:
  1. Prepare 100 g Chicken Thigh
  2. Prepare ☆Marinade☆
  3. Take 1 Table Spoon Sake
  4. Take 1 Tea Spoon Salt
  5. Take 1 Pinch Sugar
  6. Make ready 2 Tea Spoon Freshly ground black pepper
  7. Take 1/2 Tea Spoon Grinded garlic
  8. Take 1/2 Tea Spoon Grinded ginger
  9. Make ready ☆For Frying☆
  10. Make ready 3 Table spoon Potato starch (corn starch can be alternative)
  11. Make ready Oil to fry
Steps to make ”Shio Karaage ” the Japanese Fried Chicken salt flavor:
  1. ☆Marinade☆ Cut Chicken thigh into 5〜6 cm cubes (2 inches), and put in zip lock bag with all the ingredients for Marinade. Let it sit for 1 〜 2 hours in refrigerator.
  2. After the Chicken is marinated, place the Chicken on kitchen paper to take off excess marinade.
  3. Place the Potato starch on a plate to coat the chicken.
  4. Heat oil to 180℃ (350℉) and fry chicken until its thoroughly cooked.
  5. Plate and serve!
