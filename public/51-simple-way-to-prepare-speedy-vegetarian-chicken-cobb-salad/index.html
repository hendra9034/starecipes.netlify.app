The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Cobb Salad:
  1. Make ready 8 oz grilled plant-based chicken, diced
  2. Get 1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped
  3. Get 10-14 cherry tomatoes, halfed
  4. Take 3 oz blue cheese, crumbled
  5. Take 2 tbsp onion, thinly sliced
  6. Take 4-5 small bell peppers, juilenned
  7. Get 2-3 boiled eggs, quartered
  8. Get Balsamic vinaigrette
Instructions to make Vegetarian Chicken Cobb Salad:
  1. Chop iceberg lettuce into bite sized pieces and divide onto plates.
  2. Place remaining ingredients on lettuce. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
