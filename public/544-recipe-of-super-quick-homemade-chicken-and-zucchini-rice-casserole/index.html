The ingredients needed to make Chicken and zucchini rice casserole:
  1. Make ready 1 pack chicken (4 boneless breast cut up)
  2. Get 1/2 huge zucchini diced (add last to casserole dish to fill up and eyeball how much)
  3. Make ready 1 1/4-1 1/2 cups par boiled rice uncooked
  4. Make ready 1/2 (3 lb) container of skim ricotta
  5. Take 1 bag Cabot shredded yellow cheddar cheese
  6. Get 1 can healthy request cream of mushroom soup
  7. Make ready 3-4 large garlic cloves minced
  8. Take 1/2 large onion diced
  9. Get Adobo, and salt and pepper
  10. Get 3/4 cup seasoned Italian breadcrumbs to sprinkle on top once done

Instructions to make Chicken and zucchini rice casserole:
  1. Mix all wet ingredients in large greased casserole dish
  2. Add chicken, zucchini, rice, onions, and garlic and mix well. Cover with foil tightly and bake covered for 1 1/2 hrs at 375°. Uncover and add breadcrumbs and broil for 5-10 min
