Instructions to make BBQ bacon wrapped chicken tenders:
  1. I take the chicken tenders and marinate in bbq sauce all day in a pan in the fridge
  2. Since this is messy, wear latex gloves if you like, take each of the chicken tenders, add salt and pepper, and wrap with a piece of bacon, secure with a tooth pick
  3. Use a wire cooling rack on top of a baking pan or sheet and place the tenders on the rack
  4. Bake at 350° for 20 minutes, remove from oven, turn the tenders over with tongs, and replace in oven for about 10 minutes to crisp bacon all over
  5. Remove from oven, and serve with your favorite dipping sauces and enjoy
