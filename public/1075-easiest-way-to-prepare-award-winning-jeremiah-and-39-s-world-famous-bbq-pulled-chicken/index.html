The ingredients needed to make Jeremiah's World Famous BBQ Pulled Chicken:
  1. Make ready 8 medium chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 bottle Kraft Sweet & Spicy Chipotle BBQ Sauce
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Lawry's Signature Steak marinade
  4. Take 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  5. Get 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Get 3 drops liquid smoke
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp chopped dehydrated onion
Instructions to make Jeremiah's World Famous BBQ Pulled Chicken:
  1. Combine all ingredients in crock pot and cook on low for 4 hours.
  2. After chicken is cooked, shred with a mixer on its lowest setting.
  3. Continue to cook another 1-2 hours.
  4. Serve on a stiff roll.
