The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Crispy Coriander Fried Chicken:
  1. Get 4 pcs chicken legs, cut each leg into 4 pieces
  2. Take 500 ml vegetables oil for deep frying
  3. Make ready Ground spices:
  4. Get 5 cloves garlic
  5. Get 2 inches turmeric, peeled, grilled on a pan for a while
  6. Make ready 2 tsp coriander
  7. Take 2 tsp salt or to taste
  8. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper peppercorns
  9. Get 500 ml water/coconut water
Instructions to make Indonesian Crispy Coriander Fried Chicken:
  1. Boil chicken with ground spices until the chicken cooked. Remove from the heat.
  2. Set aside. Let the chicken cool down then deep fry the chicken until golden brown.
