The ingredients needed to make Hong Kong Style Fried Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 Kilo chicken leg and thigh
  2. Get 2 teaspoons salt
  3. Take 2 teaspoons crushed black pepper
  4. Take 1 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine
  5. Get 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup ginger juice (grate then squeeze the ginger)
  8. Make ready Chives or spring onions
Instructions to make Hong Kong Style Fried Chicken:
  1. Combine all the ingredients together and marinate the chicken overnight (at least 2 hours).
  2. Remove from the marinade. (Keep the marinade for the sauce).
  3. Fry the chicken until cooked all the way through then let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. On a small sauce pan, pour the marinade and boil it for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Plate the fried chicken, then using a spoon, drizzle all the cooked marinade over the chooks.
  6. Garnish with chopped chives or spring onions. Bon appetit.
