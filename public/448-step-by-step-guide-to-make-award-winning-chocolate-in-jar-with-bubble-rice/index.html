The ingredients needed to make Chocolate In Jar with Bubble Rice:
  1. Take chocolate compound
  2. Prepare cooking oil
  3. Get bubble rice
Instructions to make Chocolate In Jar with Bubble Rice:
  1. Chop chocolate compound to thin.
  2. Heat the oil (in 2 minutes with a small fire) and mix the oil with chocolate.
  3. Cool the chocolate at room temperature daily until chocolate becomes viscous (can cooled in the refrigerator).
  4. Put bubble rice into the jar.
  5. Add the prepared chocolate into the jar.
  6. Serve…
