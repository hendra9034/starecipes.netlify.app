The ingredients needed to make Egg Rolls:
  1. Get 1 Cabbage (thinly sliced)
  2. Take 2 carrot (thinly sliced)
  3. Make ready 2 lbs ground pork
  4. Make ready 1 package Egg Rolls Wrappers (15)
  5. Prepare Oyster sauce, soy sauce, white pepper, Chinese cooking wine, chicken powder, sesame oil
  6. Take Green onions (chopped)
Instructions to make Egg Rolls:
  1. Marinate ground pork overnight with Chinese cooking wine, oyster sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, chicken powder and green onions.
  2. Add salt to the cabbage and let it sit for 1 hour. Squeeze the juice out of the cabbage.
  3. Mix the cabbage and carrot with marinated ground pork. Wrap the filling with egg roll wrappers.
  4. Deep fry the egg rolls with heated oil under golden brown.
