The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken Over Buttered Biscuits with Sweet Peas:
  1. Make ready 1 Can (8ct.) Butter Flavored Biscuits
  2. Take 1 Can (14 oz.) Sweet Peas, undrained
  3. Prepare 1 Can (5 oz.) Chicken Breast in Water, drained and flaked
  4. Prepare 2 Cans (10.5ozs.) Cream of Chicken Soup, undiluted
  5. Make ready 1 Can (15 oz.) Sliced Peaches
  6. Prepare 1 Tbsp. Garlic Powder
  7. Get 1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
  8. Prepare 2 Tbsps. Butter
  9. Make ready 1/4-1/2 Cup Milk or Heavy Cream
Instructions to make Creamy Chicken Over Buttered Biscuits with Sweet Peas:
  1. Preheat oven according to the directions on the biscuit container. Arrange biscuits onto baking sheet. Once oven is preheated cook according to package directions.
  2. While biscuits are baking In a medium pot add the cans of soup, can Chicken, black pepper, milk/cream, and garlic powder. On medium heat cook and stir occasionally til completely heated through. Set aside.
  3. Once biscuits are done remove from oven and let set for 2-3 minutes. In the meantime add the can of peas to a microwaveable bowl. Then add a dash of salt and 2Tbsps. Butter. Heat in microwave for 2-3 minutes til heated through. While the peas is heating open the can of sliced Peaches. Once peas are done start plating.
