The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken Pieces with Lime and Honey:
  1. Take 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  2. Take Juice from half a lime
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 dash pepper
  5. Take 2 tsp honey
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Get 3 tbsp flour
  8. Make ready 1 tsp mild homemade rub – https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/2667371-mild-homemade-rub
  9. Prepare Oil for frying
Steps to make Fried Chicken Pieces with Lime and Honey:
  1. Mix chicken, lime juice, salt, pepper, and honey. Put the mixture in the fridge all night. (at least one hour)
  2. Whisk the egg.
  3. Combine flour and mild homemade rub. (I put them in an airtight container)
  4. Dip the chicken one by one to the egg, then to the flour mixture. Every time you put the chicken in the container, close it and then shake it. Continue until all chicken pieces are coated with egg and then flour mixture.
  5. Heat the oil in a pan. I use medium heat. When the oil is hot, put the chicken pieces one by one.
  6. Flip them after a couple of minutes. When the chicken pieces are golden brown, and the pieces are a bit firm, they are ready!
