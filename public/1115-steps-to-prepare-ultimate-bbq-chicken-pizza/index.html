The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken Pizza:
  1. Take 1 Pre-made pizza crust (or make your own)
  2. Take 3 Chicken breasts
  3. Prepare 1/2 Onion, thinly sliced
  4. Get 2 cup Shredded mozzarella
  5. Prepare 1 cup Shredded sharp cheddar
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Sweet Baby Rays BBQ Sauce
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup Pizza sauce

Steps to make BBQ Chicken Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Bake crust alone until slightly golden brown
  3. Cut chicken into bite sized chunks and brown. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine chicken and BBQ sauce
  5. Spread pizza sauce on baked crust
  6. Spread BBQ chicken on crust next
  7. Top with mozzarella and cheddar
  8. Sprinkle onion over the top
  9. Bake until cheese is brown and bubbly
