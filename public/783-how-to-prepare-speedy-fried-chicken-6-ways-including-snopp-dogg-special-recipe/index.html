The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken 6 ways (Including Snopp Dogg Special Recipe):
  1. Make ready 1 cup Flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Corn Starch
  3. Get 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Black Pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Paprika
  7. Take 1 tsp Salt
  8. Make ready 1 cup Fish Cracker or Oats or Chips
  9. Prepare 500 ml Water (marinate)
  10. Make ready 4 tbs Sugar (marinate)
  11. Take 2 tbs Salt (marinate)
  12. Get 1 Orange Peel (marinate)
  13. Make ready 2 Bay leaf (marinate)
  14. Prepare 2 Chilli (marinate)
  15. Make ready 10 Chicken Wing
Steps to make Fried Chicken 6 ways (Including Snopp Dogg Special Recipe):
  1. Put all (marinate) ingredient into medium size pot, boil 15 minutes
  2. Pour marinate into chicken wings, let it marinate overnight
  3. Prepare batter, mix all other ingredients together in a zip lock bag
  4. Put chicken wing in zip lock bag, squeeze to allow chicken wing to be fully covered with batter
  5. Deep fry for 8 minutes in low heat, remove from oil
  6. Double fry for 1-2 minutes, remove from oil, and it's ready
