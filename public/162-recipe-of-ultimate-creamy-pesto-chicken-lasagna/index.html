The ingredients needed to make Creamy Pesto Chicken Lasagna:
  1. Make ready 2 Roma tomato
  2. Make ready 2 1/2 C shredded cooked chicken
  3. Make ready 2 C alfredo sauce
  4. Get 1/3 C pesto sauce
  5. Prepare salt and pepper to taste
  6. Make ready 1 egg
  7. Take 15 oz ricotta cheese
  8. Make ready 12 lasagna noodles
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 c shredded mozarella cheese
Instructions to make Creamy Pesto Chicken Lasagna:
  1. Dice tomato. Combine tomato and next 4 ingredients into small bowl.
  2. Mix egg and ricotta in different bowl.
  3. Spray a dish with non-stick spray.
  4. Layer lasagna with noodles, ricotta cheese, chicken mixture, mozzarella then repeat two more times. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese. - Cover with foil - To Cook - Bake covered in a 375 degree oven for 40-50 minutes or until center is hot and bubbly.
