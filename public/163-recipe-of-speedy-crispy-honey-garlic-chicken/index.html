The ingredients needed to make Crispy Honey Garlic Chicken:
  1. Prepare 3-4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 2 large eggs +1 tbsp water
  3. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Take 1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper
  5. Prepare 1 tsp smoked paprika
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper
  8. Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup canola oil
  10. Prepare Honey Garlic Sauce
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup honey
  12. Prepare 2 tsp minced garlic
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch +3 tbsp cold water
Steps to make Crispy Honey Garlic Chicken:
  1. In a medium sauce pan, bring honey, soy sauce, garlic and cornstarch plus water to a boil. Then whisk into boiling honey sauce until thickened. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. In a wide, shallow dish together the flour, cayenne pepper, paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. In a second shallow dish whisk together eggs and 1 tablespoon water. Dredge the chicken in the egg wash, then in the flour mixture, tossing to coat all sides of the chicken.
  3. Pour oil in a large skillet and bring to temperature over medium heat. Cook chicken in the oil, 4-5 minutes on each side, until crispy on the outside and cooked through in the middle.
  4. Toss chicken in the honey garlic sauce, coating all sides very well. Serve immediately.
  5. Notes: add 3 tablespoons buffalo wing sauce to the honey garlic sauce for a little sweet kick!
