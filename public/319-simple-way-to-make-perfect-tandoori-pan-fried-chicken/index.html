The ingredients needed to make Tandoori Pan-Fried Chicken:
  1. Make ready 2 Chicken Breasts
  2. Get Salt
  3. Make ready Pepper
  4. Prepare Tandoori Spice Mix
Instructions to make Tandoori Pan-Fried Chicken:
  1. Pat the chicken dry.
  2. Spread tandoori spice over the chicken and season with s&p.
  3. Preheat a pan to medium heat with a drizzle of oil (gas stove).
  4. Cook the chicken for 4 minutes on each side (6-8 for electric) while partially covered.
