The ingredients needed to make Homemade Masala Chai (spice tea) Blend:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup star aniseed
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup black peppercorns
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup fennel seeds
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup cloves
  5. Get 1/4 cup green cardamom
  6. Take 2 Tbsp ginger powder
  7. Take 2 Tbsp cinnamon powder
  8. Prepare 2 Tbsp nutmeg
Instructions to make Homemade Masala Chai (spice tea) Blend:
  1. Blend spices to a semi-fine powder in a blender. Sift if looking for super fine masala chai or leave coarse as I did 1. Package in a clean jar and use 1/4tsp per cup, with hot water, a little sugar and your favorite creamer. You can sieve your tea with a strainer or enjoy the bits and pieces of different spices as you chuck it down
  2. Enjoy!
