The ingredients needed to make Orange bread:
  1. Take 4 larg eggs in room temperature
  2. Prepare 1 tsp vanella extract
  3. Make ready 1 cup of sugar
  4. Prepare 2 table spoon unsalted putter in room temperature
  5. Get 3 table spoon extra virgin olive oil
  6. Prepare 1 orange zest
  7. Take 1 cup of fresh orange juice
  8. Get 2 and a half cups of flour
  9. Get 1 table spoon baking powder

Instructions to make Orange bread:
  1. In a large bowl mix the eggs and sugar with vanilla extract by mixer for 5 mins.
  2. Then add the butter, oil, orange zest and mix
  3. Add orange juice and mix.
  4. In another dray bowl mix the flour with baking powder.
  5. And then add the flour to the mix and stir with a spoon until combined.
  6. Divide the mixture into 2 trays and put it in the middle shelf of the oven for 25 mins.
