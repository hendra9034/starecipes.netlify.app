Steps to make Homemade Granola recipe #uwehallslife:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C). line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl mix the oats, nuts, seeds, cinnamon and salt. - - Pour in olive oil and honey. Mix well, until all coated.
  3. Spread the mixture in a thin layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for about 30 minutes, stirring every 8-10 minutes, until golden brown to your likeness.
  5. Let cool completely, then break the granola into pieces with your hands, transfer to a airtight container.
