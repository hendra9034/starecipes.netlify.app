The ingredients needed to make Duck Sauce Bbq üçó Wings üêî:

  5. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce

  7. Make ready 20 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions to make Duck Sauce Bbq üçó Wings üêî:

  4. Place your sliced onions all over chicken wings…….
  5. Pour your duck sauce mixture all over chicken wings and sliced onions…….
