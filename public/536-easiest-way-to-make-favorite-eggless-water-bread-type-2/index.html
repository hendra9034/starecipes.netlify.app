The ingredients needed to make Eggless water bread (type 2):
  1. Get 1 and qwatr mug atta flour(stone ground wheat)
  2. Get 1 and qwatr mug plain white flour
  3. Take 3 tablespoon brown sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 tsp dark vanila non alcohol
  6. Take 4 tablespoon cooking oil
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoon butter
  8. Make ready 2 mugs warm Milk,
  9. Prepare 1 mug plain warm water
  10. Make ready Filling/to looking of choice(here used nutella and fresh banana
Steps to make Eggless water bread (type 2):
  1. To a large -medium deep bowl preferably,add flours and then the well add the oil butter sugar salt vanila(find this way bit easier to manually mix whisk/.then add the milk and water warmed up helps again mix in better inshaAllah
  2. Now. Heat your pan and make your round batter bases,use a medium size spatula and level out the batter as even along edges as you pour in.then wait until brown and cooked on one side.
  3. Then cook.on another side..this has already oil and butter in so no need add more butter or oil and shud really work well esp if hav a non-stick medium (28 inch pan wud be preferably)you can make smaller ones if you are patient enof I guess!
  4. Do all tje rest or can even freeze this batter if cant complete cooking at once!Bismillah enjoy with filling of choice.wud be great with e.g. honey,blueberries banana strawberry. Maybe even some yogart if you feel healthy!…add say possibilities are endless Alhamdulillah!
