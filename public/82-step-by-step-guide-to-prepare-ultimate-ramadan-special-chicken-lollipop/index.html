The ingredients needed to make Ramadan special chicken lollipop:
  1. Get 500 grams Chicken lollipop
  2. Take 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  3. Take 1 tbsp Vinegar
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  5. Get 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  6. Get 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  8. Get 1/4 cup Cornflour
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp All purpose flour
  10. Get 2 tbsp Rice flour
  11. Prepare 1 egg
  12. Get 1 tbsp Ginger paste
  13. Get Oil for frying

Steps to make Ramadan special chicken lollipop:
  1. Whisk together soy sauce, vinegar, tomato ketchup, red chilli powder, garlic paste, salt in a bowl and toss the chicken lollipops in it. Marinade for half an hour.
  2. In the same bowl, add cornflour, all purpose flour, rice flour, egg and ginger paste and mix well to create a thick batter and coat the lollipops in it.
  3. Heat about two inches oil in a wok or kadhai to 350F/ 176C. Add the battered lollipops one by one to the oil in such a way that the meat is at the bottom and the bone is on top (watch the video to see how I do it). Fry these on medium high heat till golden brown â€“ approx 3-4 minutes
  4. Take these out on a tissue paper to blot out any excess oil and serve hot with schezwan sauce.
