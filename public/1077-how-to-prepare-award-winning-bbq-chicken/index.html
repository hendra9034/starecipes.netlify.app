The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken:
  1. Get 1 half onion chopped
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp minced garlic
  3. Take olive oil, extra virgin
  4. Take 1 can tomato soup
  5. Get 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp vinegar
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  8. Take 1/2 tsp mustard
  9. Take 5 chicken thighs
  10. Prepare 4 chicken drumsticks

Steps to make BBQ Chicken:
  1. Cook onion and garlic until soft, with olive oil in pan on med heat
  2. Meanwhile, in bowl combine all ingredients and stir
  3. In 13x9 baking dish place chicken skin side down and season with salt and pepper
  4. Add half of mixture on top of chicken
  5. At 400° cook uncovered for a half hour
  6. After half hour flip the chicken and add remaining sauce, cook another half hour!
