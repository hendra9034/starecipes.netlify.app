The ingredients needed to make Indian Egg Curry:
  1. Get Onion: 1 Large
  2. Make ready Tomato: 1 large
  3. Take Green Chili: 1 large
  4. Get Eggs: 3 boiled (whites and yolk separated)
  5. Get Red chili: half table spoon
  6. Make ready 1 tea spoon Salt:
  7. Get 1/4 tea spoon Turmeric:
  8. Get 1/2 tea spoon Cumin powder:
  9. Make ready 1/2 tea spoon Coriander powder:
  10. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon Chaat Masala:
  11. Make ready 1 Chicken stock cube:
  12. Prepare 2 table spoon Oil:
Steps to make Indian Egg Curry:
  1. Fry onions
  2. Add tomato and green chili
  3. Add the rest of the spices and let it cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add egg yolk and mash, add water to make the curry
  5. Once done add egg whites
  6. Serve with roti, naan, bun or pauv
