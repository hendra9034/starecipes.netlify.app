The ingredients needed to make Hot Fried Chicken with Veggies:
  1. Make ready Hot oil dip ———-
  2. Take 1 tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup ground paprika
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup pickle juice
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon sugar
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup extra Virgin olive oil
  7. Take Chicken———
  8. Prepare 2-1/2 pound chicken thighs
  9. Get breading 1————–
  10. Prepare 1-1/2 cup all purpose flour
  11. Take batter 2———
  12. Get 2 large eggs
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  14. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  15. Get 1/4 cup hot sauce
  16. Take 2 cup all purpose flour
  17. Take 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  18. Take 1 teaspoon celery salt
  19. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  20. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground onion powder
  21. Take 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  22. Prepare 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  23. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground sage
  24. Prepare 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
  25. Take 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  26. Take garnish ——–
  27. Get as needed pickle slices
  28. Get as needed oil to fry chicken
  29. Take Vegetables ————-
  30. Prepare 1/2 pound mixed vegetables
  31. Take sauce for vegetables ————
  32. Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  33. Take 1 tablespoon heaping prepared Horseradish
Steps to make Hot Fried Chicken with Veggies:
  1. Heat oil mix the hot oil ingredients and set aside
  2. Flour the chicken with the breading ingredients.
  3. Let sit 5 minutes
  4. Mix the batter ingredients
  5. Dip the breaded chicken into the batter and fry till done move to a bowl with a lid
  6. Add hot oil mixture after stirring it making sure the oil is mixed up and not separated.
  7. Add pickle on top and serve.
  8. Mix the mayonnaise and Horseradish
  9. Coat the mixed vegetables and fry
  10. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive oils
  11. I hope you enjoy!
  12. To make this mild omit the hot oil and hot sauce in the batter
