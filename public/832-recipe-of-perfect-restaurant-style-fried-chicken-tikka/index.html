The ingredients needed to make Restaurant style fried chicken tikka:
  1. Get 500 gm Chicken with bones
  2. Get 2 tbsp Curd
  3. Get 1 tsp Lemon juice
  4. Prepare to taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp Jeera powder
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Pepper powder
  9. Get 2 tbsp Cornflour
  10. Get 2 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  11. Get 1 pinch Food colour (optional)
  12. Get 1/2 tsp Chaat masala
  13. Take 1 tsp Tikka masala
  14. Make ready as needed Oil to cook

Instructions to make Restaurant style fried chicken tikka:
  1. Wash chicken thoroughly and allow excess water to squeeze out.
  2. Mix all the ingredients except oil and marinate for 2 hours in refrigerator.
  3. After 2 hours heat oil in a pan and put chicken to it and cook it water evaporates on high flame (approximately 10 min)
  4. Remove from the flame and after that take tikka sticks and skew chicken to the sticks.
  5. Now again take a pan heat 2 tbsp of oil and put chicken sticks to shallow fry it on high flame turning sides regularly.. Till golden.
  6. Sprinkle some chaat masala over it. Serve hot.
