Instructions to make Healthier fried chicken:
  1. Marinate pieces of chicken with salt and pepper, herbs and olive oil for a 3h or more.
  2. Preheat oven at 180C for 15mins
  3. Crush 3-4 cups of wheat cereal
  4. Beat eggs in a bowl then dip chicken in egg mix, then coat with crushed cereal.
  5. Bake in oven for 20mins
