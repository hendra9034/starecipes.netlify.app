Steps to make Clay Pot BBQ Braise Chicken With Thai Herbs:
  1. Wash,clean and pat dry chicken pieces
  2. Slice lemongrass,crush garlic and shallots
  3. Set pot on medium heat,this receive I use clay pot to get good aroma from herbs ,simple pot is fine :) ,add some oil enough to fry crush garlic and shallots ,fry until light brown
  4. Add kafir lime leaves,lemongrass ,stir until fragrance
  5. Add chicken pieces ,skin side down ,brown all pieces
