Steps to make $1.73 Chicken sandwich:
  1. Brine the chicken by placing it in a bowl with salt and water. Set the oven to 400F
  2. Once the oven reaches 400, cover a cast iron skillet with foil and add chicken (you may need more than 1 skillet). Place in the oven
  3. Measure out the mayo and mustard mixture and mix together.
  4. Chop the celery and keep 16oz, refrigerate the rest.
  5. Tip: it's easy to propagate celery if you have a garden. Save the base of the stalk and place in water. Removing the cost of celery reduces the price of each full sandwich by $0.06.
  6. Check on the chicken after about half an hour. Once the skin starts to turn golden brown. Remove and slice a sample section in two to ensure its cooked all the way through
  7. Shred the chicken by holding the breast in place with one fork and tearing with the other.
  8. Mix in the mayo mustard mixture and celery.
  9. If you cooked more than 3lb of chicken feed the extra to your dog.
  10. Assemble your bread and add an apple. Tada!
