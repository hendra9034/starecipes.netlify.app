The ingredients needed to make Honey Mustard Chicken w/ Greek Seasoned Veggies:
  1. Make ready 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  2. Take 1 Lg. Zucchini, cubed
  3. Get 2 Sm. Yellow Squash, cubed
  4. Prepare 2 Lg. Potatoes, peeled and sliced
  5. Take 1 C. Yellow Mustard
  6. Prepare 1/4 C. Honey
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp. Black Pepper
  8. Make ready Greek Seasoning Blend
  9. Get Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Instructions to make Honey Mustard Chicken w/ Greek Seasoned Veggies:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a 13X9 baking dish cover with parchment paper. Set aside. - In a large mixing bowl add the veggies. Drizzle with olive oil and some of the Seasoning Blend about 3Tbsps. Mix well. Spread over bottom of baking dish and make 4 indentations for the chicken. Set aside.
  3. In a medium mixing bowl add the Mustard, Honey, and Black Pepper. Mix well (add more or less honey according to how u like it). Set aside. - Place Chicken Breasts in baking dish indentations. Cover chicken with the Honey Mustard mixture.
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes til chicken is no longer pink and cooked through. Remove from oven. Cover with foil and let set for 5 minutes. Then serve immediately and Enjoy ðŸ˜‹.
