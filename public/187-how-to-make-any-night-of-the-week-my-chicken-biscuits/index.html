The ingredients needed to make My Chicken Biscuits:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken thighs or brests (rinsed)
  2. Get 1 cup oil
  3. Get 1/2 stick butter
  4. Get 1 cup corn starch
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cups soy milk for chicken or 3 eggs
  7. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp basil
  11. Take 1/8 tsp ground ginger
  12. Get 4 pickle stackers
  13. Get Dash hot sauce
  14. Take Salt and pepper to taste
Steps to make My Chicken Biscuits:
  1. In a medium or large skillet add your oil and butter.
  2. In medium bowl add the soy milk or eggs and dashes of hot sauce. Submerge chicken.
  3. In a medium mixing bowl add flour and corn starch and all seasonings. Mix well. Add chicken.
  4. Fry until done.
  5. Let chicken drain on paper towel or cooling racks and now add your chicken to the biscuit along with the pickles and dressing of your choice. - (see recipe)
