The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread (+glutinous rice flour):
  1. Make ready 200 g pumpkin
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Prepare 30 g dried raisins
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoon sugar
  5. Get 6 g dried yeast
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 3 tablespoon glutinous rice flour
  8. Get 9 tablespoon bread flour
Instructions to make Pumpkin Bread (+glutinous rice flour):
  1. Cut and microwave pumpkin. Soak dried grape. Put eggs and pumpkin in a food processor and smooth.
  2. Put dry ingredients and mix with spatula well.
  3. Mix well and make smooth dough. Kneading is not necessary. Cover with film and rest the dough until the size is double. Then mix again with spatula and add raisins and rest again.
  4. Put the bread dough in mold. And rest for 20 minutes.
