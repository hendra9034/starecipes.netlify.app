The ingredients needed to make Last Minute BBQ Chicken:
  1. Get 1 Chicken Breast
  2. Make ready 2 Sliced Golden Potatos
  3. Prepare 1/8 Cup sliced Onion
  4. Prepare 1/8 Cup Diced Jalape√±os
  5. Get 1 Teaspoon chopped Cilantro
  6. Get Salt
  7. Get Pepper
  8. Make ready Olive Oil
  9. Make ready BBQ Sauce - Your Favorite
  10. Make ready 2 Parchment papers (or enough to fold over the chicken)
Steps to make Last Minute BBQ Chicken:
  1. Preheat the oven to 395¬∞F
  2. On a cookie sheet lay down one piece of parchment paper. Thinly slice the golden potatos and place them in two even lines in the middle of the parchment.
  3. Lightly drizzle the potatos with olive oil. Then sprinkle the salt and pepper to your liking.
  4. Lay the chicken breast on the center of potatos. Add additional salt and pepper if you choose. Pour a thick layer of your favorite BBQ sauce over the top of the chicken.
  5. Sprinkle the cilantro over the BBQ sauce. Add the onions and jalapenos.
  6. Cover the entire dish with another piece of parchment and fold over each of the edges to create a seal. (I fold it over twice for good measure)
  7. Place the cookie sheet in the oven for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove and enjoy!
