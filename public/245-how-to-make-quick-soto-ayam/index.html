The ingredients needed to make Soto Ayam:
  1. Prepare Seasoning
  2. Make ready 1 stick lemongrass
  3. Take 3 keffir lime leaves
  4. Prepare 2 of 5cm turmeric or 2tsp turmeric powder
  5. Get 4 cloves garlic
  6. Make ready 5 Shallots
  7. Prepare 2 cm galangal
  8. Take 2 Bay leaves
  9. Get Salt
  10. Get Pepper
  11. Take Mushroom powder
  12. Take 2 litres water or chicken broth
  13. Get Topping
  14. Prepare 500 gr chicken
  15. Take 1 pack vermicelli
  16. Get 4 boiled eggs
  17. Take 1 tomato
  18. Make ready 1 stalk celery/coriander
  19. Get 1 spring onion
  20. Make ready Fried Shallots for sprinkle (made out of 150gr Shallots)
  21. Prepare 2 limes
Steps to make Soto Ayam:
  1. In a blender add turmeric, garlic, shallots, galangal, salt, pepper, mushroom powder and oil. Blend til smooth (you can add water to help the blender work)
  2. In a pot, pour turmeric mixture, lime leaves, bay leaves, and lemongrass sauté til it fragrant, (if you add water to your mixture, cook until all of the water evaporate and all of the oil is coming out)
  3. Pour water or chicken broth to the pot, once it boiled add the chicken, turn the heat to medium low and cook it for 40 minutes.
  4. While the broth cooked, chop the topping (tomato, celery and spring onion) slice the egg, soak the vermicelli in hot water for 2 minutes and slice the lime.
  5. For the fried Shallots, slice the Shallots and fried it on medium high until it almost golden, take it out and sprinkle some salt.
  6. After 40 minutes, take out the chicken, let it cool and shred it. Check the taste of the broth, add some more salt and mushroom powder if needed.
  7. To assembly, add vermicelli on the bottom, and then tomatoes, celery, spring onion, chicken and egg. Pour the broth and then sprinkle some fried Shallots.
  8. Your Soto Ayam is ready to serve, don't forget to squeeze some lime juice!
