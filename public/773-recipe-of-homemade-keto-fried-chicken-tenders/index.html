The ingredients needed to make Keto fried Chicken tenders:
  1. Get Boneless skinless tenders or breast cutlets
  2. Take 1 tsp paprika
  3. Take 1 tsp sea salt
  4. Get 1/2 cup heavy cream
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Get 1.5 cups coconut flour
  7. Get 1 cup almond flour
  8. Prepare Cooking oil for frying

Instructions to make Keto fried Chicken tenders:
  1. In large bowl, combine eggs, heavy cream, paprika, and salt
  2. Add and toss chicken in mixture
  3. Coat chicken in separate bowl where you have combined coconut and almond flours well
  4. Add chicken to HOT oil in Instant pot set to saute and turn after 3-4 minutes until both sides are done
