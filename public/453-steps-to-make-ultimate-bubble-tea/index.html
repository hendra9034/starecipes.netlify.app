Steps to make Bubble Tea:
  1. make tapioca pearls as to directions on type purchased
  2. place finshed soft plump pearls of tapioca into simple syrup of 1/2 hot water 1/2 brown sugar to sweeten them up wait 15 mins
  3. while waiting heat 1 cup water to make the green tea concentrated by adding all 3 tea bags to one cup of hot water
  4. then in a cup add sweetend tapioca and set aside
  5. in a shaker mix a little condensed milk a little ice and a dash of water and a dash of concentrated green tea and some flavour. adjust amounts to your desired taste preference.
  6. shake shaker and pour into cup with tapioca pearls put bubble tea straw in or use a spoon and enjoy!
