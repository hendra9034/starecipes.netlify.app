The ingredients needed to make Pop_p Fried Chicken (PFC):
  1. Get 250 grams Chicken
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons meat masala
  3. Take 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  4. Prepare Salt as required
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida/hing
  6. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon red chilli powder
  7. Get 1 egg
  8. Get Coating Ingredients
  9. Get 1 tablespoon all purpose flour (maida)
  10. Get 1 cup breadcrumbs
Steps to make Pop_p Fried Chicken (PFC):
  1. Mix well all the marination ingredients and let it rest for about 1 hour or more
  2. Add 1/2 cup water to all purpose flour and mix well
  3. Dip the marinated chicken well in all purpose flour solution fix and then coat well with breadcrumbs
  4. Deep fry/air fry. Hot crispy and crunchy PFC is ready!! Voila
