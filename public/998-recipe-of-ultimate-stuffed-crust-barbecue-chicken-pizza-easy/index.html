The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Crust Barbecue Chicken Pizza (EASY):
  1. Prepare Refrigerated pizza crust (your favorite brand)
  2. Take 6-8 pieces string cheese
  3. Make ready Sweet Baby Ray's original BBQ sauce
  4. Prepare Shredded rotisserie chicken (approx. 1 cup)
  5. Get 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Stuffed Crust Barbecue Chicken Pizza (EASY):
  1. Preheat oven per the instructions on your pizza crust. (Usually about 425°F)
  2. Unroll the crust onto a baking sheet. Place the pieces of string cheese around the edges of the crust. You may have to cut some in half to make them fit. (It doesn't have to be perfect)
  3. Roll the string cheese so that it's all cozy inside the crust. Just up and over the cheese. Seal the edges to prevent cheese leakage.
  4. Spread some BBQ sauce onto the bottom of the crust, follow with chicken, then a little more sauce (I mix the sauce in with the chicken a bit). Top everything with shredded mozzarella.
  5. Bake for about 15-20 mins. And yer done!!
