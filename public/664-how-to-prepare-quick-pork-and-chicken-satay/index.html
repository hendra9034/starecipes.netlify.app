The ingredients needed to make Pork and Chicken Satay:
  1. Take marinate
  2. Take 300 grams Lean pork
  3. Prepare 300 grams Chicken Fillet
  4. Get 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
  5. Take 1 Tablespoon Fish Sauce
  6. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Sriracha
  7. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Curry powder
  8. Get 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  9. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Peanut butter
  10. Make ready 1/4 Cup Pineapple juice
  11. Get 3 Cloves Garlic, minced
  12. Make ready Turmeric powder
  13. Take Sesame oil
  14. Get Dipping Sauce
  15. Make ready 230 ml Coconut milk / Evaporated mil
  16. Make ready 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  17. Get 1 Tablespoon Curry powder
  18. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Soy sauce
  19. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Sriracha
  20. Prepare 4 Tablespoons Peanut Butter
  21. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Vinegar
  22. Take Garnish / Side Dish
  23. Prepare Cabbage
  24. Make ready Carrots
  25. Take Tomatoes
  26. Get Japanese mayo
  27. Get Roasted Sesame Seeds
Instructions to make Pork and Chicken Satay:
  1. Cut pork and chicken into 1-inch chunks wash and drain.
  2. In mixing bowl, combine all the marinate Ingredients together with chunks pork and chicken cover and set aside.
  3. To make the peanut sauce, whisk together coconut milk, brown sugar, curry powder, soy sauce, Sriracha, peanut butter and vinegar in sauce pan. Whisk or stir occasionally until desired consistency is reached; set aside.
  4. Thread chicken and pork onto skewers.
  5. Preheat grill. Add skewers to grill, and cook, turning occasionally, until the chicken and pork is completely cooked through, reaching an internal temperature of 165 degrees F, about 12-15 minutes.
  6. Garnish/ side dish: chop cabbage and carrots as thinly as possible (you can also add mangoes) then add japanese mayo. Also cut tomatoes or you can just use cherry tomatoes.
  7. Serve immediately with peanut sauce.
