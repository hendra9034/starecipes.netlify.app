The ingredients needed to make Crushed Crispy Fried Chicken (Ayam goreng geprek):
  1. Prepare 1 pcs chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 pcs chicken thigh
  3. Make ready 100 g seasoned flour
  4. Take 1 pcs egg
  5. Take 20 pcs bird eye chillies
  6. Get 2 cloves garlics
  7. Prepare Salt & sugar
  8. Take Cooking oil
  9. Get Indonesian basil
Steps to make Crushed Crispy Fried Chicken (Ayam goreng geprek):
  1. Prepare the chicken then cut
  2. Beat the egg, rub the chicken.
  3. Cover the chicken with the seasoned flour
  4. Make the chilli: 20 bird eye chilli + garlics + salt and sugar
  5. Deep fry the chicken until crisp and golden brown and fragrant.
  6. Crush and shred the chicken on the sambal and garnish with indonesian basil.
