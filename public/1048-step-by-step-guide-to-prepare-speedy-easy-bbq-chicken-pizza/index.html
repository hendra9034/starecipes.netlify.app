Instructions to make Easy BBQ chicken pizza:
  1. 2 cans of premium breast chicken and 1 whole bottle of any kind of bbq sauce.
  2. I took these spices and mixed it in a bowl with the chicken and bbq sauce.
  3. Chop up a red onion (in small pieces) and mix in the bowl of chicken and bbq sauce.
  4. Pour the rest of the bbq sauce on the crust and then take your bbq chicken mix and pour all over the crust. I added a little more red onion after I got it all put on. After that, have your oven pre-heated to 350°F let bake for around 20-25 minutes.
