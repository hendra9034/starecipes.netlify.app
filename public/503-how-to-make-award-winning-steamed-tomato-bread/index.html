The ingredients needed to make Steamed Tomato Bread:
  1. Get tomato
  2. Prepare egg
  3. Prepare dried yeast
  4. Make ready sugar
  5. Prepare salt
  6. Get glutinous rice powder
  7. Make ready bread flour
  8. Prepare dried tomato (soaked)
  9. Make ready chopped green onion
Instructions to make Steamed Tomato Bread:
  1. Remove tomato skin and blend egg and tomato. The total amount of egg and tomato mixture is 250cc this time. Add dried yeast, sugar, salt, glutinous rice flour and bread flour.
  2. Mix well and wrap with film. Rest until the size is double.
  3. Put chopped dried tomato and green onion. Ferment again.
  4. Put the batter in mold and steam with high flame for 1 hour.
  5. This steamed bread is lovely colored and very moist. Baking this batter on pan like pancake is also delicious.
