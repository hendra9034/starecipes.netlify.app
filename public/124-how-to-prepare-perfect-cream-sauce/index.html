The ingredients needed to make Cream sauce:
  1. Get 2 tablespoons butter
  2. Get 2 tablespoons flour
  3. Get 1 cup chicken stock
  4. Prepare Little salt
  5. Make ready Little paprika
  6. Make ready 2 egg yolks
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup cream
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  9. Take Celery salt
  10. Prepare Onion seasoning
  11. Make ready Red pepper
