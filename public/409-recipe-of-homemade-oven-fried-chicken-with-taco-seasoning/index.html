The ingredients needed to make Oven Fried Chicken with taco seasoning:
  1. Prepare 1 kg (about 2 lbs) chicken thigh bone-in
  2. Get 2 tbs taco seasoning
  3. Prepare 2 large eggs (lightly beaten)
  4. Make ready 50 gr all purpose flour
  5. Take 60 gr panko or bread crumb
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  8. Get To taste ground black pepper

Steps to make Oven Fried Chicken with taco seasoning:
  1. Coat the chicken pieces with taco seasoning. Put it in the fridge all night.
  2. Put beaten egg, flour and panko in seperate bowls. Add salt, garlic powder and pepper to the panko. Stir to combine.
  3. Prepare a baking sheet lined with alumunium foil then put wire rack on top. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  4. Dip chicken pieces in flour to coat all sides. Dip in egg then in panko mixture.
  5. Heat about 1/4 cup of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook chikcken about 2 - 3 minutes per-side.
  6. Place chicken on the wire rack. Bake for about 40 - 50 minutes or until fully cooked.
