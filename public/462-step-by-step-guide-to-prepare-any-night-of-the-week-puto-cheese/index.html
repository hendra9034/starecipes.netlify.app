The ingredients needed to make Puto Cheese:
  1. Make ready ingredients
  2. Take ✔️1 cup cake flour (or all purpose flour)
  3. Take ✔️1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare ✔️1 tbsp. baking powder
  5. Prepare ✔️1 pc egg
  6. Make ready ✔️1/4 cup evaporated milk (or whole/fresh milk)
  7. Prepare ✔️3/4 cup water
  8. Make ready ✔️1 tbsp. melted butter
  9. Make ready ✔️1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  10. Get ✔️cheese toppings (or any desired toppings)
  11. Take procedure
  12. Take . Combine cake flour, baking powder and sugar. Sift to remove lumps. Set aside
  13. Make ready . Combine egg, milk, water, and melted butter. Mix well. Add to the flour mixture. Mix well, but slowly, until smooth. Rest 15 minutes
  14. Take Enjoy!
  15. Prepare . Pour in molds, filling only 3/4 as it will rise as you steam. Top with desired toppings. I used cheese
  16. Take RECIPE NOTES:
  17. Get . Steam 10 minutes. Longer for larger molds. I used medium-sized
  18. Get ✔️Make sure the baking powder is still good before using or it will not rise. How to know if it's still good? Pour 1/2 cup boiling water over 1/2 tsp baking powder. If it bubbles, it's still good
  19. Make ready . After 10 minutes, remove from heat. Let it cool a little before removing from molds
  20. Get ✔️Grease or put oil ONLY AT THE BOTTOM of the mold. DO NOT grease the sides to achieve dome appearance of the puto
  21. Take ✔️DO NOT OVERCOOK. The puto becomes kuchinta when overcooked
