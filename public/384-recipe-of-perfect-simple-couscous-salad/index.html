The ingredients needed to make Simple Couscous Salad:
  1. Take couscous
  2. Make ready stock cube (I used chicken stock)
  3. Make ready Boiling water
  4. Get red pepper
  5. Get large tomatoe or handful of cherry tomates
  6. Get Olives
  7. Prepare Feta
  8. Take cucumber deseeded and sliced
  9. Get Juice of half a lemon
  10. Take Olive oil
Instructions to make Simple Couscous Salad:
  1. Crumble the stock cube over the couscous and pour boiling water to just about cover the couscous. Cover with a lid or plate and leave for 10 minutes or until the water has been fully absorbed
  2. Chop up the vegetables and add to the cooked couscous and feta cheese, mix well and squeeze the juice of half a lemon and a drizzle of olive oil to taste.
