The ingredients needed to make Island Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 Chicken breast
  2. Take 1 tsp Vinegar
  3. Prepare Pinch Salt
  4. Make ready Pinch Pepper
  5. Take 2 Spring onions chopped
  6. Take 1 tsp Garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 /Onion
  8. Prepare leaves Mint
  9. Make ready Chilli powder
  10. Prepare Corn starch
  11. Get Eggs
Instructions to make Island Chicken:
  1. Marinate all ingredients but flour for 30 min 1. Add flour and eggs, mix well
  2. Shallow Fry all ingredients and as the edges start crisping watch the pieces disappear
