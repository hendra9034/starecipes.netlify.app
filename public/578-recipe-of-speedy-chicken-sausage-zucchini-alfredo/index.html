The ingredients needed to make Chicken Sausage Zucchini Alfredo:
  1. Get 4 quarts water
  2. Make ready 16 oz egg noodles
  3. Get 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup sweet yellow onion, émincéd
  5. Take 100 ml olive oil
  6. Prepare 1 cup zucchini, julienned
  7. Make ready 2 cups yellow squash, paysanned
  8. Take 2 tbsp salt
  9. Take 3 tsp pepper
  10. Get 2 tsp basil
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  12. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  13. Prepare 1 cup whole milk
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  15. Take 2 tsp parsley
  16. Make ready 1 lb chicken sausage
  17. Take 8 oz grape tomatoes
Instructions to make Chicken Sausage Zucchini Alfredo:
  1. Prep the vegetables, parmesan, and sausage. Let the cream cheese and butter soften.
  2. Boil four quarts of water in a large pot, then add the egg noodles, 1/2tsp salt, and boil for 6 minutes until al dente.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the garlic and onion in olive oil on low heat until yellowed.
  4. Add the zucchini and squash. Season with salt, pepper, and basil. Sauté about 10 minutes: until most of the moisture is gone from the pan. Make sure to drain the pasta and set aside in the pot.
  5. Create room in the pan to melt the butter and slowly mix in the milk and cream cheese until melted. Dissolve the parmesan cheese into the sauce and season with parsley.
  6. Add the sausage and let the sauce thicken. When the sauce is the consistency of yogurt, mix it into the egg noodles.
  7. Serve with grape tomatoes, extra parmesan, and parsley as a garnish!
