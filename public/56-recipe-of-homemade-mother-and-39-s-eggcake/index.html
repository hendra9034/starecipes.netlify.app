The ingredients needed to make Mother's Eggcake:
  1. Take 6 chicken eggs (big brown eggs)
  2. Get 5 tsp sugar
  3. Take Condensed milk
  4. Prepare Black coffee
  5. Take Water
Instructions to make Mother's Eggcake:
  1. Break 6 eggs, whip and mix them thoroughly. Use whole eggs
  2. Condensed milk + sugar = 1/2 eggs volume, then add coffee and water until full volume
  3. Mix all well
  4. Pour eggs into the mixture, mix well
  5. Low heat, steam for 12 minutes
