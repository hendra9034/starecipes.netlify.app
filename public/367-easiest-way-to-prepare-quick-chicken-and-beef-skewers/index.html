The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Beef Skewers:
  1. Prepare Chicken Marinade
  2. Take 4-5 chicken breasts, cut into bite size cubes
  3. Take 2 tsp garlic, crushes
  4. Make ready 1 tsp ground garlic
  5. Get 1 chicken stock cube
  6. Take 1 Tbsp mixed herbs
  7. Get 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tsp all spice
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup natural or greek yoghurt
  10. Take Juice of 1 lemon
  11. Prepare Beef Marinade
  12. Prepare 500 g beef, cubed
  13. Take 3 tsp garlic, crushed
  14. Prepare 1 Tbsp all purpose seasoning
  15. Take 1 Tbsp dried basil
  16. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  17. Prepare 1 Tbsp paprika
  18. Take 1 beef stock cube
  19. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  20. Take 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  21. Prepare 1 Tbsp olive oil
  22. Make ready What you will need
  23. Prepare 1 cup melted butter
  24. Get 2 bell peppers, red & green, cut into large chunks
  25. Prepare 14 wooden skewers
  26. Get 2 bowls
Instructions to make Chicken and Beef Skewers:
  1. Chicken marinade - in a bowl, mix all chicken marinade ingredients. Add the chicken to the marinade and mix well coating all chicken pieces. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  3. Thread the chicken and peppers onto skewers. Do the same for the beef.
  4. Grill for 8-10 minutes, turning them frequently and baste with the melted butter frequently.
  5. Serve with a salad and sauce to dunk in.
  6. Enjoy…
