The ingredients needed to make Chicken minch porridge:
  1. Get Chicken meat
  2. Take Rice 1 cup for three people
  3. Prepare Water
  4. Make ready Sesame oil
  5. Take Egg
  6. Prepare Soy sauce
  7. Get Pepper powder
  8. Make ready Corn flour
  9. Prepare Ginger
Instructions to make Chicken minch porridge:
  1. Wash rice and put into rice cooker
  2. Pour in water up till 80% full rice cooker - After that when you see bubble, slightlly take up the cover to prevent rice water over flow, continue let it cook with rice cooker
  3. Cut the chicken meat into slices and chop until minch or you can use grinder to grind the chicken meat into minch meat
  4. Soak chicken meat with soy sauce, corn flour and pepper powder after that try to mix them together using spoon to ensure equally distrbute the taste
  5. Use spoon to make as a fish ball type of shape and throw into rice cooker
  6. Chop the ginger into slices and put into rice cooker
  7. After rice cooked into porridge or congee, put soy sauce and sesame oil
  8. Finally you can optional crack two eggs into porridge to make it taste even bettet must kacau it
