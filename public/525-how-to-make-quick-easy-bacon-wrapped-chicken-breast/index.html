The ingredients needed to make Easy Bacon wrapped chicken breast:
  1. Get 4 chicken breast (3-4oz each)
  2. Get 8 strips Bacon (I used applewood)
  3. Get 1 tbsp Olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ginger powder
  8. Get 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (I used honey mustard)
  9. Take 1 tsp parsley for garnish
Steps to make Easy Bacon wrapped chicken breast:
  1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Line a roasting pan with foil and place a olive oiled roasting rack into the pan.
  2. In a small bowl, mix salt, pepper, garlic and ginger powder.
  3. Sprinkle both sides of the chicken breasts with seasoning and wrap each chicken breast with 2 strips of bacon.
  4. Divide BBQ sauce. Place wrapped chicken breasts on the rack and brush with BBQ sauce.
  5. Bake the chicken breasts for 20-25 minutes or until they reach an internal temperature of 160-165F and the bacon is browned.
  6. Take out chicken breasts from the oven and loosely cover with foil for 10 minutes. Then, brush on some of the remaining sauce and sprinkle with parsley. Serve.
