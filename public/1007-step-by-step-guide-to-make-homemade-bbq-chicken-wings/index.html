Instructions to make BBQ chicken wings:
  1. Put chicken wings in big bowl
  2. Cut onion up finely
  3. Add sauce and onion to chicken wings
  4. Then add parsley, half a lemon juice and gate lemon peel
  5. Mix together with hands then wrap and put in fridge for 4 hours
  6. Get fraying pan and put a tsp of butter and let melt
  7. Add chicken and cook until golden, let rest for 5-10minutes and enjoy!
