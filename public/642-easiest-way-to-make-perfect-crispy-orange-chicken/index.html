The ingredients needed to make CRISPY Orange Chicken:
  1. Prepare Crispy Chicken
  2. Get 3 chicken breasts medium diced
  3. Make ready 1.5 cup AP Flour
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup corn starch
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  7. Get 2 egg
  8. Prepare Orange Sauce
  9. Make ready 3/4 cup Fresh orange juice
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp orange zest
  11. Take 2 garlic cloves minced
  12. Get 1/8 cup rice vinegar
  13. Get 1/3 cup water
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  15. Prepare 2 Tbs soy sauce
  16. Take 2 tsp ginger minced
  17. Get 11/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  18. Make ready 1 tbsp cornstarch plus 1 Tbs water mixed together
Steps to make CRISPY Orange Chicken:
  1. For the sauce
  2. Add tsp of olive oil to a sauce pan and sauté garlic about a minute then add orange juice, zest,vinegar,soy sauce, brown sugar - Cook about 3 minutes
  3. Add cornstarch mixture to sauce. Cook low about 2 min then remove from heat. Now start the chicken.
  4. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. - Combine flour and corn starch with more salt and pepper - Whisk eggs add 2 tbsp of water
  5. Preheat about 2 cups of avocado oil med high
  6. Dip chicken in flour spice mix - Then dip in egg mixture - Return back to flour mix
  7. Add to hot oil and fry until crunchy golden
  8. Remove from oil on to a paper towel then Sprinkle with salt
  9. In a bowl Toss some of the sauce in with the crunchy chicken until just covered with sauce add more for extra saucy
