The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Take 1 kilo chicken
  2. Take 1 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 tsp pepper
  4. Get 2 cups flour
  5. Take 1 tbsp parsley
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp powder garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  8. Get 2 lemon juice
  9. Make ready * 2 cups yougart
  10. Take 1/2 cup fresh milk
  11. Get 2 eggs, beaten
  12. Take salt, pepper
  13. Take cooking oil

Steps to make Fried Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken according to desire size, drained.
  2. In a bowl mix yougart, milk, salt, pepper and eggs.
  3. Rub chicken with salt and lemon.. don't discard lemon
  4. Put yougart mixture on the chicken and let it marinate for half hour or more.
  5. In a food container with lid, mix flour and other ingredients..
  6. Put chicken inside container and cover.. give a good shake to coat chicken with flour mixture.
  7. Prepare to fry on medium heat.
