The ingredients needed to make Fried chicken breast:
  1. Get 1/4 kg chicken breast (diced into cubes)
  2. Prepare 2 cubes chicken cube
  3. Make ready 2 cups vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 2 red bell papers
  5. Prepare 2 green bell papers
  6. Take 1/2 tbsp curry powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 cucumber
