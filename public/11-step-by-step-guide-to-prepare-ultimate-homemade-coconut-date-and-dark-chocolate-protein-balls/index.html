The ingredients needed to make Homemade Coconut, Date and Dark Chocolate Protein Balls:
  1. Take Dates
  2. Take Chocolate Protein Powder (Vegan if Preferred)
  3. Take Desiccated Coconut (more for rolling)
  4. Take Ground Oats
  5. Take Dark Cocoa Powder
Instructions to make Homemade Coconut, Date and Dark Chocolate Protein Balls:
  1. Combine all ingredients together to form a paste.
  2. Roll into balls
  3. Roll in Desiccated coconut flakes
  4. Place in container and keep refrigerated
