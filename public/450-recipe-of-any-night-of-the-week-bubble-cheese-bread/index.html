Instructions to make Bubble cheese bread:
  1. Pre heat oven 350°
  2. Mix all the cheeses together..and really you can use any mix of cheese u want..Add long as it had the blue cheese..that one's important.
  3. Add ranch dressing to cheeses and mix well
  4. Add oregano and garlic stir well
  5. Slice bread into thin 1/2in. Slices
  6. Spread cheese mix on sliced bread about 1/4in thick on top of bread
  7. Pop in oven for about 10 minutes until ewwwy gooey and slightly golden
  8. Finish in broiler for a minute to Brown up top
