The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken wings.:
  1. Get 1 kg Chicken wings (washed)
  2. Prepare to taste Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Mustard powder
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp soya sauce
  5. Take 2 tbsp chilli sauce
  6. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Take 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp lemon juice
  10. Take for coating*
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  12. Prepare 1/2 tbsp peprika powder
  13. Prepare Pinch salt
  14. Make ready 1/3 tsp black pepper
  15. Make ready Chilled water
Instructions to make Fried Chicken wings.:
  1. In a large bowl add wings,salt, mustard powder,soya sauce, chilli powder, chilli flakes, chilli sauce, ginger garlic paste and lemon juice mix well and leave for 2 -3 hours.
  2. for coating**. In a bowl add flour,salt, black pepper, peprika powder and chilled water and mix well.
  3. Now coat all wings one by one in a mixture…
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry chicken wings and fry until cook completely.
  5. Serve hot with fries, sauce or ketchup ☺️
