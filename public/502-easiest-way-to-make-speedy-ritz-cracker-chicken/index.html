The ingredients needed to make Ritz Cracker Chicken:
  1. Prepare 4 Chicken breasts
  2. Take 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  3. Get 2 tbsp Parsley Flakes
  4. Make ready 12 Ritz Crackers
  5. Prepare 1 cup Greek Yogurt
Instructions to make Ritz Cracker Chicken:
  1. Turn oven on to 350
  2. Crush Ritz Crackers
  3. Mix crackers, flakes, cheese in bowl
  4. Dip chicken in Greek yogurt
  5. Dredge chicken through mixture
  6. Put in pan and cook 20-25 mins
