The ingredients needed to make Pineapple chicken & bbq rice:
  1. Get 10-12 boneless skinless chicken thighs (or any other cut)
  2. Prepare 1 bag frozen (or fresh) peas or green beans
  3. Get 2 cups uncooked rice
  4. Prepare 1 cup Pineapple chunks
  5. Get 3 1/2 cups water
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup bbq sauce
  7. Make ready 2 tea spoons hot sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tea spoon mustard
  9. Take 2 tea spoons Worcestershire sauce
  10. Prepare cummin
  11. Make ready minced onion
  12. Make ready Steak seasoning
  13. Make ready salt & pepper
  14. Prepare garlic powder
  15. Make ready Brown sugar

Steps to make Pineapple chicken & bbq rice:
  1. Put rice in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Sprinkle with salt, cummin, minced onion, garlic powder & pepper
  2. Pour bag of frozen peas on top of rice
  3. In a bowl, wisk together mustard, hot sauce, water, bbq sauce & Worcestershire sauce until evenly blended
  4. Pour liquid mixture into baking dish
  5. Coat chicken in Brown sugar and place on top of rice.. Season with steak seasonings and salt and pepper
  6. Put pineapple chunks on top.of chicken
  7. Bake at 375° in a preheated oven for 1 hour or until rice and chicken are cooked threw.
