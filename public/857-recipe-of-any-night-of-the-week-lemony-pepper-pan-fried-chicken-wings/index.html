The ingredients needed to make Lemony Pepper Pan-Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready 10 chicken wings, cut into two, wing tips removed
  2. Get 1 tsp lemon pepper seasoning (if you don't have any, 2 tsp mixture or grated lemon zest and crushed black pepper would do)
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 tbsp flour + 1 tbsp katakuriko (or maizena)
  5. Get 1/2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
  6. Take 1 pinch salt
  7. Make ready 2-3 tbsp oil for frying

Instructions to make Lemony Pepper Pan-Fried Chicken Wings:
  1. Mix wingettes and drumettes with 1 tsp lemon pepper, 1/2 tsp salt. Store in an airtight container in a fridge overnight.
  2. Remove chicken pieces from the fridge. Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine flour + katakuriko with lemon pepper and salt. Put chicken pieces inside and coat them well.
  4. Pan-fry until golden brown.
