  2. Prepare 100 ml water
  3. Make ready 300 gr gluten free flour
  4. Get 50 gr rice flour
  5. Make ready 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  6. Get 1 tsp onion granules
  7. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp blackpepper
