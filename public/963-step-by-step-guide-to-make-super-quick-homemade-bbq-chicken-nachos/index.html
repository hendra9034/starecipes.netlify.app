The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken Nachos:
  1. Make ready 2 bottles bbq sauce
  2. Take 4 chicken breast
  3. Take Tortilla chips
  4. Prepare 1 jar double cheddar sauce
Steps to make BBQ Chicken Nachos:
  1. In a crockpot on low add chicken and bbq sauce and cook on low for 24 hrs
  2. Shred up the chicken
  3. On Your plate layer chips chicken and cheese sauce
  4. Have a fork and napkins handy as this is messy but soooo yummy
