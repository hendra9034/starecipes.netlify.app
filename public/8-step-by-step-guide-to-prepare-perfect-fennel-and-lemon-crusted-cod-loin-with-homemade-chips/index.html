The ingredients needed to make Fennel and Lemon Crusted Cod Loin with Homemade Chips:
  1. Get For the Fish:
  2. Get Cod Loin
  3. Take Eggs
  4. Get Panko Breadcrumbs
  5. Take Fennel Seeds
  6. Prepare Lemon Zest
  7. Take Plain Flour
  8. Take Salt
  9. Get Pepper
  10. Make ready For the Homemade Chips:
  11. Prepare Medium sized Desireé Potatoes or any Red Potatoes
  12. Prepare Olive oil
  13. Get Salt
  14. Prepare Pepper
Steps to make Fennel and Lemon Crusted Cod Loin with Homemade Chips:
    1. Pre heat your oven to 190 degrees Celsius
    1. To make the chips, slice each of your potatoes into 6-8 wedges (depending on size) and arrange in a baking tray with olive oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Bake in the oven until crispy and golden brown, occasionally turning the potatoes.
  4. For the cod, place seasoned flour and whisked eggs in separate bowls.
  5. Toast your fennel seeds in a small saucepan until you begin to smell the aroma and see the seeds browning. Allow to cool and grind to a fine powder.
  6. Combine the fennel with grated lemon zest and the breadcrumbs to make your crumb mix.
  7. Pat your cod dry with tissue and coat in the flour, eggs and then the crumb mix.
  8. Repeat one more time to allow a double crust which will allow a crisper finish.
  9. Place on lined baking tray and bake until the crust is crispy and the cod is cooked to 50 degrees Celsius.
  10. Once the chips and cod is ready, serve alongside your favourite salad and accompaniments.
