The ingredients needed to make Chicken Sausage Roll Bread:
  1. Get 7 chicken sausages
  2. Prepare bread:
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Get 3 tbsp melted butter
  5. Take 1 cup.flour
  6. Get 1 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  8. Get 1 dash salt
  9. Get Half bowl fresh milk
  10. Prepare 1 tsp yeast diluted in water
  11. Take 1 egg for egg wash
Steps to make Chicken Sausage Roll Bread:
  1. Combine dry ingredients first like……yeast sugar flour salt then.mix well.Nrxt the wet ingredients…egg butter vanilla yeast milk.Mix it well.Then knead it for five mins and form a dough.Put in a bowl.cover with cling wrap and set aside for an.hour.To make thebdough double in size.
  2. After an hour,vut and separate dough on seven.pieces.Roll it and form a string like dough.Take your sausage and wrap around it.
  3. When.finish brush with egg wash and bake in the oven for 18 minscat 200c.After removing from.the oven brush with melted butter
