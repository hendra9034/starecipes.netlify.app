The ingredients needed to make Orange Bread:
  1. Take eggs
  2. Get white sugar
  3. Prepare oranges (approx 2 large oranges) + juice from 1/2 an orange
  4. Take softened butter
  5. Make ready plain flour
  6. Get baking powder
Instructions to make Orange Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 190c.
  2. Mix the egg and sugar together until its creamy and almost frothy.
  3. Juice 2 large oranges which should give you about 120ml though I just poured it into the mixture without measuring it). Add the orange juice and butter then using an electric mixer mix it together.
  4. Add the baking powder and flour to the butter mixture and using the electric blender again mix it all together until its combined well.
  5. Pour the batter into a loaf tin lined with greaseproof.
  6. Transfer to the oven and bake for approximately 60 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. When it's ready pour the juice of about 1/2 a large orange on top of the bread. Let it sit in the loaf tin for 30 minutes and then transfer to a wire rack or plate.
