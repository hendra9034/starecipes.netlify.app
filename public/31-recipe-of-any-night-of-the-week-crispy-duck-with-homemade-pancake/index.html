The ingredients needed to make Crispy duck with homemade pancake:
  1. Make ready 2 duck thighs
  2. Prepare Hoisin sauce (see recipe)
  3. Prepare Pancake (see recipe)
  4. Make ready 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  5. Get 2-3 spring onions, julienned
Instructions to make Crispy duck with homemade pancake:
  1. Score the duck’s skin Lightly and marinate your duck with hoisin sauce. Leave it for 20 min or overnight. - (see recipe)
  2. Roast in the oven for 25-30 min or until your duck cooked. Turn over half way through. 1. While waiting for duck to cook. Making pancakes follow this recipe or you can use ready make one. Stake them up and cover with kitchen foil. - (see recipe) 1. Once your duck cooked and rested for 15 min. Use fork and knife shredded the duck.
  3. Serve your duck on top of pancake, add some hoisin sauce, cucumber and spring onions.
