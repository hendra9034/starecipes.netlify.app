The ingredients needed to make Nasi Goreng:
  1. Get sweet soy sauce
  2. Prepare 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  3. Prepare 4 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  4. Take nasi goreng
  5. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Cooking Oil
  6. Get 2 Tablespoons Chopped Onion
  7. Take 2 Cloves Garlic
  8. Prepare 2-3 Pieces Red Chili
  9. Get 100 Grams Boneless Chicken
  10. Get 1 Tablespoon Sweet Soy Sauce
  11. Take 1 Egg
  12. Make ready 1 1/2 Teaspoons Belacan Paste (Shrimp Paste)
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 Cup Cooked Rice
  14. Prepare 1/4 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  15. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Soy Sauce
  16. Make ready 1/2 Ground Black Pepper
  17. Take garnish
  18. Get 1 Sunny Side Up Egg
  19. Take Chopped spring onion
Instructions to make Nasi Goreng:
  1. Simmer soy sauce with brown sugar on low heat until thickens
  2. Set aside the sweet soy sauce for later use.
  3. In Wook, heat oil add chopped onion together with garlic and red chili. Stir for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Add the bite size boneless chicken and sweet soy sauce that we made earlier.
  5. Add whisked egg.
  6. Add Belacan Paste (shrimp paste) and cooked rice. Mix well.
  7. Add Soy Sauce, ground black pepper and turmeric powder. Salt to taste.
  8. Mix well and garnish with chopped spring onion and sunny side up egg before serving
