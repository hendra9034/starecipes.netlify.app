Steps to make Bread pizza:
  1. For filling
  2. Boiled the potatoes.
  3. Then peel of the potatoes skin and mash them.
  4. Heat butter in a deep pot
  5. Add onions cook till they are pinkish.
  6. Add the corn soup and cook for 2 minutes.

  8. Cook them for 5 minutes. Let it cool.
  9. Then take a bread, put the mixture on the bread and on top add grated cheese.
  10. Per heat the oven 10 minutes before placing the bread pizza
