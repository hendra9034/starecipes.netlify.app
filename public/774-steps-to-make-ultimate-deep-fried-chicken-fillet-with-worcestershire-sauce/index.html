The ingredients needed to make Deep Fried Chicken Fillet with Worcestershire Sauce:
  1. Take 500 grams chicken thigh fillets cut diced
  2. Prepare 1 egg beaten
  3. Make ready 350 grams flour
  4. Take 20 grams corn flour
  5. Take 1 teaspoon chicken stocks powder
  6. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  7. Take 1 tomato cut diced
  8. Get 60 ml Water
  9. Prepare Half Onion 🧅 thick sliced
  10. Make ready Sauce Ingredients
  11. Get 1 tablespoon sugar
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon Indonesian Sweet Soy Sauce “ABC”
  13. Make ready 1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  14. Get 1 tablespoon Hoisin Sauce
  15. Make ready 1 tablespoon Oysters Sauce
  16. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  17. Take 50 grams butter
  18. Take Cooking oil for stir fry
  19. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sesame Oil
Steps to make Deep Fried Chicken Fillet with Worcestershire Sauce:
  1. Mixed all ingredients such as flours,corn flour,egg, water and combined together with chicken thigh fillets
  2. Deep Fried mixed Chicken fillet until golden brown. Let it cool off and separate it with strainer
  3. Stir fry tomatoes,onions,with butter and cooking oil
  4. Pour the remaining ingredients for sauce such as sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce,worcestershire sauce,etc into the stir fried onions,mix it all together until it combined well and a bit thicker as sauce. Put the deep fried chicken inside the sauce mixture,combined well. Ready to served
