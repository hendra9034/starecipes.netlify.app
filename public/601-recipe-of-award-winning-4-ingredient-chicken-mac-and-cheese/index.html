The ingredients needed to make 4 Ingredient Chicken Mac and Cheese:
  1. Take 1-2 Boxes Mac and Cheese
  2. Take 1/2 Cup Milk
  3. Prepare 1-2 Tbsps. Butter
  4. Make ready 1 (5 oz.) Can Chicken, drained and broken up a little bit
Instructions to make 4 Ingredient Chicken Mac and Cheese:
  1. Fill a medium pot half way with water. Bring to a boil. Add 2 Tablespoons of Salt and pasta from boxes (reserving the cheese packets). Turn heat down to medium low and let simmer stirring occasionally til pasta is soft. Drain the water. Add the butter and stir til completely melted. Add the cheese packets, milk, and chicken.
