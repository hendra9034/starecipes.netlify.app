The ingredients needed to make Sour Cream Spinach Ranch Chicken Thighs:
  1. Get 1 large pack chicken thighs (skin on / bone in)
  2. Make ready Sprinkle black pepper
  3. Get Sprinkle garlic powder
  4. Take Sprinkle Italian seasoning
  5. Prepare 2 packages (16 ounce) frozen cut leaf spinach, thawed
  6. Get 4 tablespoons dry ranch dressing
  7. Get 1 cup sour cream
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  9. Make ready Shredded cheddar cheese
