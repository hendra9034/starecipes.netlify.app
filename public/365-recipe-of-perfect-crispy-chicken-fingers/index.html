The ingredients needed to make Crispy chicken fingers:
  1. Make ready 250 gms boneless chicken strips
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp salt
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp dhaniya powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  7. Get 1 egg
  8. Prepare 1 bowl bread crumbs
  9. Prepare as needed Oil for frying
  10. Take 1 tbsp cornflour powder
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions to make Crispy chicken fingers:
  1. In a mixing bowl, take chicken strips add salt, red chilli powder, dhaniya powder, gingergarlic paste, cornflour powder, soya sauce, garam masala powder and mix well and marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Dip marinated chicken strips in egg and then in bread crumbs and refrigerate for 1 hour
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry chicken strips till golden brown and crispy. - Serve it with your favourite dip.
