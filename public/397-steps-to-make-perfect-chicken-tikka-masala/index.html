The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tikka Masala:
  1. Prepare Marinade:-
  2. Get Large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  3. Take plain yogurt (I use a thick Greek style)
  4. Take lemon juice
  5. Prepare garlic, minced
  6. Prepare minced ginger
  7. Get salt
  8. Prepare ground cumin
  9. Prepare garam masala
  10. Prepare paprika
  11. Make ready Option can Use a Tikka Spice mix instead of the 3 items above
  12. Make ready Sauce (masala):-
  13. Take oil
  14. Take large onion, finely chopped
  15. Get minced ginger
  16. Make ready garlic, minced
  17. Take ground cumin
  18. Prepare ground turmeric
  19. Take ground coriander
  20. Get paprika
  21. Prepare chili powder
  22. Get garam masala
  23. Take tomato puree
  24. Get Large Tin Chopped tomatoes
  25. Prepare ml
  26. Take water
  27. Make ready Double cream
  28. Prepare fresh coriander Leaves to serve
Steps to make Chicken Tikka Masala:
  1. Slice the chicken into cubes. Then add the chicken with the yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, salt, cumin, garam masala, and paprika and stir until well-coated. - Cover with Cling Film and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to 260°C. Line a high-sided baking pan or roasting tray with parchment paper. - Place the marinated chicken pieces on a wire rack over a baking tin so that the heat can go around all the chicken without needing to turn. Bake for 15 mins or until the edges start to brown.
  3. At the same time Make the sauce: Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat, then sauté the onions, ginger, and garlic until tender but not browned. Add the cumin, turmeric, coriander, paprika, chili powder, and garam masala and stir constantly for about 30 seconds, until the spices are fragrant. Stir in the tomato puree, tomato sauce, and 1 ¼ cups of water, then bring to a boil and cook for about 5 minutes. I quickly blitz with a stand mixer (optional) Pour in the cream.
  4. Remove The chicken from the Oven and put into sauce. Cook for 2 more mins then serve with some fresh coriander. Eat with my homemade Naan (see other recipe) WOW!!!!!!!
