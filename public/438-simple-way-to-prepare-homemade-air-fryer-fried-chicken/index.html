The ingredients needed to make Air Fryer Fried Chicken:
  1. Get 2 chicken thighs
  2. Get Dry Mix
  3. Get 1 cup flour
  4. Get 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ground red pepper (optional)
  8. Take Wet Mix
  9. Make ready 1 egg
  10. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1 Tbl hot sauce
Steps to make Air Fryer Fried Chicken:
  1. Mix wet mix in a bowl
  2. Mix all dry mix in a bowl
  3. Coat each thigh in dry mix, cost in wet mix, coat again in dry mix.
  4. Place in air fryer.
  5. My thighs were largish, so i cooked for 12 minutes, turned over, cooked 12 more minutes until 180F internal
  6. These are extra crispy, if you like it that way. But the meat was still moist and cooked completely.
