The ingredients needed to make Sweet BBQ Chicken sandwich:
  1. Get 1 Chicken breast
  2. Take 1/3 cup sweet corn
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup sliced canned mushrooms
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded mozarella
  5. Get 2 tbsp BBQ sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  7. Get Siracha if you like spice
