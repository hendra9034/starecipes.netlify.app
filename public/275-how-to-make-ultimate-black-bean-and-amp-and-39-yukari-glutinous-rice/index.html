The ingredients needed to make Black Bean & 'Yukari’ Glutinous Rice:
  1. Make ready Dry Black Beans *soaked in water overnight
  2. Take & 1/2 cups (180ml cup) Glutinous Rice *short grain preferred
  3. Take cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  4. Prepare Water
  5. Make ready ‘Yukari’
Instructions to make Black Bean & 'Yukari’ Glutinous Rice:
  1. Black Beans need to be soaked in water overnight. Drain and place in a saucepan, add Water and bring to the simmer. Cook for 10 minutes OR until just cooked but still firm, then drain. *Note: If you use Black Soy Beans, that would be a great choice, it might take longer time.
  2. Wash Rice and drain. Place Rice and Black Beans in the rice cooker’s inner pot, add 300ml Water, and press Cook/Start button.
  3. When the rice is cooked and steamed, sprinkle ‘Yukari’, toss the rice and gently mix to combine.
