The ingredients needed to make Quick Chinese chicken with broccoli and spicy black bean sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 chicken breast - skinless and chopped into small pieces
  2. Get 6 sprigs broccoli
  3. Make ready 1 small white onion diced
  4. Take 2 teaspoons ginger purée (use 1 teaspoon ginger powder if cannot get purée)
  5. Make ready Salt
  6. Make ready 100 ml water
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp oil (I used canola)

  2. Add the ginger to the frying pan with 1 spoon black bean sauce and then add the thinly sliced chicken. Stir and coat with the juices.
