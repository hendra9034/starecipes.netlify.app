The ingredients needed to make Garlic ranch chicken tenders:
  1. Make ready 2 chicken breasts cut in half to thin them and cut into 1 inch wide strips
  2. Make ready 4-5 eggs
  3. Get 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Take 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  8. Get 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  9. Take 1/4 teaspoon Italian herbs
