The ingredients needed to make Million Dollar Chicken:
  1. Prepare 4 boneless chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 12 oz cream cheese, softened
  3. Get 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  4. Take 10 slices bacon, crumbled
  5. Take 3/4 c green onions, chopped
  6. Make ready 2 c shredded colby jack cheese
Steps to make Million Dollar Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a medium bowl mix cr cheese, mayp. Half of bacon crumbles, half of green onions, and half of the shredded cheese until combined.
  3. Slice chicken breasts in half length wise and place in a baking dish. Spread mixture over the topof the chicken. Top with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake 30-40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through to 165 degrees.
  5. Garnish with remaining bacon and green onions and serve.
