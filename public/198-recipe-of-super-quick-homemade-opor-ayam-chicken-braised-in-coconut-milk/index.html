The ingredients needed to make Opor Ayam/Chicken braised in coconut milk:
  1. Make ready 500-600 gr chicken
  2. Take 5 boiled eggs
  3. Prepare 2 cans coconut milk
  4. Make ready 1 onion
  5. Take 5 cloves garlic
  6. Get 1.5 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Prepare 1.5 tsp coriander powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cumin powder
  9. Make ready 1.5-2 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1 lemon grass
  11. Take 3 bay leaves
  12. Get 1.5 tsp turmeric powder
  13. Take 1/4 tsp sugar
  14. Get 3 tbsp oil
  15. Make ready 2 cm ginger
  16. Get 2 cm galangal
  17. Make ready 3-4 lime leaves
Steps to make Opor Ayam/Chicken braised in coconut milk:
  1. Peel and wash the onion and garlic. Slice the onion and grate the garlic (I am using mortar to make the garlic paste with other ingredients, black pepper, cumin, and coriander seeds)./Kupas dan cuci bawang bombay dan bawang putih. Iris tipis bawang bombay dan haluskan bawang putih (saya menggunakan lesung untuk membuat pasta bawang putih dengan bahan lain, lada hitam, jinten, dan biji ketumbar).
  2. Prepare the saucepan, heat it, adds the oil, saute the onion till golden brown. Add the garlic, black pepper, coriander, cumin, turmeric powder (or the garlic paste), salt, and sugar keep sauteing for 2-3 minutes./Siapkan panci, panaskan, tambahkan minyak, tumis bawang bombay hingga berwarna cokelat keemasan. Tambahkan bawang putih halus, lada hitam, ketumbar, jintan, bubuk kunyit (atau pasta bawang putih), garam, dan gula, tumis terus selama 2-3 menit.
  3. Add the lemongrass, bay leaves, galangal, ginger, and lime leaves. Continue to sautéing for 2-3 minutes. Then add the chicken and 3 cups of water, boiled eggs, close the lid wait till the chicken is cook./Tambahkan serai, daun salam, lengkuas, jahe, dan daun jeruk. Lanjutkan menumis selama 2-3 menit. Kmd tambahkan ayam, 3 gelas air, telur rebus, tutup panci tunggu sampai ayam matang.
  4. Once the chicken is cook, add the coconut milk. Gently stir the yellow soup till the soup is boiled./Setelah ayam matang, tambahkan santan. Aduk perlahan sup kuning sampai sup mendidih. 1. Opor Ayam or chicken braised in coconut milk is ready to serve accompanied with rice and sambal to add the spicy taste./Opor Ayam atau ayam rebus dalam santan siap dihidangkan ditemani nasi dan sambal sebagai penambah selera pedas.
