The ingredients needed to make Chicken BBQ Naan Pizza:
  1. Prepare 2 cups chicken (leftover rotisserie chicken)
  2. Get 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  3. Take 2 Trader Joe’s Tandoori Naan
  4. Take 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Prepare Several pieces of Red onion
Instructions to make Chicken BBQ Naan Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Chop left over rotisserie chicken to small pieces.
  3. Take out frozen naan and put them on a baking sheet.
  4. Evenly brush the BBQ sauce on the naan.
  5. Put chicken pieces on top of the naan.
  6. Put shredded cheese and then red onions on top.
  7. Cook the pizza at 400°F in oven for 10 minutes.
  8. (Optional) Broil the pizza at 525°F for 1 minute.
  9. Hot BBQ chicken pizza ready to serve.
