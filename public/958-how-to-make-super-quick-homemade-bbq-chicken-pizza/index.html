The ingredients needed to make BBQ Chicken Pizza:
  1. Get Desired amount of the following:
  2. Take Prepared pizza crust (or pizza dough and follow instructions on how to make)
  3. Take Chicken strips or breasts or shredded rotisserie chicken
  4. Take Mozzarella cheese
  5. Prepare Parmesan cheese
  6. Prepare BBQ sauce (your favorite brand)
  7. Take Medium-sized red onion
  8. Get Sliced pineapple chunks (optional)
