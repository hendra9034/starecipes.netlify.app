The ingredients needed to make White Christmas Tartlets:
  1. Prepare 250 grams Arnotts Buttersnap Cookies
  2. Prepare 200 grams white chocolate chopped
  3. Take 1/3 cup rice bubbles
  4. Get 1/3 cup shredded coconut
  5. Get 1/3 cup halved mini pink and marshmellows
Instructions to make White Christmas Tartlets:
  1. Preheat oven 180c
  2. Place each biscuit over each hole in a 12 cup cake tray. Bake 2-3 minutes until soft. Use spoon to mould into cup shape.
  3. Place chocolate in microwave heat until smooth
  4. Add rice bubbles, coconut and marshmallows to chocolate. Stir to combine.
  5. Divide mixture between biscuits cases. Refrigerate for 20 minutes or until set.
