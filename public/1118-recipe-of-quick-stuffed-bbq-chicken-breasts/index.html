Steps to make Stuffed bbq chicken breasts:
  1. Clean and slice a pocket in each chicken breast .pound breast even .
  2. Place prosciutto in pockets of chicken breast the slice a cube of cream cheese place on the prosciutto
  3. Close breast and roll from one end .
  4. Add rolled breast seal side down in glass pan. Repeat with rest of chicken bunching them close together. (Touching looks like baked biscuts )
  5. Pour bbq sauce over top of the chicken rolls covering them .crumble remaining cream cheese over top of bbq sauce.
  6. Place in preheated oven 375°F for 45 minutes .basting with sauce every 20 minutes. .
