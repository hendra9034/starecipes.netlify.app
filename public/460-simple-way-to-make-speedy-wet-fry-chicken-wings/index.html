The ingredients needed to make Wet Fry Chicken wings:
  1. Prepare Oil
  2. Take 2 cloves garlic
  3. Prepare Pepper
  4. Prepare Gharam masala
  5. Make ready cubes Royco
  6. Get Chicken wings
Instructions to make Wet Fry Chicken wings:
  1. Crush the garlic and place in a pan with water
  2. Place the chicken wings in the pan and steam it for 30 minutes
  3. Remove the chicken wings and place them aside
  4. In a frying pan,fry onions and tomatoes
  5. Then add gharam masala and royco cubes and two pinches of salt
