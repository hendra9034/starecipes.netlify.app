The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Fried Chicken with Sweet and Spicy Drizzle:
  1. Take 1 lb chicken
  2. Prepare Salt,pepper, cayenne pepper
  3. Make ready Corn starch mixed with flour
  4. Prepare Butter milk mixed with hot sauce
  5. Make ready Honey, garlic (chopped) and habanero pepper
  6. Make ready Frying oil
Steps to make Crunchy Fried Chicken with Sweet and Spicy Drizzle:
  1. Marinate chicken with salt,pepper, cayenne, milk and hot sauce(the longer the better).
  2. Take chicken and coat with flour mixed starch. Fry until golden brown.
  3. Mix honey with chopped garlic and chopped habanero. And drizzle on top of chicken and enjoy!
