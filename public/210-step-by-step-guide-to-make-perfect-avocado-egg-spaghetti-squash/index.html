Steps to make Avocado egg spaghetti squash:
  1. Microwave until you can cut it in half. scoop the seeds out and spread olive oil and sprinkle salt & pepper on the cutting surface. Bake for 35min in 425F.
  2. Cut avocado (and chicken) in pieces. Salsa on the side.
  3. Scrape the flesh and mix with 2. Crack 2 eggs on top. Bake for another 10min.
  4. Add cheese on top, another 5min.
  5. Drizzle ketchup and dried herbs.
