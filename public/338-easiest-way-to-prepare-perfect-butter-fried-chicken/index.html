The ingredients needed to make Butter Fried Chicken:
  1. Prepare 300 ml vegetable oil for frying the chicken
  2. Take 4 pairs chicken thighs and drumsticks, cut into 2 pcs each
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp white pepper powder
  5. Prepare 5 Tbsp butter
  6. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Get 1 onion, sliced
  8. Prepare For the sauce:
  9. Take 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  10. Make ready 5 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
  11. Get 4 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
  12. Take 50 ml water
  13. Take 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  14. Make ready 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
Instructions to make Butter Fried Chicken:
