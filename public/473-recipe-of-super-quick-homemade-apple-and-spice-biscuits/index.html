The ingredients needed to make Apple and spice biscuits:
  1. Get 1 1/4 cup brown sugar
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup margarine
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup apple puree
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup honey
  5. Take 2 egg whites beaten lightly
  6. Prepare 2 1/2 cup s.r. flour
  7. Get 3 cup rice bubbles crushed to 1 cup
  8. Prepare 1 tsp ground ginger
  9. Get 2 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground cloves
Steps to make Apple and spice biscuits:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°F C
  2. Beat sugar and margarine until combined and light in colour
  3. Stir in apple puree, honey and egg whites (mixture will look curdled)
  4. Stir in rice bubbles, sifted flour and spices;mix well until combined
  5. Roll level tablespoons of mix into balls
  6. Place 5cm apart on greased oven tray
  7. Press down lightly with a fork
  8. Bake for about 8 mins. or until lightly browned
  9. Cool on wire racks, store in airtight container.
