The ingredients needed to make Omma Baked Chicken Thigh:
  1. Prepare 500 gms Chicken thigh
  2. Prepare Marinade
  3. Make ready 1.5 tbsp Apple Cider vinegar
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp honey
  5. Take 2 tbsp low sodium Soy Sauce
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp gochujang
  7. Get 1 tbsp ginger grated
  8. Prepare 4 garlic / 2 tbsp dince garlic
  9. Get Glaze
  10. Get 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  12. Take 1 tbsp gochujang
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp honey
Steps to make Omma Baked Chicken Thigh:
  1. Whisk together ingredients for marinade. Add chicken to marinade and turn until all the chicken is completely coated in the sauce. Marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes, up to overnight.
  2. When ready to cook the chicken, preheat oven to 190c. Line a baking sheet with foil for easy cleanup
  3. Arrange chicken on baking sheet, discard excess marinade. Bake chicken for a total of 25 minutes.
  4. While chicken is baking, whisk together ingredients for glaze in a small sauce pan over medium heat. Cook, whisking occasionally, for 5 minutes, or until sauce has thickened. Do not allow sauce to boil.
  5. In the last 5 minutes of cooking chicken, brush with the glaze. After chicken is done, remove from oven and brush with another coat of the glaze before serving.
