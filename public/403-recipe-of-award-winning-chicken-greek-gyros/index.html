The ingredients needed to make Chicken Greek Gyros:
  1. Get chicken breast
  2. Prepare Ken's Greek salad dressing
  3. Prepare pepperoncini rings
  4. Make ready lettuce, shredded or torn
  5. Make ready tomatoes, sliced or diced
  6. Prepare olives, sliced
  7. Get cucumbers, sliced
  8. Make ready tzaziki sauce (or plain Greek yogurt with Greek seasonings)
  9. Take coconut oil
  10. Make ready Hungry Girl mini fold-its
  11. Get Hummus
Steps to make Chicken Greek Gyros:
  1. Dice or slice chicken breasts and stir fry in coconut oil.
  2. Toss in Ken's Greek salad dressing
  3. Assemble gyros with toppings listed, according to preference
  4. Wrap in Hungry Girl (or other preferred low carb wrap)
  5. Enjoy!
