The ingredients needed to make Grilled BBQ Chicken:
  1. Prepare 3 chicken breasts
  2. Take 1/4 cup your choice Bbq sauce
  3. Get 3 Tbsp Brown sugar
  4. Take 2 Tbsp Cavendars
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Crushed red pepper
  6. Take 1 Tbsp Garlic powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Onion powder
  8. Take 1 tsp Chili powder
  9. Prepare Pinch Salt and pepper

Steps to make Grilled BBQ Chicken:
  1. Preheat gas grill to 450-500F
  2. Mix all ingredients in bowl
  3. Poke holes in chicken breasts and massage mixture into breasts, leaving a small amount for basting later.
  4. Grill for 25/30 minutes turning every 5 minutes. Baste in the last 5 minutes. Use thermometer and remove chicken when it reaches 165
  5. Partner with a veggie side!
