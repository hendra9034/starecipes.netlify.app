Steps to make Chinese chicken and rice:
  1. Mix the chicken marinade recipes together first and leave to settle while making the rest of the recipe
  2. Lightly saute the vegetables in oil for a few minutes
  3. Mix the ketchup sauce ingredients and cook boil until a bit thick in texture
  4. Add the vegetables to the sauce and cook for a little bit
  5. Deep fry the chicken in oil until cooked for a few minutes
  6. Add the chicken to the sauce and cook for just a short time till its covered with sauce but the crunchiness shouldn't disappear
  7. Serve with rice
