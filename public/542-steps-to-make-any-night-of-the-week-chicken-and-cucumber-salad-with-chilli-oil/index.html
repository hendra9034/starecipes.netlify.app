The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Cucumber Salad with Chilli Oil:
  1. Get For the chili-infused oil:
  2. Take 3 tbsp. of Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain
  3. Make ready 1 fresh chili pepper
  4. Prepare Seasoning for cooking the chicken:
  5. Prepare 2 skinned chicken thighs
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp. of Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp. of water
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp. of sake
  9. Prepare 1 tsp. of salt
  10. Get For the marinade:
  11. Make ready 7 oz. of cucumber (1 – 2 cucumbers)
  12. Make ready 3 tbsp. of Japonese soy sauce
  13. Prepare 2 tbsp. of sugar
  14. Take 1 tbsp. of mirin
  15. Get 1 tbsp. of chili-infused Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  16. Prepare 1 tsp. of sake
Steps to make Chicken and Cucumber Salad with Chilli Oil:
  1. For the chilli oil: Heat the Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain over a medium heat.
  2. Add the finely sliced chili peppers and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Strain the oil and set it aside.
  4. Try it, and if it is too hot for your taste add Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain to tone it down and give it the taste of the raw oil.
  5. For the chicken and cucumber salad: Half peel the cucumber. With a peeler remove the skin in a strip lengthwise, leaving the same amount unpeeled before the next strip, so that it is striped: 1 inch peeled, another inch unpeeled, and so on.
  6. Cut off the ends of the cucumber and cut it lengthwise into 4 parts then cut those into pieces about two inches long. Set aside.
  7. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Add the Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain to the pan and when hot add the chicken and sauté until golden brown.
  9. Add the rest of the seasoning ingredients (sake, salt, water and Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain). Cover the pan and simmer until the chicken is cooked (about 3-4 minutes).
  10. Mix the ingredients for the marinade in a large bowl.
  11. Remove the chicken and add it to the bowl with the marinade along with the cucumber.
  12. Let it marinate for at least an hour.
  13. Serve cold or at room temperature.
