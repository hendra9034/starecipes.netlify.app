The ingredients needed to make Bacon Paste BBQ Chicken Skewers:
  1. Get BBQ Sauce
  2. Take 1/2 onion, diced
  3. Make ready 1 cup ketchup
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup molasses
  5. Make ready 4 Tbsp worchestershire sauce
  6. Take 4 Tbsp cider vinegar
  7. Make ready 4 Tbsp mustard
  8. Take 1/2 tsp liquid smoke
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  11. Prepare 1 chipotle in adobo, chopped
  12. Get 1/4 cup frozen raspberries
  13. Make ready Chicken
  14. Prepare 2 lbs boneless skinless thighs (8)
  15. Take 2 tsp kosher salt
  16. Take 4 Tbsp favorite rub
  17. Get 2 Tbsp sugar
  18. Make ready 4-8 slices bacon
Instructions to make Bacon Paste BBQ Chicken Skewers:
  1. Add all the BBQ sauce ingredients to a small sauce pot and bring to a simmer. Blend with a stick blended until smooth and then retain on simmer while you prepare everything else.
  2. Add the bacon to a food processor and processor until its a smooth paste.
  3. Cut the chicken into bite size chunks and then season with the salt and toss. Add the bacon paste on top and let sit while you start the grill.
  4. Heat a grill to 450F over direct heat.
  5. Add the seasoning and sugar to the chicken and with gloves hands mix the chicken until coated.
  6. Skewer the chicken in a tight bunch.
  7. Grill over direct heat 3-4 minutes per side until the edges begin to crisp. Baste with copious sauce and turn once more until the sauce caramelizes. Remove to a platter, and rest 10 minutes. unskewer and serve as bite size chunks.
