The ingredients needed to make Bubble and squeak:
  1. Take 1 cup mashed potatoes
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup leftover boiled veggies
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped kale
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup grated broccoli
  5. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Get 2 tsp all purpose flour
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp melted butter
  9. Prepare as needed Oil for cooking

Instructions to make Bubble and squeak:
  1. First in a big bowl,take mashed potatoes,now add leftover boiled veggies,chopped kale,melted butter,salt and black pepper powder. Mix all ingredients well.
  2. Now on plan apply some flour and take small portion of potato mix and make small round shape cake. Repeat this process with remaining mix.
  3. Now in a pan take 2 tsp oil, put these potato cakes on it and cook from both sides while making these cake bubble sounds is coming out.
  4. Cook these form both sides untuk they becomes golden brown in colour. Bible and squeak is ready. Put grated broccoli as topping.
