The ingredients needed to make Stovetop BBQ Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 1 tsp chili powder
  4. Take 1/4 tsp onion powder
  5. Get 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  6. Get 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  8. Take dash cayenne pepper (optional)
  9. Get 1/2 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions to make Stovetop BBQ Chicken:
  1. Mix olive oil, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and cayenne pepper in a medium bowl. Add raw chicken and coat evenly.
  2. Heat medium skillet (preferably iron) over medium-high heat and coat with cooking spray. Place chicken breasts in skillet, cover, and cook 5 minutes.
  3. Turn chicken, replace cover, and cook an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Remove cover and check chicken for doneness with probe thermometer or testing if juices run clear. When cooked completely, pour half of BBQ sauce over chicken. Turn chicken over, and pour remaining sauce over the other side. Cook 2-3 minutes more while using a spoon or basting brush to coat chicken with sauce.
