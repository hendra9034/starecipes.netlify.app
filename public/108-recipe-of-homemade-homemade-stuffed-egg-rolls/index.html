Instructions to make Homemade stuffed Egg Rolls:
  1. Roughly chop all veggies and combine them with the shredded cheeses In a bowl,add a couple tblsps of mayo and drizzle with olive oil and stir,season with salt and Italian seasoning and squeeze lemon juice on the mixture…set aside
  2. Chop chicken breasts into small pieces and season with any seasoning you prefer, but i used salt,Italian seasoning,lawrys red seasoning/seasoning salt and lemon pepper seasoning,cook in a skillet with olive oil til fully cooked,then drain.
  3. Take a egg roll wrap and place on a plate,or chopping board,and take the veggie stuffing and place on the wrap,add a generous amount of chicken and roll it up…repeat til ingredients are gone
  4. Take a skillet and add veggie oil and deep fry your egg rolls
