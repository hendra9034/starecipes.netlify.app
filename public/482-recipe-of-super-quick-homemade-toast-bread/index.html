Steps to make Toast bread:
  1. Mix water + milk and heat them to 37 degrees C
  2. Add the yeast to the mixture (maybe a splash of honey if you're feeling kinky) and let that mix rest for 8 to 10 minutes
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ones and start mixing until smooth
  5. Slowly add the butter to the mixture. (1 tbsp at a time!! Don't rush it otherwise it will create lumps…)
  6. Once the dough is smooth, shape it into a ball and let it rest covered for 1h30
  7. After resting, punch that dough (degas it) and put it on a workbench. - Flatten the dough while making sure it will fit in the pan, and roll it to get a nice cylinder.
  8. Oil the pan and put your dough inside, and let it rest for 1 hour
  9. Preheat your oven to 180 deg and bake that bad boy for 35 minutes or until golden brown
  10. As always, let it rest for at least 30 minutes before cutting it open.
  11. Voilà
