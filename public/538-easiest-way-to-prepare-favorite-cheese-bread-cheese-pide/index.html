The ingredients needed to make Cheese Bread: Cheese Pide:
  1. Prepare 2 & 1/2 cups white flour
  2. Prepare 1 & 1/2 tsp dried yeast
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 3 Tbsp olive oil + 1 tsp for brushing
  6. Get Warm water as required
  7. Prepare 1 egg yolk for glazing
  8. Make ready 100 g Philadelphia cheese / Cream Cheese
  9. Get as needed Mozzarella & Cheddar Cheese
  10. Prepare Red chilli flakes for sprinkle
Steps to make Cheese Bread: Cheese Pide:
  1. Take a big mixing bowl add 1/2 cup lukewarm water add sugar & yeast whisk well then add oil whisk again. Add flour & salt mix well then Knead well and make a nice soft dough. - - Cover the bowl with a damp cloth or cling film and keep in a warm place for about an hour until the dough has doubled in size.
  2. Now remove the dough to a floured surface dust some dry flour and Knead it again well & divide the dough 4 equal portions and make a ball shaped cover and set aside about 15-20 mins. - - Take a one ball and roll out the dough long Oval shape brush with olive oil and then roll the edges only left and right sides (showing in pictures) - press down well. - Press & twist the both end sides well (showing in pictures).
  3. Transfer all pide in Baking tray with parchment paper cover and rest about 15-20 mins. - - First spread Philadelphia or Cream cheese with help of spoon then spread grated Mozzarella & Cheddar cheese. - Brush with egg wash sprinkle some chilli flakes. - - Bake pre-heated oven to 200¬∞C / 450¬∞F in middle rack 10-15 mins or till nice golden colour or reached your desire colour. - - Serve Hot ‚ô®Ô∏è with soup or your favourite sauces. - - Enjoy ! üçΩ
