The ingredients needed to make Chicken Parmesan With Spaghetti and Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 6 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare Salt and pepper (to taste)
  3. Take 1 cup flour
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Get 1/4 cup milk
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  7. Make ready Olive oil or corn oil to fry
  8. Take 2 cups spagetti sauce
  9. Make ready Parmesan cheese (to taste)
  10. Prepare 1 pound linguine
  11. Take 8 slices mozzarella cheese
  12. Make ready Garlic bread (see recipe)
Steps to make Chicken Parmesan With Spaghetti and Garlic Bread:
  1. Add flour, salt, and pepper in a bag….
  2. Shake it up!!!
  3. Put the Panko breadcrumbs in a bag….
  4. Crack 2 eggs….
  5. Add some milk to the eggs….
  6. Mix the eggs and milk together…. pour it into a dish….
