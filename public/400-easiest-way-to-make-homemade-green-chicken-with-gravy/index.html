The ingredients needed to make Green chicken with gravy:
  1. Take fresh chicken
  2. Make ready medium size onion
  3. Get thick Greek yoghurt
  4. Make ready garlic pods
  5. Make ready kasuri methi
  6. Take salt
  7. Get black pepper powder
  8. Make ready Garam masala powder (see recipe)
  9. Make ready green chili’s
  10. Prepare coriander leaves
  11. Take mint sprigs
  12. Prepare green food color (optional)
  13. Make ready cashews
  14. Take desiccated coconut powder
  15. Make ready charoli seeds (chironji)
  16. Make ready water for cooking
  17. Make ready oil
Steps to make Green chicken with gravy:
  1. Firstly we will make paste of green chili’s,coriander & mint leaves with garlic pods make a fine paste and set aside.further take a kadhai add oil and pop chopped onions sauté until golden brown then add cashews & desiccated coconut powder paste cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Further add whisked yoghurt greens paste as shown in the picture above. Next add salt & black pepper powder cook until oil separates then add washed & cleaned chicken Garam masala powder, green food color,coriander leaves and water cover the lid and cook until chicken is tender & oil separates.
  3. Finally add crushed kasuri methi and Pop it in a serving bowl our deliciously awesome green chicken with gravy is ready to relish with hot kulcha or parathas either. - (see recipe)
