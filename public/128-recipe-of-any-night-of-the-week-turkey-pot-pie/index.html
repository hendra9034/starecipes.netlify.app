The ingredients needed to make Turkey Pot Pie:
  1. Take 2 Cups Shredded Turkey
  2. Take 3 Cans Mixed Vegetables, drained
  3. Prepare 3 Sm. Cans Cream of Chicken Soup, undiluted
  4. Take 1 Tbsp. Garlic Powder
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp. Onion Powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp. Salt
  7. Get 1 tsp. Black Pepper
  8. Get 1 Pkg. Jiffy Cornbread Mix
  9. Take 1 Egg
  10. Take 1/3-1/4 Cup Milk
  11. Get 1/2 tsp. Salt
  12. Prepare 2 tsps. Sugar
Instructions to make Turkey Pot Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl add the shredded turkey, mixed vegetables, seasonings, and soup. Mix well til combined. Pour into a 13X9 baking dish. Spread evenly. Set aside.
  3. In a small mixing bowl add the cornbread mix, egg, teaspoon salt, sugar, and milk. Mix well and pour over top of turkey filling. Spread evenly and bake for 35-45 minutes or until golden brown n when knife inserted into the cornbread comes out clean. Serve immediately ðŸ˜‹
